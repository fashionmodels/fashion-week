How To Easily Put On Your Dr Martens Chelsea Boots

Written by Christine Oct 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you struggle with putting on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots? Are you tired of the struggle and the pain it causes? We know how frustrating it can be to try and fit your foot into a tight boot, especially when you're in a rush, but don't worry, we've got you covered. In this tutorial, we will show you exactly how to put on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots without any hassle.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to putting on Chelsea boots is getting your foot through the narrow opening. It can be a real struggle, especially if you have wider feet or high arches. This can cause discomfort and even pain when trying to force your foot into the boot. But fear not, there is a simple solution to this problem.

The first step is to loosen the laces or the elasticated side panels of your Dr Martens Chelsea boots. This will create a wider opening for your foot to slide into. Next, make sure your foot is dry and free from any excess moisture or sweat, as this will make it easier to slide your foot into the boot.

To put on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots, first, place your foot into the boot, making sure your heel is at the back of the boot. Then, using both hands, gently pull the boot up towards your ankle. If you're having difficulty, try wiggling your foot around a little to help slide it into the boot. Once your foot is in, gently tug on the boot to make sure it's in place and comfortable.

In summary, to put on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots easily, make sure to loosen the laces or elasticated side panels, ensure your foot is dry, slide your foot in heel first, gently pull the boot up towards your ankle, and adjust as necessary. With these steps, you'll be able to put on your boots with ease every time.

How to Get Dr Martens Chelsea Boots On: Personal Experience

As someone who has struggled with putting on Chelsea boots in the past, I understand how frustrating it can be. When I first got my Dr Martens Chelsea boots, I thought there was no way I'd be able to get them on without a struggle. However, after following the steps above, I was pleasantly surprised at how easy it was to put them on. I no longer dread putting on my boots and can easily slip them on without any discomfort.

Loosening the Laces or Elasticated Side Panels

The first step to putting on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots with ease is to loosen the laces or elasticated side panels. This will create a wider opening for your foot to slide into. For laces, simply untie them or loosen them enough to create a wider opening. For elasticated side panels, pull them outwards gently to create more space. This will make the process of putting on your boots much easier.

Ensure Your Foot is Dry

Another important step to putting on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots easily is to ensure your foot is dry. Excess moisture or sweat can make it difficult to slide your foot into the boot. Make sure to wipe your feet dry before putting on your boots, and if necessary, use a powder to absorb any excess moisture.

Adjust as Necessary

Once you've successfully put on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots, make sure to adjust them as necessary. This includes making sure the boot is comfortably snug around your foot, ensuring there's enough space for your toes to move around, and checking that the heel is in the right place. Adjusting your boots will ensure they fit properly and prevent any discomfort or pain.

Practice Makes Perfect

Putting on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots with ease may take some practice, especially if you're not used to wearing boots with a narrow opening. Don't be discouraged if it takes you a few tries to get it right. With practice, you'll get the hang of it and be able to put on your boots effortlessly every time.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear socks when putting on my Dr Martens Chelsea boots?
A: Yes, you can wear socks when putting on your boots. Just make sure they're not too thick, as this can make it more difficult to slide your foot into the boot.

Q: Should I use a shoe horn to put on my Chelsea boots?
A: While a shoe horn can be helpful, it's not necessary to put on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots. Following the steps above should be enough to put on your boots with ease.

Q: What if my Dr Martens Chelsea boots are still too tight?
A: If your boots are still too tight, try loosening the laces or elasticated side panels even more. You can also try wearing thinner socks or using a shoe stretcher to widen the boot.

Q: Can I use a lubricant to help slide my foot into the boot?
A: We don't recommend using a lubricant to help slide your foot into your boots. This can damage the material and affect the overall quality of your boots.

Conclusion of How to Get Dr Martens Chelsea Boots On

Putting on your Dr Martens Chelsea boots doesn't have to be a struggle. By following the steps above, you'll be able to put on your boots with ease and without any discomfort. Remember to loosen the laces or elasticated side panels, ensure your foot is dry, slide your foot in heel first, gently pull the boot up towards your ankle, and adjust as necessary. With these tips, you'll be able to wear your Dr Martens Chelsea boots with confidence and comfort.
