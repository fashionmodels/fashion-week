The History of Matchbox Cars and Their Value

Matchbox cars were first introduced in 1953 by Lesney Products, a British company. The name "Matchbox" was derived from the fact that the original cars were sold in small boxes that resembled matchboxes. Matchbox cars became popular quickly, and the company began to produce a wide range of models. The 1960's were a significant decade for Matchbox cars, and the company produced many iconic models during this era.
