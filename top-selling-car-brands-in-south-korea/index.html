The Top Selling Car Brands In South Korea A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to buying a car in South Korea, there are a lot of options to choose from. With so many brands and models on the market, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is right for you. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at the top selling car brands in South Korea and break down what makes them so popular.

If you're in the market for a new car, you're probably looking for something that is reliable, affordable, and stylish. You want a car that can get you where you need to go without breaking the bank. You also want a car that looks good and makes you feel good when you're behind the wheel.

So, what are the top selling car brands in South Korea? According to recent sales data, the top brands are Hyundai, Kia, and GM Korea (which includes Chevrolet and Cadillac). These three brands make up the majority of car sales in the country, with Hyundai and Kia alone accounting for over 70% of the market.

In summary, the top selling car brands in South Korea are Hyundai, Kia, and GM Korea. These brands are known for their reliability, affordability, and stylish designs. Let's take a closer look at each of these brands and what makes them so popular.

Hyundai: A Korean Icon

As one of the oldest and most established car brands in South Korea, Hyundai has become an icon in the country. Founded in 1967, the company has a long history of producing high-quality vehicles that are both affordable and reliable.

Personally, I have owned a Hyundai Sonata for the past 3 years and it has been a great car. It's comfortable, spacious, and has all the features I need. What I love most about it is the sleek design and the fact that it's made by a Korean company.

Hyundai's most popular models in South Korea are the Sonata, the Grandeur (known as the Azera in some markets), and the Porter truck. These models are known for their spacious interiors, fuel efficiency, and safety features.

Kia: A Rising Star

While Hyundai may be the most established car brand in South Korea, Kia is quickly rising to the top. Founded in 1944, the company has undergone a major transformation in recent years, with a focus on producing stylish and affordable vehicles.

Personally, I have never owned a Kia, but I have heard great things about the brand. My friend recently purchased a Kia Sportage and she loves it. She says it's comfortable, handles well on the road, and has all the features she needs.

Kia's most popular models in South Korea are the Sportage, the Carnival (known as the Sedona in some markets), and the K5 (known as the Optima in some markets). These models are known for their sleek designs, advanced technology, and spacious interiors.

GM Korea: The American Influence

While Hyundai and Kia are both Korean companies, GM Korea is a subsidiary of General Motors, an American company. However, GM Korea has been operating in South Korea since the 1970s and has become a major player in the country's automotive industry.

I have never owned a GM Korea car, but I have driven a Chevrolet Spark and I was impressed with the car's performance. It was zippy and fun to drive, which is not what I was expecting from a small car.

GM Korea's most popular models in South Korea are the Chevrolet Spark, the Malibu, and the Trax. These models are known for their affordability, fuel efficiency, and modern designs.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best selling car model in South Korea?

A: The Hyundai Sonata is currently the best selling car model in South Korea.

Q: Are there any luxury car brands that sell well in South Korea?

A: Yes, brands like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, and Audi are popular among luxury car buyers in South Korea.

Q: Are electric cars popular in South Korea?

A: Yes, electric cars are becoming more and more popular in South Korea, with the Hyundai Kona Electric and the Kia Niro EV being two of the most popular models.

Q: Are there any Korean car brands that are popular outside of South Korea?

A: Yes, Hyundai and Kia are both popular car brands in many countries around the world, including the United States, Europe, and Australia.

Conclusion of Top Selling Car Brands in South Korea

When it comes to buying a car in South Korea, there are a lot of options to choose from. However, the top selling car brands in the country are Hyundai, Kia, and GM Korea. These brands are known for their reliability, affordability, and stylish designs, and they offer a range of models to suit different needs and budgets. Whether you're looking for a spacious sedan, a stylish SUV, or a fuel-efficient hatchback, you're sure to find a car that meets your needs from one of these top selling brands.
