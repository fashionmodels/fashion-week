How to care for Pakistani cotton suits?

To ensure that your Pakistani cotton suits last a long time, it's essential to take proper care of them. Here are some tips for caring for your Pakistani cotton suits:

1. Wash in cold water: Always wash your Pakistani cotton suits in cold water to prevent shrinkage and damage to the fabric.

2. Use mild detergent: Use a mild detergent to wash your suits. Harsh detergents can damage the fabric and cause it to lose its color and shape.

3. Hang dry: Always hang your Pakistani cotton suits to dry. Avoid using a dryer as it can damage the fabric and cause shrinkage.

4. Iron on low heat: If you need to iron your suits, do it on low heat. High heat can damage the fabric and cause it to lose its shape.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your Pakistani cotton suits last a long time and look great.
