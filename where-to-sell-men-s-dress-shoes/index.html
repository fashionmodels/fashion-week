Where To Sell Men s Dress Shoes A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having old and unused men's dress shoes take up space in your closet? Do you want to earn some extra cash by selling them? You're not alone! Many people struggle with finding the best place to sell their gently used men's dress shoes. In this article, we'll explore different options and provide you with a comprehensive guide on where to sell men's dress shoes.

Selling men's dress shoes can be a daunting task, especially if you're not familiar with the market. You might not know where to start, or you might be worried that you won't get a fair price. It can also be time-consuming to go through the process of selling your shoes, from finding potential buyers to negotiating a price. However, with the right guidance, you can find the best platform to sell your shoes and make the process easier and more profitable.

Where to Sell Men's Dress Shoes: Online Platforms

One of the best options for selling your men's dress shoes is through online platforms. There are many websites and apps that specialize in buying and selling second-hand items, including men's dress shoes. Here are some of the most popular platforms:

eBay

eBay is one of the oldest and most reliable online marketplaces. It has a large user base, which means that your shoes have a higher chance of finding a buyer. You can set your own price, and eBay will take a small percentage of the sale as a fee. However, keep in mind that there is a lot of competition on eBay, so you'll need to make sure that your shoes stand out.

Poshmark

Poshmark is a popular app for buying and selling second-hand clothing and accessories. It's very user-friendly and has a large community of buyers and sellers. You can list your men's dress shoes for free, and Poshmark will take a small percentage of the sale as a fee. One of the benefits of using Poshmark is that you can easily promote your shoes on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

Depop

Depop is another app-based platform that's popular among young people. It's similar to Poshmark but has a more trendy and fashion-focused audience. You can list your men's dress shoes for free, and Depop will take a small percentage of the sale as a fee. If your shoes are stylish and unique, Depop might be the best platform for you.

Where to Sell Men's Dress Shoes: Brick-and-Mortar Stores

If you prefer to sell your men's dress shoes in person, there are still some options available. Here are some of the best brick-and-mortar stores that buy second-hand shoes:

Plato's Closet

Plato's Closet is a popular chain of stores that specializes in buying and selling gently used clothing and accessories. They have a specific section for men's shoes, including dress shoes. You can bring your shoes to a Plato's Closet location, and they will offer you a price based on their condition and demand.

Buffalo Exchange

Buffalo Exchange is another popular chain of stores that buys and sells second-hand clothing and accessories. They also have a specific section for men's shoes, including dress shoes. You can bring your shoes to a Buffalo Exchange location, and they will offer you a price based on their condition and demand.

Local Consignment Stores

There are many local consignment stores that specialize in buying and selling second-hand clothing and accessories. These stores might be a good option if you're looking for a more personalized experience. You can research consignment stores in your area and contact them to see if they're interested in buying your men's dress shoes.

Cobbler Shops

If your men's dress shoes need some repairs or cleaning, you can take them to a cobbler shop. Some cobbler shops also buy gently used shoes, so it's worth asking if they're interested in purchasing your shoes. However, keep in mind that cobbler shops might not offer the best prices for your shoes.

Conclusion of Where to Sell Men's Dress Shoes

Selling your men's dress shoes can be a profitable and rewarding experience, as long as you choose the right platform. Whether you prefer to sell your shoes online or in person, there are many options available. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you'll be able to find the best place to sell your shoes and earn some extra cash.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best platform to sell my men's dress shoes online?

A: It depends on your shoes' condition and style. eBay, Poshmark, and Depop are all great options, but you'll need to research the market and competition to find the best fit.

Q: How much can I expect to earn by selling my men's dress shoes?

A: The price of your shoes will depend on their condition, brand, and demand. However, you can expect to earn anywhere from $20 to $200 per pair, depending on these factors.

Q: Should I clean my shoes before selling them?

A: Yes, you should clean and polish your shoes before selling them. This will make them look more attractive to potential buyers and increase their value.

Q: Can I sell my men's dress shoes if they're damaged?

A: It depends on the extent of the damage. If your shoes have minor scuffs or scratches, you might still be able to sell them. However, if the damage is severe, it might be difficult to find a buyer.
