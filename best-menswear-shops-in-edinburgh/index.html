The Best Menswear Shops In Edinburgh

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you on the hunt for the best menswear shops in Edinburgh? Perhaps you're struggling to find the perfect outfit for a special occasion, or you're simply looking to refresh your wardrobe with some stylish new pieces. Whatever your reason, we've got you covered with our guide to the top menswear shops in Edinburgh.

If you're tired of trawling through endless high street stores, or scrolling through online retailers with little success, then it's time to explore the best menswear shops in Edinburgh. With an impressive array of independent boutiques, luxury retailers and high-end department stores, Edinburgh boasts some of the best shopping experiences in the UK.

Our guide will take you through the top menswear shops in Edinburgh, from classic tailors to contemporary streetwear boutiques. We'll cover everything from the latest trends to timeless classics, so you can find the perfect pieces to suit your style and budget.

The Best Menswear Shops in Edinburgh: A Personal Experience

As someone who loves fashion, I'm always on the lookout for the best menswear shops in Edinburgh. One of my favourite spots is Walker Slater, a traditional Scottish tweed specialist with a modern twist. Their stylish blazers, waistcoats and trousers are perfect for any occasion, from a wedding to a day at the races.

Another great option is The Brotique, a unique store that sells a wide range of men's grooming products, accessories and clothing. Their selection of quirky ties, hats and cufflinks are perfect for adding a touch of personality to any outfit.

Top Menswear Shops in Edinburgh: Tailoring

If you're in search of bespoke tailoring, then Edinburgh has some of the best options in the UK. One of the most popular is Dickson & MacNaughton, a family-run business that has been providing high-quality tailoring since 1868. Their skilled tailors can create bespoke suits, jackets and trousers to fit your exact measurements and preferences.

Another excellent option is Stewart Christie & Co, a bespoke tailoring and tweed specialist that has been in business since 1720. Their experienced tailors can create bespoke suits, kilts and tweed jackets using the finest materials and traditional techniques.

The Best Menswear Shops in Edinburgh: Streetwear

If you're looking for something a little more contemporary, then Edinburgh also has plenty of streetwear options. One of the best is Fat Buddha, a streetwear and sneaker store that stocks the latest trends from brands such as Adidas, Nike and Carhartt. Their selection of trainers, hoodies and t-shirts are perfect for those looking to add some urban edge to their wardrobe.

The Best Menswear Shops in Edinburgh: Accessories

No outfit is complete without the right accessories, and Edinburgh has some great options for men. One of our favourites is 21st Century Kilts, a specialist kilt and accessories store that offers a modern take on traditional Scottish dress. Their range of sporrans, belts and buckles are perfect for adding a touch of Scottish style to any outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best menswear shop in Edinburgh for bespoke tailoring?

A: Dickson & MacNaughton and Stewart Christie & Co are both excellent options for bespoke tailoring in Edinburgh.

Q: Where can I find the latest streetwear trends in Edinburgh?

A: Fat Buddha is a great store for the latest streetwear and sneaker trends.

Q: What is the best menswear shop in Edinburgh for accessories?

A: 21st Century Kilts is a specialist store that offers a modern take on traditional Scottish dress, with a range of sporrans, belts and buckles.

Q: What is the best menswear shop in Edinburgh for traditional Scottish tweed?

A: Walker Slater is a traditional Scottish tweed specialist with a modern twist.

Conclusion of the Best Menswear Shops in Edinburgh

Whether you're in search of bespoke tailoring, contemporary streetwear or traditional Scottish tweed, Edinburgh has something to offer every style and budget. We hope our guide to the top menswear shops in Edinburgh has inspired you to explore the city's vibrant shopping scene and find the perfect pieces to suit your individual style.
