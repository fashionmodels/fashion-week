New Menswear Brands 2021 A Guide To The Latest Fashion Trends

Written by Luffy Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old clothes? Do you want to upgrade your wardrobe and stay on-trend with the latest fashion styles? Look no further than new menswear brands 2021. These emerging fashion labels are shaking up the industry with fresh designs and innovative materials. In this guide, we'll explore the top new menswear brands 2021 and how they can elevate your fashion game.

Many men struggle to find clothing that fits their personal style and body type. They may also feel limited by the options available at traditional retail stores. New menswear brands 2021 offer a solution to these pain points. They provide unique and diverse clothing options that cater to a range of preferences and sizes. Additionally, many of these brands prioritize sustainability and ethical production practices.

The target of new menswear brands 2021 is anyone who wants to update their fashion choices and stand out from the crowd. Whether you're a fashion-forward trendsetter or a minimalist looking for high-quality basics, there's a brand out there for you. These labels are also popular among younger generations who prioritize individuality and sustainability in their purchasing decisions.

In summary, new menswear brands 2021 offer a refreshing take on fashion with unique designs, inclusive sizing, and sustainable practices. They cater to a diverse range of preferences and are popular among individuals who want to stand out from traditional retail offerings.

1. Lemaire

Lemaire is a French brand founded by Christophe Lemaire and Sarah-Linh Tran. The label is known for its minimalist yet sophisticated designs that prioritize high-quality materials and functional details. Lemaire offers a range of clothing items, including tailored jackets, relaxed trousers, and oversized knitwear. The brand's color palette is neutral and muted, making it easy to mix and match pieces for a cohesive wardrobe.

2. Bianca Saunders

Bianca Saunders is a London-based designer who launched her eponymous label in 2018. Her collections explore themes of masculinity and identity, with a focus on deconstructing traditional menswear silhouettes. Saunders' designs often feature asymmetrical cuts, intricate draping, and unexpected details like leather harnesses. The brand's clothing is meant to be layered and styled in unique ways, allowing wearers to express their individuality.

3. Nudie Jeans

Nudie Jeans is a Swedish brand that specializes in denim clothing. The label is committed to sustainability and ethical production practices, using organic cotton and recycled materials in its clothing. Nudie Jeans offers a range of fits and styles, from slim to wide-leg, and also offers repairs and recycling services for its products. The brand's signature design element is its orange stitching, which can be found on all of its denim items.

4. Story MFG

Story MFG is a UK-based brand founded by Katy Katazome and Saeed Al-Rubeyi. The label focuses on sustainable and ethical production methods, using natural materials and plant-based dyes in its clothing. Story MFG's designs are inspired by vintage workwear and traditional craft techniques, resulting in unique and artisanal pieces. The brand's clothing often features intricate embroidery and patchwork details.

How to Shop for New Menswear Brands 2021

Shopping for new menswear brands 2021 can be exciting but also overwhelming. Here are some tips to help you navigate the process:

1. Start by researching different brands and their design aesthetics. Look at their websites and social media pages to get a sense of their clothing offerings and prices.

2. Consider your personal style and the types of clothing items you need. Do you want to invest in versatile basics or statement pieces?

3. Read customer reviews and sizing guides to ensure you order the correct size and fit.

4. Take advantage of sales and promotions to save money on your purchases.

5. Q&A about New Menswear Brands 2021

Q: What sets new menswear brands 2021 apart from traditional retail options?

A: New menswear brands 2021 offer unique designs, inclusive sizing, and sustainable production practices that prioritize ethical and environmentally conscious values.

Q: Are new menswear brands 2021 more expensive than traditional retail options?

A: Not necessarily. While some brands may have higher price points due to the use of high-quality materials and ethical production methods, there are also affordable options available.

Q: How can I find new menswear brands 2021 that cater to my personal style?

A: Research different brands and their design aesthetics to find ones that align with your preferences. Follow them on social media and sign up for their newsletters to stay up-to-date on new releases and promotions.

Q: Are new menswear brands 2021 only popular among younger generations?

A: No, new menswear brands 2021 are popular among individuals of all ages who prioritize individuality and sustainability in their fashion choices.

Conclusion of New Menswear Brands 2021

New menswear brands 2021 offer a fresh take on fashion with unique designs, inclusive sizing, and sustainable practices. These labels cater to a diverse range of preferences and are popular among individuals who want to stand out from traditional retail offerings. By exploring different brands and their design aesthetics, you can find clothing items that align with your personal style and values.
