Question and Answer

Q: What are safety shoes?

A: Safety shoes are a type of personal protective equipment (PPE) that workers wear to protect their feet from hazards such as falling objects, sharp objects, and electrical hazards.

Q: What should I consider when buying safety shoes?

A: When buying safety shoes, you should consider the type of hazards you are exposed to, the level of protection you need, and the comfort of the shoes.

Q: What features should I look for in safety shoes?

A: You should look for features such as steel toes, slip-resistant soles, electrical hazard protection, shock absorption, and comfortable insoles.

Q: How do I know if my safety shoes fit properly?

A: Your safety shoes should fit snugly but not be too tight. You should be able to wiggle your toes comfortably, and there should be enough room to insert a finger between your heel and the back of the shoe.
