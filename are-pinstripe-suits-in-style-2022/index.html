How to Style a Pinstripe Suit in 2022

When it comes to styling a pinstripe suit in 2022, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, opt for a modern fit, such as a slim or tailored fit. The History of Pinstripe Suits

Pinstripe suits originated in London in the early 20th century and were originally worn by bankers and businessmen. The stripes were a subtle way of expressing wealth and status, as they were made from expensive fabrics such as wool and silk. Pinstripe suits became a popular choice among the elite and eventually made their way into mainstream fashion in the 1920s. The trend continued through the 1930s and 1940s, but fell out of fashion in the 1950s and 1960s. However, pinstripe suits made a comeback in the 1980s and have remained a classic style in men's fashion ever since.
