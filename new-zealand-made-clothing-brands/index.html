New Zealand Made Clothing Brands A Guide To Buying Locally

Written by Oliver May 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of buying clothes that fall apart after a few wears? Are you looking for high-quality, sustainable clothing options? Look no further than New Zealand made clothing brands. These brands are not only stylish and long-lasting, but they also support local businesses and reduce your carbon footprint. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at the benefits of buying New Zealand made clothing brands and share some of our favorite brands.

Many people are frustrated with fast fashion and the negative impact it has on the environment. Clothes made in overseas factories often have low labor standards and are made with cheap materials that don't last. By choosing New Zealand made clothing brands, you can be sure that you're supporting ethical and sustainable business practices. Additionally, buying locally reduces the amount of carbon emissions associated with shipping clothes across the world.

The target of New Zealand made clothing brands is anyone who values quality, sustainability, and supporting local businesses. Whether you're looking for everyday basics or high-end fashion pieces, there is a New Zealand made brand for you.

In summary, New Zealand made clothing brands offer a sustainable, ethical, and stylish alternative to fast fashion. By supporting local businesses, you can feel good about your purchase and reduce your carbon footprint. In the following sections, we'll explore some of the best New Zealand made clothing brands in more detail.

Kowtow Clothing

Kowtow Clothing is a New Zealand based brand that is committed to using sustainable and ethical production methods. Their clothes are made with organic cotton and fair trade practices. I personally own several Kowtow pieces and love the quality and style. Their pieces are versatile and can be dressed up or down.

Untouched World

Untouched World is a New Zealand brand that focuses on using natural, sustainable fibers like merino wool and possum fur. Their clothes are not only environmentally friendly but also stylish and functional. I love their cozy sweaters and scarves for cold winter days.

Why Choose Merino Wool?

Merino wool is a popular choice for New Zealand made clothing brands because it is a renewable resource, biodegradable, and naturally insulating. Additionally, merino wool is soft and comfortable to wear, making it a great choice for base layers and activewear.

Caring for Merino Wool

To keep your merino wool clothes looking their best, it's important to wash them with care. Use a gentle detergent and wash in cold water. Avoid using fabric softener, which can damage the fibers. Lay flat to dry and store folded, not hung.

Icebreaker

Icebreaker is another popular New Zealand made brand that specializes in merino wool clothing. Their pieces are designed for outdoor adventures but are also stylish enough for everyday wear. I love their leggings for running and hiking.

Question and Answer

Q: Are New Zealand made clothing brands more expensive than fast fashion brands?

A: Yes, New Zealand made clothing brands are often more expensive than fast fashion brands. However, the higher price reflects the use of high-quality materials and ethical production methods. Additionally, investing in higher-quality clothes means you'll need to replace them less often.

Q: Can I find New Zealand made clothing brands outside of New Zealand?

A: Yes, many New Zealand made clothing brands have international shipping options. Additionally, some brands have physical stores in other countries.

Q: Are New Zealand made clothes only for outdoor activities?

A: No, New Zealand made clothing brands offer a variety of styles for everyday wear as well as outdoor activities.

Q: How do I know if a clothing brand is truly sustainable and ethical?

A: Look for information on the brand's website about their production methods and materials. Additionally, third-party certifications like Fair Trade and GOTS can help verify a brand's ethical and sustainable practices.

Conclusion of New Zealand Made Clothing Brands

New Zealand made clothing brands offer a sustainable, ethical, and stylish alternative to fast fashion. By supporting local businesses, you can feel good about your purchase and reduce your carbon footprint. Brands like Kowtow Clothing, Untouched World, and Icebreaker offer high-quality clothes made with natural and renewable materials. By choosing New Zealand made clothing brands, you can invest in clothes that will last and support businesses that prioritize sustainability and ethics.
