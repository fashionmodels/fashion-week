Cost Of Wedding In Lagos Nigeria A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Planning a wedding is one of the most exciting times in a couple's life, but it can also be one of the most stressful. One of the biggest concerns for many couples is the cost of their wedding, especially in Lagos Nigeria where weddings are known to be grandiose and expensive affairs. In this blog post, we'll take a comprehensive look at the cost of weddings in Lagos Nigeria, and provide you with the information you need to plan your dream wedding without breaking the bank.

Weddings are notorious for being expensive, and Lagos Nigeria is no exception. Couples are often overwhelmed by the amount of money they need to shell out for their big day, from the venue to the catering to the decorations. It's no surprise that many couples find themselves going over budget, causing financial stress and strain on their relationship.

The cost of a wedding in Lagos Nigeria varies depending on a number of factors, including the number of guests, the venue, the time of year, and the type of vendors you choose. On average, a wedding in Lagos Nigeria can cost anywhere from ₦3,000,000 to ₦20,000,000 or more, depending on how lavish you want your wedding to be.

So, how can you plan a wedding in Lagos Nigeria without breaking the bank? One of the first things you should do is set a budget and stick to it. Determine how much money you can realistically afford to spend on your wedding, and then prioritize the most important aspects of your day, such as the venue, food, and entertainment. Consider having your wedding on a weekday or during the off-season to save money, and be open to negotiating with vendors to get the best deals.

Cost of Venue in Lagos Nigeria

When it comes to the cost of a wedding in Lagos Nigeria, the venue is one of the biggest expenses. Depending on the location and size of the venue, you can expect to pay anywhere from ₦500,000 to ₦5,000,000 or more for your wedding venue. One way to save money on your venue is to choose a location that is not typically used for weddings, such as a community center or public park. You can also consider having your ceremony and reception at the same venue to save on transportation costs.

Cost of Catering in Lagos Nigeria

The cost of catering is another major expense when it comes to planning a wedding in Lagos Nigeria. Depending on the type of catering you choose, you can expect to pay anywhere from ₦1,500 to ₦15,000 per plate. To save money on catering, consider having a buffet-style meal instead of a plated dinner, and opt for local cuisine instead of international dishes.

Cost of Photography and Videography in Lagos Nigeria

Photography and videography are important aspects of any wedding, and in Lagos Nigeria, they can be quite expensive. Depending on the type of package you choose, you can expect to pay anywhere from ₦500,000 to ₦2,000,000 or more for your wedding photos and videos. To save money, consider hiring a local photographer or videographer who is just starting out, or negotiate with your vendor for a more affordable package.

How to Save Money on Your Wedding in Lagos Nigeria

There are many ways to save money on your wedding in Lagos Nigeria, including choosing a less expensive venue, having a smaller guest list, and DIY-ing your decorations. You can also opt for a cash gift registry instead of traditional gifts, and ask friends and family to help with certain aspects of your wedding, such as music or photography. Remember, your wedding day is about celebrating your love for each other, not about impressing your guests with an extravagant affair.

Cost of Wedding Planning Services in Lagos Nigeria

If you're feeling overwhelmed by the cost of planning a wedding in Lagos Nigeria, you may want to consider hiring a wedding planner. Wedding planning services can be quite expensive, with some planners charging upwards of ₦2,000,000 for their services. To save money, consider hiring a planner who is just starting out, or opt for a package that includes only the services you need.

Question and Answer

Q: How much does a wedding in Lagos Nigeria cost on average?

A: On average, a wedding in Lagos Nigeria can cost anywhere from ₦3,000,000 to ₦20,000,000 or more, depending on how lavish you want your wedding to be.

Q: What are some ways to save money on a wedding in Lagos Nigeria?

A: Some ways to save money on a wedding in Lagos Nigeria include choosing a less expensive venue, having a smaller guest list, and DIY-ing your decorations. You can also opt for a cash gift registry instead of traditional gifts, and ask friends and family to help with certain aspects of your wedding, such as music or photography.

Q: Is it necessary to hire a wedding planner in Lagos Nigeria?

A: While it's not necessary to hire a wedding planner in Lagos Nigeria, it can be helpful if you're feeling overwhelmed by the planning process. Wedding planning services can be quite expensive, but you can save money by hiring a planner who is just starting out, or opting for a package that includes only the services you need.

Q: How can I negotiate with vendors to get the best deals?

A: To negotiate with vendors to get the best deals, be upfront about your budget and what you're looking for. Ask if they have any packages or discounts available, and be willing to compromise on certain aspects of your wedding if it means getting a better deal.

Conclusion

Planning a wedding in Lagos Nigeria can be a daunting task, but with the right information and a little bit of creativity, you can have your dream wedding without breaking the bank. Remember to set a budget, prioritize the most important aspects of your day, and be open to negotiating with vendors to get the best deals. Your wedding day is about celebrating your love for each other, not about impressing your guests with an extravagant affair.
