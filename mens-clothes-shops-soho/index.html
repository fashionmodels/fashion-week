The Best Mens Clothes Shops In Soho A Guide For Fashion Forward Men

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of scouring malls and department stores for stylish menswear? Do you want to upgrade your wardrobe without breaking the bank? Look no further than the mens clothes shops in Soho, the trendy neighborhood in downtown Manhattan known for its eclectic mix of shops, restaurants, and nightlife.

If you're a fashion-forward man who wants to make a statement with your clothes, Soho is the perfect destination. With a wide range of mens clothes shops, from high-end boutiques to vintage stores, you'll find everything you need to create a unique and stylish look.

Here's a guide to the best mens clothes shops in Soho, including what to expect and where to go for the latest trends and classic styles.

The Best High-End Shops

For those who are willing to splurge on designer clothes, Soho has plenty of options. Start with the iconic Ralph Lauren store on Prince Street, which offers a range of classic American styles, from preppy polo shirts to tailored suits. If you're looking for Italian luxury, head to Brunello Cucinelli on Wooster Street, which features sleek and sophisticated pieces made from the finest materials.

The Best Vintage Stores

If you're on a budget or want to find one-of-a-kind pieces, Soho's vintage stores are a must-visit. Try out the vintage T-shirt selection at What Goes Around Comes Around on West Broadway, or check out the curated collection of vintage denim at 3x1 on Mercer Street.

The Best Streetwear Shops

For the latest trends in streetwear, Soho has you covered. Stop by Supreme on Lafayette Street for limited-edition drops and collaborations, or head to Kith on Broadway for a mix of streetwear and high-end fashion. And if you're into sneakers, don't miss the NikeLab store on Mercer Street, which features exclusive releases and collaborations.

How to Find the Best Deals

While Soho is known for its high-end shops, there are also plenty of affordable options. Check out Uniqlo on Broadway for stylish basics at reasonable prices, or head to Topman on Broadway for trendy pieces that won't break the bank. And if you're willing to dig through racks of clothes, Buffalo Exchange on West 26th Street offers gently used designer pieces at a fraction of the original price.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time to visit Soho's mens clothes shops?
A: Weekdays are generally less crowded than weekends, so try to visit during the week if possible. And if you're looking for the best deals, be sure to visit during the end-of-season sales in January and July.

Q: What should I wear when shopping in Soho?
A: Dress comfortably in stylish but practical clothes and shoes, as you'll be doing a lot of walking. And make sure you have a cross-body bag or backpack to keep your hands free for browsing.

Q: Can I bring my significant other or friend who doesn't like shopping?
A: Absolutely! Soho has plenty of options for non-shoppers, including restaurants, bars, and art galleries.

Q: Are there any mens clothes shops in Soho that offer alterations?
A: Yes, many of the higher-end shops offer tailoring services on-site, so be sure to ask if you need alterations.

Conclusion of Mens Clothes Shops Soho

Whether you're a fashionista or simply looking to upgrade your wardrobe, Soho's mens clothes shops offer something for everyone. From high-end boutiques to vintage stores and streetwear shops, you'll find the latest trends and classic styles in this trendy neighborhood. So grab your wallet and get ready to shop 'til you drop!
