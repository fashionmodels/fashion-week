Best Mens Designer Bathing Suits A Guide To Looking Stylish On The Beach

Written by William Sep 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same boring bathing suits to the beach every year? Do you want to turn heads and look stylish on the shore? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll discuss the best mens designer bathing suits and how they can elevate your beachwear game.

It can be frustrating to shop for bathing suits, especially if you're unsure of what style or brand to choose. You may have trouble finding bathing suits that fit well, flatter your body type, or reflect your personal style. Additionally, you may be hesitant to spend a lot of money on bathing suits, since they're typically worn for only a few months out of the year.

However, investing in high-quality mens designer bathing suits can be worth the cost. These bathing suits are made from premium materials and are designed to last for many summers to come. They often feature unique patterns, colors, and details that set them apart from generic, mass-produced bathing suits. Plus, they can make you feel more confident and fashionable on the beach.

In this article, we'll explore the best mens designer bathing suits on the market, including their features, benefits, and drawbacks. We'll also provide tips for choosing the right bathing suit for your body type and personal preferences.

Orlebar Brown

One of the most popular mens designer bathing suit brands is Orlebar Brown. This British company is known for its tailored, sophisticated swimwear that's inspired by 1960s poolside glamour. Orlebar Brown offers a range of bathing suits in various colors, prints, and styles, from classic mid-length shorts to more contemporary swim trunks.

Personally, I love Orlebar Brown's Bulldog shorts, which are made from quick-drying fabric and feature adjustable waist tabs and a zip fly. They're versatile enough to wear both in and out of the water, and they come in a wide range of colors and prints to suit every taste.

Vilebrequin

If you're looking for a mens designer bathing suit that's playful and whimsical, Vilebrequin is a great choice. This French brand is known for its bold, colorful swimwear that often features quirky patterns and motifs, such as turtles, lobsters, and palm trees.

I recently purchased a pair of Vilebrequin's Moorea swim trunks, which are made from lightweight, fast-drying fabric and feature an elasticized waistband and drawstring closure. I love the bright blue color and the fun, retro-inspired print of surfboards and palm trees. They're perfect for a day spent lounging on the beach or playing in the waves.

Onia

If you're in search of a more minimalist, streamlined mens designer bathing suit, Onia is a great option. This brand focuses on high-quality materials and simple, timeless designs that are both stylish and functional.

One of Onia's most popular styles is the Charles swim trunk, which is made from a quick-drying nylon-spandex blend and features a tailored, slim fit. The Charles comes in a variety of colors and patterns, from classic black to bold floral prints.

How to Choose the Right Mens Designer Bathing Suit for You

When shopping for mens designer bathing suits, it's important to consider your body type, personal style, and intended use. Here are some tips to help you choose the right bathing suit:

- If you have a slender build, opt for swim trunks with a shorter inseam and a slimmer cut to create the illusion of more height and definition.

- If you have a muscular or athletic build, look for swim trunks with a looser fit and a longer inseam to provide more coverage and comfort.

- Consider the color and pattern of the bathing suit. Bold, bright colors and prints can be eye-catching and fun, while neutral colors and classic patterns can be more versatile and timeless.

- Think about the intended use of the bathing suit. If you plan to swim or play sports in it, consider a style with a secure waistband and stretchy, quick-drying fabric. If you plan to wear it for lounging or socializing, you may prioritize comfort and style over function.

Question and Answer

Q: Are mens designer bathing suits worth the cost?

A: It depends on your priorities and budget. While designer bathing suits can be expensive, they often offer higher quality materials, unique designs, and a more flattering fit than cheaper options. Additionally, they can make you feel more confident and stylish on the beach.

Q: How do I know what size to order?

A: Be sure to consult the brand's size chart before ordering. Many mens designer bathing suits have a slim, tailored fit, so you may need to size up if you prefer a looser fit.

Q: Can I wear designer bathing suits for activities other than swimming?

A: Yes! Many mens designer bathing suits are versatile enough to wear for other activities, such as playing volleyball, going for a hike, or having a casual lunch.

Q: What should I look for in a high-quality mens designer bathing suit?

A: Look for bathing suits made from durable, quick-drying materials such as nylon or polyester. Additionally, pay attention to details such as the waistband, pockets, and lining, which can affect the fit and comfort of the suit.

Conclusion of Best Mens Designer Bathing Suits

Investing in a high-quality mens designer bathing suit can be a great way to elevate your beachwear game and feel more confident and stylish on the shore. By considering your body type, personal style, and intended use, you can choose the perfect bathing suit for your needs. Whether you prefer classic, tailored styles or bold, playful prints, there's a mens designer bathing suit out there for you.
