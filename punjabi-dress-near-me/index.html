What Are Some Popular Punjabi Dress Styles?

There are several popular Punjabi dress styles, including salwar kameez, churidar, and dupatta. Salwar kameez is a traditional Punjabi dress that consists of a long tunic (kameez), loose pants (salwar), and a scarf (dupatta). Churidar is similar to salwar kameez but features tight-fitting pants (churidar) instead of loose pants. Dupatta is a scarf that is typically worn with salwar kameez or churidar.

How to Take Care of Your Punjabi Dress?
