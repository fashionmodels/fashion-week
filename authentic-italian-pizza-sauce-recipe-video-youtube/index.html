Step-by-Step Guide on How to Make Authentic Italian Pizza Sauce

To make authentic Italian pizza sauce, you'll need the following ingredients:

  • 28 oz can of San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced

Here's how to make the sauce:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F.
  2. Drain the tomatoes and crush them with your hands in a bowl.
  3. Add the salt, oregano, and black pepper to the bowl and mix well.
  4. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the tomato mixture to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Transfer the sauce to a blender and blend until smooth.
  7. Pour the sauce into a baking dish and bake for 15-20 minutes until it thickens and turns a deeper red color.
  8. Let the sauce cool before using it on your pizza.

Tips for Making the Best Authentic Italian Pizza Sauce

To make the best authentic Italian pizza sauce, be sure to use San Marzano tomatoes as they're known for their sweet flavor and low acidity. You can also add a pinch of sugar to the sauce if you prefer a sweeter taste.

Additionally, be sure to let the sauce cool completely before using it on your pizza. This will help it thicken and prevent it from making your pizza crust soggy.
