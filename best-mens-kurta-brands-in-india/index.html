Best Mens Kurta Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for the best mens kurta brands in India but not sure where to start? Perhaps you're tired of browsing through endless options that don't fit your style or budget. Well, you're in luck because this comprehensive guide will help you navigate through the top kurta brands that India has to offer.

When it comes to traditional Indian wear, the kurta is a staple piece in any man's wardrobe. It's versatile, comfortable, and perfect for any occasion - whether it's a wedding, festival, or casual outing. However, with so many brands out there, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to choose.

In this guide, we will cover the best mens kurta brands in India based on style, quality, affordability, and popularity. We will also include some personal experiences and recommendations to help you make an informed decision.

1. FabIndia

FabIndia is a popular brand that offers a wide range of traditional Indian wear for both men and women. Their kurtas are stylish, comfortable, and made with high-quality materials such as cotton, silk, and linen. What sets FabIndia apart from other brands is their focus on sustainability and promoting Indian handloom textiles. They also offer a range of sizes to accommodate all body types.

Personally, I have bought several kurtas from FabIndia and have been impressed with the quality and fit. Their prices are also reasonable, with kurtas ranging from INR 999 to INR 5000.

2. Manyavar

Manyavar is a popular brand that caters to the modern Indian man. Their kurtas are stylish, trendy, and perfect for any occasion - whether it's a wedding or a casual outing. They offer a wide range of designs, from simple and elegant to bold and vibrant. Manyavar also offers a range of sizes to fit all body types.

I have personally worn Manyavar kurtas to weddings and have received many compliments on their style and fit. However, their prices can be on the higher side, with kurtas ranging from INR 1500 to INR 10,000.

3. Khadi

Khadi is a brand that focuses on promoting Indian handloom textiles and supporting local artisans. Their kurtas are made with natural materials such as cotton, silk, and wool, and are perfect for those who prefer a more rustic and traditional look. Khadi kurtas are also affordable, with prices ranging from INR 500 to INR 5000.

While I haven't personally worn Khadi kurtas, I have heard great things about their quality and affordability. Their focus on sustainability and supporting local artisans is also a plus.

4. W for Woman

W for Woman is a brand that offers a range of traditional Indian wear for women, but they also offer a selection of kurtas for men. Their kurtas are trendy, stylish, and perfect for those who prefer a more modern look. They offer a range of sizes to fit all body types, and their prices are affordable, with kurtas ranging from INR 1000 to INR 3000.

While I haven't personally worn W for Woman kurtas, I have seen their collection and have been impressed with their style and affordability. Their focus on inclusivity and offering a range of sizes is also a plus.

Question and Answer:

Q: What should I consider when buying a kurta?

A: When buying a kurta, consider the occasion, your personal style, and the fit. If you're attending a wedding or formal event, opt for a more traditional and elegant style. If you prefer a more modern look, choose a kurta with bold prints or trendy designs. The fit is also important - make sure to choose a size that fits well and is comfortable to wear.

Q: What materials are kurtas made of?

A: Kurtas are made of a variety of materials, including cotton, silk, linen, and wool. Cotton is the most common material and is perfect for everyday wear, while silk and linen are more suitable for formal occasions. Wool is perfect for colder weather.

Q: How do I care for my kurta?

A: Kurtas should be washed in cold water and hung to dry. Avoid using a dryer, as it can damage the fabric. Iron on low heat if necessary.

Q: How do I choose the right size?

A: When choosing a size, refer to the brand's size chart and measure yourself accordingly. If you're unsure, it's always best to go up a size rather than down.

Conclusion of Best Mens Kurta Brands in India:

Choosing the right kurta brand can be overwhelming, but with this comprehensive guide, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect one. From FabIndia to Manyavar, Khadi, and W for Woman, there's a brand out there for every style and budget. Consider the occasion, your personal style, and the fit when making your decision, and don't be afraid to try something new. Happy shopping!
