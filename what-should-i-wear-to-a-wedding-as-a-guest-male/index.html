What Should I Wear To A Wedding As A Guest Male

Written by Christine Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a wedding as a guest male can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to deciding on the appropriate attire. You want to look stylish and presentable without overshadowing the groom or being underdressed. In this article, we will guide you on what to wear to a wedding as a guest male, taking into consideration the type of wedding and dress code.

Deciding on what to wear to a wedding can be stressful for most men. You don't want to be underdressed or overdressed for the occasion. You also don't want to clash with the wedding party or other guests. The key is to strike a balance between looking stylish and presentable without stealing the spotlight from the groom.

When deciding on what to wear to a wedding as a guest male, you need to consider the type of wedding and dress code. Is the wedding a black-tie event, a beach wedding, or a casual ceremony? The dress code will give you an idea of what to wear, and you can then choose an appropriate outfit that fits the occasion.

In summary, what you wear to a wedding as a guest male is dependent on the type of wedding and dress code. You should aim to look stylish and presentable without drawing too much attention to yourself.

Formal Wedding

When attending a formal wedding, a classic black or navy suit is a safe go-to outfit. Pair it with a white or light-colored dress shirt, a tie, and dress shoes. You can also add a pocket square or cufflinks to add some personality to your outfit. Avoid bright-colored suits or flashy accessories that will draw attention away from the groom.

Beach Wedding

Beach weddings are usually more casual, and you can opt for a lighter-colored suit or even a blazer and dress pants combination. A linen shirt or a patterned shirt paired with dress shoes or sandals will complete the look. Avoid wearing shorts or flip flops to a beach wedding as they are too casual.

Casual Wedding

A casual wedding dress code allows for more flexibility in what you can wear. You can wear a dress shirt with dress pants or khakis and loafers or dress shoes. A blazer or sport coat can be added to elevate the outfit. Avoid wearing jeans or sneakers to a casual wedding as they are too informal.

Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and you should choose them carefully. A tie, pocket square, or cufflinks can add personality to your outfit, but avoid anything too flashy or bright-colored. A watch or a belt can also complete the look, but ensure they match the color of your shoes.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a tuxedo to a wedding as a guest male?

A: It depends on the dress code of the wedding. If the wedding is a black-tie event, then a tuxedo is appropriate. However, if the dress code is not specified, then a tuxedo may be too formal and draw attention away from the groom.

Q: Can I wear a colored suit to a wedding as a guest male?

A: Yes, but ensure the color is not too bright or flashy. A navy, gray, or even a light-colored suit is appropriate for most weddings, but avoid wearing a suit that is too casual or informal.

Q: Can I wear sneakers to a wedding as a guest male?

A: No, sneakers are too casual for a wedding. Instead, opt for dress shoes or loafers that match your outfit.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a wedding as a guest male?

A: It depends on the type of hat and the dress code. A fedora or a Panama hat can add some personality to your outfit, but ensure it matches the dress code and does not draw too much attention away from the groom.

Conclusion of What Should I Wear to a Wedding as a Guest Male

Attending a wedding as a guest male can be stressful, but it doesn't have to be. By considering the type of wedding and dress code, you can choose an appropriate outfit that is stylish and presentable without overshadowing the groom. Remember to avoid anything too bright or flashy and ensure your accessories match your outfit. With these tips, you can confidently attend any wedding as a guest male.
