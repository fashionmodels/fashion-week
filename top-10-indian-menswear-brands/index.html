Top 10 Indian Menswear Brands A Guide To The Best

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you on the hunt for the best Indian menswear brands? Look no further.

It can be overwhelming to navigate the world of menswear, but investing in high-quality pieces from top brands can make all the difference. From traditional sherwanis to modern suits, Indian menswear has something for everyone.

In this guide, we will be exploring the top 10 Indian menswear brands and what sets them apart.

1. Manyavar

Manyavar is one of the most well-known Indian menswear brands, and for good reason. They specialize in traditional wear, including sherwanis, kurtas, and dhotis. Their designs are timeless and elegant, featuring intricate embroidery and high-quality fabrics.

Personally, I have worn Manyavar for weddings and other formal events, and have always received compliments on my outfit. Their attention to detail and commitment to quality make them a standout brand in the Indian menswear market.

2. Raymond

Raymond has been a trusted name in menswear for over 90 years. They offer a wide range of products, from suits and blazers to casual wear and accessories. Their suits are especially noteworthy, with impeccable tailoring and classic designs.

I have purchased several suits from Raymond over the years and have always been impressed with the fit and quality. If you're in the market for a new suit, Raymond is definitely worth checking out.

3. Fabindia

Fabindia is a brand that celebrates India's rich textile heritage. They offer a range of products, including menswear, womenswear, and home decor. Their menswear collection features a mix of traditional and contemporary designs, with a focus on natural fabrics and handcrafted details.

I recently purchased a kurta from Fabindia and was blown away by the quality and comfort. It's the perfect blend of style and practicality.

4. Manyavar Mohey

Manyavar Mohey is a newer addition to the Manyavar family, offering more modern and trendy designs. Their collection includes everything from suits and jackets to fusion wear and accessories. Their designs are perfect for the fashion-forward man who wants to make a statement.

I haven't personally worn Manyavar Mohey, but have seen their designs on others and have been impressed with the creativity and attention to detail.

5. Peter England

Peter England is a brand that offers high-quality, affordable menswear. They are known for their wide range of products, including formal wear, casual wear, and accessories. Their designs are classic and timeless, making them a great option for everyday wear.

I have several shirts and trousers from Peter England and have always been happy with the quality and fit. Their products are perfect for the budget-conscious man who still wants to look good.

6. Louis Philippe

Louis Philippe is a premium menswear brand that offers a range of products, including suits, shirts, trousers, and accessories. Their designs are elegant and sophisticated, with a focus on clean lines and classic silhouettes.

I have worn Louis Philippe for formal events and have always felt confident and stylish. Their suits are especially noteworthy, with impeccable tailoring and luxurious fabrics.

7. Blackberrys

Blackberrys is a brand that offers contemporary menswear with a focus on innovation and style. They offer a range of products, from formal wear to casual wear, and are known for their attention to detail and quality fabrics.

I haven't personally worn Blackberrys, but have seen their designs on others and have been impressed with the modern and edgy aesthetic.

8. Indian Terrain

Indian Terrain is a brand that offers casual wear with a focus on comfort and functionality. Their designs are inspired by the outdoors and are perfect for the adventurous man. They offer a range of products, including shirts, trousers, and jackets.

I have several shirts from Indian Terrain and love how comfortable and stylish they are. They are perfect for a day out or a casual dinner with friends.

9. Allen Solly

Allen Solly is a brand that offers contemporary menswear with a focus on color and pattern. They offer a range of products, from formal wear to casual wear, and are known for their bold designs and playful aesthetic.

I haven't personally worn Allen Solly, but have seen their designs on others and have been impressed with the fun and quirky vibe.

10. Arrow

Arrow is a brand that offers classic menswear with a focus on quality and sophistication. They offer a range of products, including formal wear, casual wear, and accessories. Their designs are timeless and elegant, making them a great option for any occasion.

I have several shirts from Arrow and have always been impressed with the quality and fit. Their products are perfect for the man who wants to look polished and put together.

Conclusion of Top 10 Indian Menswear Brands

Investing in high-quality menswear from top Indian brands is a great way to elevate your style and feel confident in any situation. Whether you're looking for traditional wear or contemporary designs, there is something for everyone on this list.

Question and Answer

Q: What sets Indian menswear apart from Western menswear?

A: Indian menswear is known for its intricate embroidery, bold colors, and luxurious fabrics. It often features traditional designs and silhouettes, making it a unique and distinctive option.

Q: Are these brands affordable?

A: The price points vary for each brand, but there are options for every budget. Some brands, like Peter England and Indian Terrain, offer more affordable options, while others, like Manyavar and Louis Philippe, are more high-end.

Q: Where can I purchase these brands?

A: Many of these brands have their own stores and websites, and are also available at multi-brand retailers like Myntra and Jabong.

Q: Are these brands only for Indian men?

A: Not at all! While these brands specialize in Indian menswear, they offer a range of products that can appeal to anyone. Many of their designs are versatile and can be worn by men of all backgrounds.
