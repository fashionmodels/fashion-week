Popular Clothing Stores Of The 80S

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a fan of 80s fashion? Do you miss the days of neon colors, big hair, and shoulder pads? If so, you're not alone. The 80s was a decade of bold fashion statements and iconic styles that are still influencing fashion today. One of the most memorable aspects of 80s fashion was the popular clothing stores of the time. In this blog post, we'll take a trip down memory lane and explore some of the most popular clothing stores of the 80s.

The 80s was a time when fashion was all about making a statement. It was a decade of excess, and this was reflected in the clothing stores of the time. From brightly colored clothing to oversized accessories, 80s fashion was all about standing out from the crowd. However, with so many different clothing stores to choose from, it could be overwhelming for shoppers to find the perfect outfit.

If you're looking to recreate the iconic 80s look, it's important to understand the popular clothing stores of the time. By knowing where to shop, you can find the perfect outfit to channel your inner 80s icon.

In this post, we'll explore some of the most popular clothing stores of the 80s and what made them so iconic. From department stores to specialty boutiques, there were a variety of options for 80s shoppers. We'll also take a look at some of the key fashion trends of the 80s and how these were reflected in popular clothing stores of the time.

1. The Limited

The Limited was a popular clothing store in the 80s that catered to young professional women. The store was known for its stylish and affordable clothing, which was perfect for women who wanted to look fashionable without breaking the bank. The Limited was also known for its bold advertising campaigns, which featured young women in powerful positions.

Personally, I remember shopping at The Limited for my first job interview outfit. I found a stylish blazer and skirt combo that made me feel confident and professional.

2. Esprit

Esprit was a popular clothing store in the 80s that was known for its colorful and playful clothing. The store was founded in San Francisco in the 1960s, but it wasn't until the 80s that it really took off. Esprit was known for its bright colors and bold prints, which were perfect for the fashion-forward youth of the time.

I remember wearing an Esprit t-shirt with neon colors and bold graphics to my first day of middle school. It was the perfect way to make a statement and show off my personal style.

3. Benetton

Benetton was a popular clothing store in the 80s that was known for its colorful and diverse clothing. The store was founded in Italy in the 1960s, but it wasn't until the 80s that it became a global phenomenon. Benetton was known for its bold advertising campaigns, which featured models of different races and ethnicities.

One of my favorite memories of Benetton was seeing their United Colors of Benetton ads in magazines. I loved how the ads celebrated diversity and showed that fashion was for everyone.

4. Macy's

Macy's is a department store that has been around for over 150 years, but it was in the 80s that it really became a fashion destination. Macy's was known for its wide selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories, which made it a go-to destination for shoppers of all ages.

I remember going to Macy's with my mom to shop for back-to-school clothes. We would spend hours browsing the racks and trying on different outfits. It was always a fun and exciting experience.

Question and Answer

Q: What were some of the key fashion trends of the 80s?

A: Some of the key fashion trends of the 80s included neon colors, oversized accessories, acid-washed denim, and shoulder pads.

Q: What was the most popular type of clothing in the 80s?

A: One of the most popular types of clothing in the 80s was the power suit. This was a tailored suit with shoulder pads that was worn by women in the workforce to assert their authority and professionalism.

Q: What made the 80s such an iconic decade for fashion?

A: The 80s was an iconic decade for fashion because it was a time of bold statements and excess. Fashion was about making a statement and standing out from the crowd, which led to some truly memorable styles and trends.

Q: Are any of the popular clothing stores of the 80s still around today?

A: Yes, some of the popular clothing stores of the 80s are still around today. Macy's and The Limited are still in business, although their styles have evolved to reflect modern fashion trends.

Conclusion of Popular Clothing Stores of the 80s

The 80s was a decade of iconic fashion and memorable styles. The popular clothing stores of the time played a big role in shaping these trends and helping shoppers find the perfect outfit to express their personal style. From The Limited to Macy's, these stores were a fashion destination for shoppers of all ages. By understanding the popular clothing stores of the 80s, you can recreate the iconic styles of the time and channel your inner 80s icon.
