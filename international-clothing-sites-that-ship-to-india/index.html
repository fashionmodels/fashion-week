International Clothing Sites That Ship To India Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of not being able to shop at your favorite international clothing sites because they don't ship to India? Do you find it frustrating to constantly search for alternatives that may not meet your style preferences? Look no further, because we have compiled a list of international clothing sites that ship to India, so you can shop till you drop without any worries.

Shopping for clothes online can be a daunting experience, especially when it comes to international sites. The fear of not receiving your order, dealing with customs and duties, and not being able to try on clothes before purchasing can be overwhelming. However, with the right knowledge and guidance, shopping at international clothing sites can be a breeze.

So, let's get into it. Here are some of the best international clothing sites that ship to India.

ASOS

ASOS is a UK-based online fashion and beauty retailer that ships to over 200 countries, including India. The site offers a wide range of clothing, shoes, accessories, and beauty products for both men and women at affordable prices. They also have a dedicated section for plus-size fashion and tall sizes, making it an inclusive shopping destination.

I personally love shopping at ASOS because of their vast selection of styles, fast shipping, and easy returns. I have never faced any issues with customs or duties, and the customer service is excellent.

Boohoo

Boohoo is a UK-based online fashion retailer that ships to over 100 countries, including India. They offer trendy and affordable clothing, shoes, and accessories for both men and women, with new arrivals added daily. Boohoo also has a section dedicated to sustainable fashion, making it a great option for conscious shoppers.

I have personally shopped at Boohoo multiple times and have always been happy with my purchases. The shipping is fast, and the clothes are of good quality for the price. However, I have faced customs and duty charges a few times, so it's important to keep that in mind when placing an order.

Zara

Zara is a Spanish fast-fashion retailer that ships to India. They offer a range of clothing, shoes, and accessories for both men and women, with new collections released every season. Zara is known for its trendy and stylish pieces, with a focus on quality and affordability.

I love shopping at Zara for their unique and stylish pieces that are also of good quality. The shipping is fast, and I have never faced any issues with customs or duties. The only downside is that the sizes can be a bit inconsistent, so it's important to read the size guide before purchasing.

Shein

Shein is a China-based online fashion retailer that ships to over 220 countries, including India. They offer a wide range of trendy and affordable clothing, shoes, and accessories for both men and women, with new arrivals added daily. Shein is known for its fast fashion and low prices, making it a great option for those on a budget.

Personally, I have had mixed experiences with Shein. While the clothes are affordable and trendy, the quality can be hit or miss, and the sizing can be inconsistent. It's important to read reviews and check the size guide before placing an order. Additionally, I have faced customs and duty charges a few times, so it's important to keep that in mind when shopping at Shein.

Conclusion of International Clothing Sites That Ship to India

Shopping at international clothing sites can be a great way to expand your style options and find unique pieces that may not be available in India. However, it's important to do your research and keep in mind any potential customs and duty charges when placing an order. With this guide, you can now shop confidently at some of the best international clothing sites that ship to India.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it safe to shop at international clothing sites that ship to India?

A: Yes, it is safe to shop at international clothing sites that ship to India as long as you do your research and shop from reputable sites.

Q: Will I face customs and duty charges when shopping at international clothing sites?

A: It is possible to face customs and duty charges when shopping at international clothing sites, especially if the order value exceeds a certain amount. It's important to keep this in mind when placing an order.

Q: How long does shipping take when ordering from international clothing sites?

A: The shipping time varies depending on the site and your location. Some sites offer express shipping options that can be delivered within a few days, while others may take a few weeks to arrive.

Q: Can I return items that I purchase from international clothing sites?

A: Yes, most international clothing sites offer returns and exchanges. However, it's important to read the return policy before placing an order to ensure that you are eligible for a return.
