Find The Best Leather Jacket In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for the perfect leather jacket to add to your wardrobe? Look no further than India for some of the best options on the market. But with so many different styles, materials, and brands to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start your search. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to find the best leather jacket in India for your individual taste and needs.

When it comes to choosing a leather jacket, there are a few key factors to consider. First, think about your personal style and the purpose of the jacket. Are you looking for a classic, timeless piece that you can wear for years to come? Or do you want something more trendy and fashion-forward? You'll also want to consider the fit and size of the jacket, as well as the type of leather and any additional features, such as pockets, zippers, or lining.

Now that you have a better idea of what to look for in a leather jacket, let's dive into some of the best options available in India.

Classic Leather Jackets

If you're looking for a timeless, versatile leather jacket that you can wear with anything, then a classic option is the way to go. These jackets typically feature a simple, streamlined design with minimal embellishments or details. Some of the best brands for classic leather jackets in India include Hidesign, Blackberrys, and Allen Solly.

Personally, I love my Hidesign leather jacket. It's made from high-quality leather that has only gotten better with age and wear. The fit is perfect, and the jacket looks great dressed up or down. Plus, the brand offers a wide range of classic styles to choose from, so you're sure to find something that suits your taste.

Biker Jackets

If you're looking for a leather jacket that's a bit more edgy and stylish, then a biker jacket might be the perfect option for you. These jackets typically feature a more rugged design with lots of zippers, studs, and other details. Some great brands for biker jackets in India include Royal Enfield, Harley Davidson, and Wildcraft.

I recently purchased a Wildcraft biker jacket, and I love how it instantly adds a cool factor to any outfit. The leather is thick and durable, and the jacket is surprisingly warm despite its lightweight feel. I also appreciate the attention to detail in the design, such as the asymmetrical zipper and the quilted shoulder panels.

Shearling Jackets

For those colder months, a shearling jacket is the perfect option for both style and warmth. These jackets are typically made from leather on the outside with a soft, fluffy shearling lining on the inside. Some great brands for shearling jackets in India include U.S. Polo Assn., Louis Philippe, and Levi's.

I've had my eye on a Louis Philippe shearling jacket for a while now, and I love the way it looks and feels. The leather is soft and supple, and the shearling lining is incredibly cozy. The jacket also features some nice details, such as the ribbed cuffs and hem and the large collar.

Caring for Your Leather Jacket

Once you've found the perfect leather jacket in India, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts for years to come. Here are a few tips for keeping your jacket looking its best:

  • Store your jacket in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat.
  • Avoid getting your jacket wet, and if it does get wet, let it dry naturally away from any heat sources.
  • Use a leather conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple, and to prevent cracking or drying out.
  • Avoid wearing your jacket in extremely hot or cold temperatures, as this can damage the leather.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it better to buy a leather jacket in-store or online?

A: It's really a matter of personal preference. If you prefer to try on jackets before you buy them, then shopping in-store might be the way to go. However, if you're comfortable with ordering online and returning if necessary, then shopping online can give you access to a wider variety of options.

Q: How do I know if a leather jacket fits properly?

A: A properly fitting leather jacket should feel snug but not tight, with enough room to move your arms comfortably. The sleeves should hit just at or slightly above your wrists, and the length should hit at your waist or hips.

Q: What's the best way to clean a leather jacket?

A: It's generally best to leave cleaning your leather jacket to the professionals. However, if you need to spot clean a small area, you can use a damp cloth and mild soap, and then let the area air dry.

Q: How do I know if a leather jacket is made from high-quality leather?

A: Look for jackets made from full-grain or top-grain leather, as these are typically the highest quality options. You can also check for any imperfections or inconsistencies in the leather, as these can be signs of lower quality.

Conclusion of Best Leather Jacket India

Whether you're looking for a classic, edgy, or cozy leather jacket, India has plenty of options to choose from. By considering your personal style, fit and size, and the type of leather and features you want, you can find the perfect jacket to add to your wardrobe. And by taking proper care of your jacket, you can enjoy it for years to come.
