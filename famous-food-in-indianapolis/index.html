Famous Food in Indianapolis: A Guide to the City's Best Eats

Are you visiting Indianapolis and looking for the best food spots? Or are you a local who wants to explore the city's culinary scene? Look no further because this guide will take you on a delicious journey of the famous food in Indianapolis.

Indianapolis is known for its diverse food culture, from classic American dishes to international cuisine. But with so many options, it can be overwhelming to decide where to go and what to try. That's why we've put together a list of the must-try famous food in Indianapolis.

Let's dive into the famous food scene in Indianapolis and satisfy your cravings.

Famous Food in Indianapolis: The Classic Indianapolis Sandwich

One of the most famous food in Indianapolis is the breaded pork tenderloin sandwich. It's a local favorite that you can find in most restaurants and diners in the city. The sandwich is made of a pork tenderloin that's breaded and deep-fried, then served on a bun with toppings such as lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise. It's a must-try if you're in Indianapolis, and some of the best places to get it are Mug-n-Bun, The Tamale Place, and Shapiro's Delicatessen.

Famous Food in Indianapolis: International Cuisine

Indianapolis has a diverse food scene that includes international cuisine. One of the most famous international dishes in Indianapolis is the chicken tikka masala. It's a creamy, tomato-based curry dish that originated in India and is now a popular dish in many Indian restaurants in the city. You can try it at places like India Garden, Spice Nation, and Shalimar Indian Restaurant.

Famous Food in Indianapolis: The Uniquely Indy Pizza

If you're a pizza lover, you have to try the famous food in Indianapolis called the "Hoosier" pizza. It's a pizza that's loaded with toppings such as sausage, pepperoni, bacon, and mushrooms. Some places even add local ingredients like corn and maple syrup to make it uniquely Indy. You can find this pizza at places like Jockamo Upper Crust Pizza, Bazbeaux Pizza, and Puccini's Smiling Teeth Pizza.

Famous Food in Indianapolis: The Sweet Treats

No meal is complete without a sweet treat, and Indianapolis has some famous dessert spots. One of the most famous is the Long's Bakery, which has been serving up fresh, delicious donuts since 1955. Another must-visit spot is the Flying Cupcake, which offers a variety of cupcakes with unique flavors like bacon maple, red velvet, and lemon raspberry.

Famous Food in Indianapolis: Question and Answer

Q: What is the most famous dish in Indianapolis?
A: The breaded pork tenderloin sandwich is one of the most famous dishes in Indianapolis.

Q: Where can I find the best chicken tikka masala in Indianapolis?
A: Some of the best places to try chicken tikka masala in Indianapolis are India Garden, Spice Nation, and Shalimar Indian Restaurant.

Q: What is the Hoosier pizza?
A: The Hoosier pizza is a pizza that's loaded with toppings like sausage, pepperoni, bacon, and mushrooms. Some places even add local ingredients like corn and maple syrup to make it unique to Indy.

Q: What are some famous dessert spots in Indianapolis?
A: Long's Bakery and the Flying Cupcake are two famous dessert spots in Indianapolis.

Conclusion of Famous Food in Indianapolis

Indianapolis has a lot to offer when it comes to food, from classic American dishes to international cuisine. The famous food in Indianapolis is a mix of unique dishes that reflect the city's diverse food culture. Whether you're a local or visiting the city, make sure to try the must-try dishes on our list to satisfy your cravings.
