Question and Answer:

Q: What are some common spices used in Indian cuisine?

A: Some common spices used in Indian cuisine include cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala.

Q: What's the difference between curry and masala?

A: Curry is a dish that typically contains meat or vegetables in a sauce made with a blend of spices, while masala refers to a spice blend that is commonly used in Indian cuisine.

Q: Is Indian food spicy?

A: Indian cuisine can be spicy, but not all dishes are spicy. It largely depends on the spices used in the dish.

Q: What's the difference between naan and roti?

A: Naan is a leavened bread made with white flour and typically brushed with butter or ghee, while roti is an unleavened bread made with whole wheat flour.
