Question and Answer Section

Q: What's the difference between 100% agave tequila and mixed tequila?

A: 100% agave tequila is made entirely from agave plants, while mixed tequila is made with a combination of agave and other sugars (such as cane sugar). 100% agave tequila is generally considered to be of higher quality and has a more authentic taste.

Q: How should I store my Mexican tequila?

A: Tequila should be stored in a cool, dark place (such as a pantry or liquor cabinet) away from direct sunlight. Once opened, tequila should be consumed within a few months to ensure optimal flavor.

Q: What's the best way to taste tequila?

A: To fully appreciate the flavors and aromas of tequila, it's best to taste it neat at room temperature or over a large ice cube. Take a small sip and allow the tequila to sit on your tongue for a few seconds before swallowing. This will allow you to fully experience the flavor profile of the spirit.

Q: Can I use Mexican tequila in cocktails?

A: Absolutely! Mexican tequila is a popular ingredient in many classic cocktails, including the margarita, Paloma, and Tequila Sunrise. Just be sure to use a high-quality tequila that's suited for mixing.

Conclusion of Good Mexican Tequila Brands

By choosing a high-quality bottle made with 100% agave and traditional production methods, you can enjoy the true taste of Mexico in every sip.
