Tailored Fit Vs Regular Fit Suit Which One Is Right For You

Written by Eveline May 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right suit can be a daunting task, especially when you're faced with the decision of whether to go for a tailored fit or a regular fit suit. But fear not, we're here to guide you through this process and help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to buying a suit, there are many factors to consider, such as the occasion, the color, and the fabric. But one of the most important decisions you'll have to make is whether to choose a tailored fit or a regular fit suit. This decision can be particularly challenging if you're not familiar with the differences between the two options.

The main difference between a tailored fit and a regular fit suit is the way they are cut. A tailored fit suit is designed to fit closely to your body, while a regular fit suit is cut with more room in the chest and waist area.

If you're looking for a suit that is both stylish and comfortable, a tailored fit suit might be the way to go. It's a great option for those who want a modern look without sacrificing comfort. On the other hand, if you prefer a more traditional look with a bit of breathing room, a regular fit suit might be a better choice.

My Experience with Tailored Fit vs Regular Fit Suit

When I was shopping for my first suit, I wasn't sure which fit to go for. I tried on both a tailored fit and a regular fit suit and found that the tailored fit suit looked much better on me. It was more fitted and made me look slimmer, which was a confidence booster. Plus, it was comfortable to wear, even during long events.

However, I also noticed that the regular fit suit was more comfortable in the chest and waist area. While it didn't look as stylish as the tailored fit suit, it was still a great option for more relaxed occasions.

When to Choose a Tailored Fit Suit

If you're attending a formal event or an important business meeting, a tailored fit suit is the way to go. It's a stylish option that will help you make a great first impression. It's also a great choice if you want to show off your physique and look slimmer.

When to Choose a Regular Fit Suit

A regular fit suit is a great choice for more relaxed occasions, such as weddings or family gatherings. It's also a great option if you prefer a more traditional look or if you want a bit more room to move around in.

Factors to Consider When Choosing Between Tailored Fit vs Regular Fit Suit

When deciding between a tailored fit and a regular fit suit, there are several factors to consider:

  • Your body type: If you have a slim or athletic build, a tailored fit suit will flatter your physique. If you have a larger build, a regular fit suit might be more comfortable.
  • The occasion: Tailored fit suits are great for formal events, while regular fit suits are more appropriate for relaxed occasions.
  • Your personal style: If you prefer a more modern look, a tailored fit suit might be the way to go. If you prefer a more traditional look, a regular fit suit might be a better choice.
  • Your comfort level: If you're not comfortable wearing a suit that is too tight, a regular fit suit might be a better option for you.

How to Get the Perfect Fit

Regardless of whether you choose a tailored fit or a regular fit suit, it's important to get the perfect fit. Here are some tips:

  • Get measured by a professional tailor to ensure that the suit fits you perfectly.
  • Make sure the suit jacket is the correct length and covers your backside.
  • The sleeves of the suit jacket should end at your wrist bone.
  • The trousers of the suit should sit comfortably on your waist and be hemmed to the correct length.

Conclusion of Tailored Fit vs Regular Fit Suit

Choosing between a tailored fit and a regular fit suit ultimately comes down to personal preference and the occasion. A tailored fit suit is a great option for formal events or if you want a modern look, while a regular fit suit is ideal for more relaxed occasions or if you prefer a traditional look. Regardless of which fit you choose, it's important to ensure that the suit fits you perfectly to look your best.

Question and Answer

Q: Can a tailored fit suit be altered to fit more like a regular fit suit?

A: Yes, a tailored fit suit can be altered to fit more like a regular fit suit. A professional tailor can make adjustments to the suit to ensure that it fits you comfortably.

Q: Are tailored fit suits more expensive than regular fit suits?

A: Tailored fit suits can be more expensive than regular fit suits, as they require more time and effort to create. However, the price difference can vary depending on the brand and the fabric used.

Q: Can a regular fit suit be tailored to fit more like a tailored fit suit?

A: Yes, a regular fit suit can be altered to fit more like a tailored fit suit. However, the extent to which the suit can be altered depends on the original cut of the suit and the fabric used.

Q: What should I consider when choosing the color of my suit?

A: When choosing the color of your suit, consider the occasion and your personal style. For formal events, a dark-colored suit such as navy or black is a safe choice. For more relaxed occasions, you can opt for lighter colors such as gray or beige.
