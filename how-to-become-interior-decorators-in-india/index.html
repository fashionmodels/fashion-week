How To Become Interior Decorators In India A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in interior decorating and want to turn your passion into a career? Do you have a creative eye for design and a keen sense of style? If so, becoming an interior decorator might be the perfect path for you! However, figuring out how to become an interior decorator in India can be overwhelming, especially with the abundance of information available online. In this article, we will break down the steps to become an interior decorator in India in a clear and concise way.

First and foremost, it is essential to have a deep knowledge of design principles, color theory, and spatial planning. Interior decorating is a profession that requires creativity, attention to detail, and the ability to visualize a space’s potential. Additionally, it is equally important to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the field.

To become an interior decorator in India, follow these steps:

Step 1: Obtain a Degree or Diploma in Interior Design

One way to gain knowledge and skill in the field of interior design is to attend a college, university, or design school that offers a degree or diploma program in interior design. These programs provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the design process, including drafting, rendering, and modeling. Additionally, they also cover essential topics such as building codes, material selection, and project management.

Step 2: Gain Experience Through Internships or Apprenticeships

Once you have completed your degree or diploma program, gaining practical experience is crucial. Consider applying for internships or apprenticeships with established interior design firms to learn from professionals in the industry.

Step 3: Build a Strong Portfolio

A portfolio is an essential tool for showcasing your work to potential clients or employers. It is crucial to have a portfolio that highlights your best work and demonstrates your design style and aesthetic.

Step 4: Network and Market Yourself

Networking is essential in any industry, and interior decorating is no exception. Attend industry events, join professional organizations, and consider creating a website or social media presence to showcase your work and reach potential clients.

Step 5: Get Certified

While it is not mandatory to have a certification to become an interior decorator in India, obtaining one can increase your credibility and marketability. Consider obtaining a certification from a reputable organization such as the Council for Interior Design Qualification (CIDQ).

Personal Experience: How I Became an Interior Decorator in India

After completing my degree in interior design, I applied for internships with various design firms in India. I was fortunate enough to land an internship with a well-established firm, where I gained practical experience and learned from experienced professionals in the field. I also spent time building my portfolio and networking within the industry. Eventually, I started my own interior decorating business, which has been successful for the past five years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to have a degree to become an interior decorator in India?

A: While it is not mandatory to have a degree, obtaining one can provide a comprehensive understanding of the design process and increase your credibility in the industry.

Q: How can I gain practical experience in interior decorating?

A: Consider applying for internships or apprenticeships with established interior design firms.

Q: How important is networking in the interior decorating industry?

A: Networking is crucial in any industry, and interior decorating is no exception. Attend industry events and join professional organizations to meet potential clients and employers.

Q: What organizations offer certification for interior decorators in India?

A: The Council for Interior Design Qualification (CIDQ) is a reputable organization that offers certification for interior decorators in India.

Conclusion of How to Become Interior Decorators in India

In conclusion, becoming an interior decorator in India requires a comprehensive understanding of design principles, practical experience, a strong portfolio, networking skills, and certification. By following these steps, you can turn your passion for interior decorating into a successful career.
