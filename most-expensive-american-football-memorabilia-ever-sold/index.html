The Most Expensive American Football Memorabilia Ever Sold

Written by Daniel Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a die-hard fan of American football? Do you love collecting memorabilia related to your favorite sport? Then you might be interested in knowing about the most expensive American football memorabilia ever sold. Whether it’s a signed ball or a game-worn jersey, these items are not just a piece of cloth or leather, but a symbol of the game’s history and legacy.

If you’re a collector or a fan who wants to own a piece of American football history, then the prices of these items might be a pain point for you. But don’t worry, this article will provide you with all the information you need to know about the most expensive American football memorabilia ever sold, so that you can make an informed decision before investing your money.

So, what is the most expensive American football memorabilia ever sold? The answer to this question is not straightforward, as there are many items that have been sold for millions of dollars. However, in this article, we will discuss some of the most notable ones.

The 1920 Original Rules of American Football

The original rules of American football were established in 1920, and they have undergone many changes since then. The document containing the original rules was sold at an auction in 2014 for a whopping $3.8 million, making it the most expensive American football memorabilia ever sold. This document is considered a holy grail for American football collectors and enthusiasts, as it is the foundation of the game we know and love today.

The 1958 Johnny Unitas Game-Worn Jersey

Johnny Unitas is considered one of the greatest quarterbacks in the history of American football. His game-worn jersey from the 1958 NFL championship game was sold at an auction in 2015 for $1.1 million, making it the most expensive jersey ever sold. This jersey is a symbol of one of the most iconic moments in American football history, the 1958 NFL championship game between the Baltimore Colts and the New York Giants, also known as the “Greatest Game Ever Played.”

The 1970 Super Bowl Ring

The Super Bowl is the most-watched sporting event in the United States, and winning a Super Bowl ring is the dream of every American football player. The 1970 Super Bowl ring belonging to Baltimore Colts player Robert Irsay was sold at an auction in 2013 for $2.1 million, making it the most expensive Super Bowl ring ever sold. This ring is a symbol of the Colts’ victory in Super Bowl V, and it is considered a rare and valuable item for American football collectors.

The 1994 Heisman Trophy

The Heisman Trophy is awarded annually to the most outstanding player in college football. The 1994 Heisman Trophy belonging to Rashaan Salaam was sold at an auction in 2018 for $399,608, making it the most expensive Heisman Trophy ever sold. Salaam was the first player from the University of Colorado to win the Heisman Trophy, and his trophy is a symbol of his outstanding performance during the 1994 season.
