What To Wear To An East Indian Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Attending an East Indian wedding can be a wonderful experience, full of vibrant colors, delicious food, and lively music. However, one of the most challenging aspects of attending an East Indian wedding is figuring out what to wear. With so many customs and traditions to consider, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect outfit. But don't worry, in this guide, we'll provide you with everything you need to know to dress appropriately for an East Indian wedding.

East Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and traditional attire. The dress code for these weddings is usually formal, with guests expected to wear traditional Indian clothing. However, the specific attire can vary depending on the region, religion, and customs of the wedding.

The general rule of thumb for men is to wear a sherwani, which is a long coat-like garment worn over a kurta and churidar. Women can wear a saree, lehenga choli, or salwar kameez. It's important to keep in mind that these outfits should be modest and cover your shoulders and knees. Avoid wearing revealing or tight-fitting clothes as they may not be appropriate for the occasion.

To summarize, when attending an East Indian wedding, it's essential to dress in traditional Indian attire that is modest and covers your shoulders and knees. Men should wear a sherwani, while women can choose from sarees, lehenga cholis, or salwar kameez.

Choosing the Right Colors

Colors play an important role in Indian weddings, with each color symbolizing something different. For example, red is considered to be a symbol of love and purity, while yellow represents happiness and joy. When choosing your outfit, it's important to consider the colors that are traditionally worn at Indian weddings.

My personal experience at an East Indian wedding was unforgettable. I wore a beautiful red saree, which is the most popular color for brides. As a guest, it's best to avoid wearing white, which is traditionally worn at funerals. Instead, opt for bright colors such as red, pink, orange, or green.

Accessorizing Your Outfit

Accessories are an important part of any Indian outfit. For men, a turban or safa can be worn to complete their look. Women can accessorize their outfit with jewelry, such as bangles, earrings, and necklaces. It's important to keep in mind that your accessories should complement your outfit and not overpower it.

Makeup and Hairstyles

When it comes to makeup and hairstyles, it's best to keep it simple and elegant. For women, a simple bun or braid is a classic choice, while men can style their hair with a pompadour or slicked-back look. As for makeup, a natural look with bold lips or eyes can be a great choice.

What to Wear as a Bridesmaid or Groomsman?

If you're a bridesmaid or groomsman, the couple will usually provide you with specific instructions on what to wear. It's important to follow these instructions as closely as possible to ensure that you're dressed appropriately and don't clash with the rest of the wedding party.

Conclusion of What to Wear to an East Indian Wedding

Attending an East Indian wedding can be a wonderful experience, but figuring out what to wear can be challenging. By following the guidelines in this guide, you can ensure that you're dressed appropriately for the occasion. Remember to choose traditional Indian clothing that is modest and covers your shoulders and knees, choose bright colors, accessorize with jewelry that complements your outfit, keep your makeup and hairstyle simple and elegant, and follow any instructions provided by the wedding party if you're a bridesmaid or groomsman. With these tips, you're sure to make a lasting impression at any East Indian wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a Western-style suit or dress to an East Indian wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing Western-style clothing to an East Indian wedding. Traditional Indian clothing is the norm, and wearing a Western-style suit or dress may make you stand out in a negative way.

Q: Can I wear black to an East Indian wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing black to an East Indian wedding as it's traditionally associated with mourning. Instead, opt for bright colors such as red, pink, orange, or green.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an East Indian wedding?

A: No, jeans are not appropriate for an East Indian wedding. Traditional Indian attire is expected, and wearing jeans may be seen as disrespectful.

Q: Can I wear open-toed shoes to an East Indian wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing open-toed shoes to an East Indian wedding. Closed-toe shoes are more appropriate, and if you're wearing a saree or lehenga choli, you may want to consider wearing traditional Indian footwear such as juttis or mojdis.
