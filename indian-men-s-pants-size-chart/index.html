How to Measure Your Pants Size

To measure your pants size accurately, you need to measure your waist and inseam. To measure your waist, wrap a measuring tape around your waistline, just above your belly button. To measure your inseam, measure the length from your crotch to your ankle. Once you have these measurements, refer to the Indian Men's Pants Size Chart to find your perfect fit.
