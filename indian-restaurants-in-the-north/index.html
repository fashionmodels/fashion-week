Discovering The Best Indian Restaurants In The North

Written by Christine Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a fan of Indian cuisine and looking for the best places to satisfy your cravings in the North? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the top Indian restaurants in the area that are sure to leave you feeling satisfied and eager for more.

When it comes to Indian restaurants in the North, there are many options to choose from. However, finding the right one that offers authentic flavors, exceptional service, and a comfortable ambiance can be challenging. Not to mention, finding a restaurant that caters to dietary restrictions and preferences can be an added struggle.

Our target is to help you find the perfect Indian restaurant that ticks all the boxes and leaves you with a memorable dining experience.

In this article, we will cover some of the best Indian restaurants in the North and highlight their unique features, specialties, and customer reviews.

1. Spice Lounge

Walking into Spice Lounge, you are welcomed by the aroma of Indian spices and flavors that instantly transport you to India. The restaurant is located in the heart of Manchester and offers a contemporary twist on traditional Indian cuisine. Their menu boasts an array of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options that are freshly prepared with locally sourced ingredients.

During my visit, I tried their signature dish, the Lamb Rogan Josh, which was cooked to perfection and had a rich and flavorful sauce. The staff were friendly and attentive, and the ambiance was warm and inviting.

2. Mughli Charcoal Pit

Mughli Charcoal Pit is a family-run restaurant located in Rusholme, Manchester. They specialize in traditional Pakistani and Indian cuisine and have been serving customers for over 20 years. Their menu features a wide range of dishes, from succulent kebabs to flavorful curries.

During my visit, I tried their Mixed Grill, which was a combination of chicken tikka, lamb chops, and seekh kebab. The meat was juicy and tender, and the spices were perfectly balanced. The staff were friendly and knowledgeable and provided excellent recommendations.

3. Akbars

Akbars is a chain of Indian restaurants that have been operating in the North since 1995. They have several branches in cities such as Bradford, Leeds, Manchester, and Newcastle. Their menu features a range of Indian and Pakistani dishes that are cooked to perfection.

During my visit to the Manchester branch, I tried their Chicken Biryani, which was fragrant and bursting with flavors. The portion size was generous, and the staff were accommodating and attentive.

4. Scene Indian Street Kitchen

Located in Spinningfields, Manchester, Scene Indian Street Kitchen offers a unique dining experience that is inspired by the bustling streets of India. Their menu features a range of street food that is freshly prepared and packed with flavors.

During my visit, I tried their Pani Puri, which was a crispy, hollow ball filled with spiced potatoes and topped with tamarind chutney. The burst of flavors and textures was a delight to my taste buds. The staff were friendly and provided excellent service.

Question and Answer

Q: Do these restaurants cater to dietary restrictions?

A: Yes, all the restaurants mentioned in this article offer vegetarian and vegan options, and some also cater to gluten-free and halal dietary restrictions.

Q: Are reservations required?

A: It is recommended to make a reservation, especially during peak hours, to avoid disappointment.

Q: Do these restaurants offer take-out and delivery services?

A: Yes, all the restaurants mentioned in this article offer take-out and delivery services.

Q: Are these restaurants budget-friendly?

A: The prices vary depending on the restaurant and the menu items, but most of the restaurants mentioned offer affordable prices without compromising on flavor and quality.

Conclusion of Indian Restaurants in the North

Whether you are a fan of traditional Indian cuisine or looking for a modern twist on classic dishes, the North has a diverse range of Indian restaurants to offer. From the warm and inviting ambiance of Spice Lounge to the vibrant street food experience at Scene Indian Street Kitchen, there is something for everyone.

So, the next time you are in the North and craving some delicious Indian food, be sure to check out one of these top-rated restaurants for an unforgettable dining experience.
