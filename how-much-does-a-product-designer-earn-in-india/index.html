How Much Does A Product Designer Earn In India

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you curious about the salary of a product designer in India? Do you want to know what factors affect their earnings? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll answer the question of how much does a product designer earn in India and provide valuable insights into this career path.

As a product designer in India, you may face several challenges, including finding the right job, negotiating salary, and dealing with the pressure of deadlines. Additionally, the salary range for product designers in India can vary significantly based on several factors such as experience, location, and the company you work for.

Answering the question of how much does a Product Designer earn in India

The average salary for a product designer in India is around INR 5,00,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on different factors such as experience, education, location, and employer. For example, a product designer in Bangalore can earn up to 20% more than the national average, while a fresher in the same city may earn around INR 2,50,000 per year.

Product designers with more than five years of experience can earn up to INR 15,00,000 per year in India. However, their salary depends on the company they work for, their portfolio, and how they negotiate their salary.

The impact of experience on a Product Designer's salary in India

Experience is one of the most significant factors that can impact a product designer's salary in India. For instance, a fresher with less than a year of experience can expect to earn around INR 2,50,000 per year, while a product designer with 1-4 years of experience can expect to earn INR 4,50,000 to INR 6,00,000 per year. Similarly, product designers with 5-9 years of experience can expect to earn INR 7,00,000 to INR 12,00,000 per year, while those with more than 10 years of experience can earn up to INR 20,00,000 per year or more.

The impact of location on a Product Designer's salary in India

Location is another significant factor that can impact a product designer's salary in India. For instance, product designers in Bangalore and Mumbai can expect to earn higher salaries compared to those in other cities. On average, a product designer in Bangalore can earn up to 20% more than the national average, while a product designer in Mumbai can earn up to 10% more than the national average.

Factors that influence a Product Designer's salary in India

Several factors can influence a product designer's salary in India, including:

  • Experience
  • Education
  • Location
  • Industry
  • Company size
  • Portfolio

Negotiating your salary as a Product Designer in India

When it comes to negotiating your salary as a product designer in India, you need to keep several things in mind. Firstly, research the average salary for your experience level and location. Secondly, prepare a portfolio that showcases your skills and experience. Thirdly, be confident and assertive during the negotiation process, and don't be afraid to ask for what you think you're worth.
