While not exclusively a toy store, Barnes & Noble has an impressive selection of books, games, and toys for kids of all ages. If your child loves stuffed animals, they'll have a blast at Build-A-Bear Workshop. Here, they can create their own custom stuffed animal from start to finish, choosing everything from the type of animal to the outfit and accessories.
