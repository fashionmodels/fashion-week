The Best Hawaiian Clothing Brands For Your Next Tropical Vacation

Written by Luffy Jun 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Aloha! Are you planning a trip to Hawaii soon and wondering what to wear? Look no further than the best Hawaiian clothing brands to make sure you fit right in with the locals and feel comfortable in the tropical climate.

When it comes to finding the perfect clothing for a Hawaiian vacation, you want something that is both stylish and comfortable. You also want to make sure you are supporting local businesses and not just buying generic tourist gear.

In this article, we will be discussing the top Hawaiian clothing brands for your next tropical vacation. We will cover the history and style of each brand, as well as where to find their products and what makes them unique.

Here are the best Hawaiian clothing brands you should know about:

Aloha Beach Club

Founded in 2013 in San Diego, Aloha Beach Club was created with the idea of bringing the laid-back Hawaiian lifestyle to the mainland. Their clothing is inspired by vintage Hawaiian prints and styles, and they use high-quality materials like Japanese cotton and linen. You can find their products online or at their two brick-and-mortar stores in San Diego and Hawaii.

Avanti Shirts

Since 1950, Avanti Shirts has been creating high-quality, custom-made Hawaiian shirts for men. Their shirts are made in Hawaii and feature unique prints and styles that are perfect for any occasion. You can find their products online or at their flagship store in Honolulu.

Reyn Spooner

Founded in 1956, Reyn Spooner is one of the most well-known Hawaiian clothing brands. They are known for their iconic aloha shirts, which feature bold prints and bright colors. Their clothing is made in Hawaii and can be found online or at their flagship store in Honolulu.

How to Style Hawaiian Clothing

When it comes to styling Hawaiian clothing, you want to keep it simple and let the prints do the talking. For men, pair a Hawaiian shirt with shorts or chinos and sandals. For women, a flowy sundress or skirt paired with sandals or wedges is the perfect look for a day at the beach or exploring the island.

Torpedo

Founded in 2012, Torpedo is a newer Hawaiian clothing brand that is making waves with their unique prints and styles. They are known for their bold designs and use of sustainable materials like organic cotton and recycled polyester. You can find their products online or at their flagship store in Honolulu.

Surf Line Hawaii

Surf Line Hawaii has been creating surf-inspired clothing in Hawaii since 1979. Their clothing features bold prints and bright colors, and they use high-quality materials like rayon and cotton. You can find their products online or at their flagship store in Honolulu.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I wear under a Hawaiian shirt?

A: A Hawaiian shirt is typically worn unbuttoned over a t-shirt or tank top. You can also wear it buttoned up for a more formal look.

Q: Can women wear Hawaiian shirts?

A: Absolutely! Hawaiian shirts come in both men's and women's styles, so there is something for everyone.

Q: Are Hawaiian shirts only for tourists?

A: No way! Hawaiian shirts are a staple in many locals' wardrobes and can be worn for any occasion.

Q: What shoes should I wear with Hawaiian clothing?

A: For a casual look, sandals or slip-on sneakers are the perfect choice. For a more formal look, dress shoes or wedges work well.

Conclusion

Whether you are a local or a tourist, wearing Hawaiian clothing is a great way to embrace the tropical lifestyle and feel comfortable in the warm climate. By supporting local Hawaiian clothing brands, you can also feel good about contributing to the local economy and preserving the unique culture of Hawaii.
