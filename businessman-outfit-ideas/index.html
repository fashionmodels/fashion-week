Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a suit?

A: Yes, you can wear sneakers with a suit. However, make sure that the sneakers are clean and in good condition. Stick to classic white sneakers, and avoid bright colors or bold patterns.

Q: Can I wear a turtleneck to the office?

A: Yes, you can wear a turtleneck to the office. However, make sure that the turtleneck is made of high-quality material and fits you well. Stick to classic colors like black, grey, or navy.

Q: Can I wear a denim jacket to a formal event?

A: No, you should not wear a denim jacket to a formal event. Stick to classic blazers or suits for formal events.

Q: Can I wear a cardigan with shorts?

A: No, you should not wear a cardigan with shorts. Stick to trousers or chinos for a more polished look.
