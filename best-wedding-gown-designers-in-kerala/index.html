Top Wedding Gown Designers In Kerala A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Every bride dreams of her perfect wedding dress. It's a once-in-a-lifetime moment that needs to be special and memorable. However, finding the right wedding gown can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. And if you're getting married in Kerala, you may be wondering where to start your search for the perfect dress.

If you're looking for the best wedding gown designers in Kerala, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll introduce you to some of the most talented and sought-after designers in the state. We'll also provide you with some tips on how to choose the right designer and dress for your big day.

First things first, let's talk about what you should look for when choosing a wedding gown designer in Kerala. There are a few things to keep in mind:

Quality of Work

When it comes to wedding gowns, quality is everything. You want a dress that's well-made and looks stunning. Make sure you choose a designer who has a reputation for creating high-quality gowns that stand the test of time.

Style and Aesthetics

Every designer has their own style and aesthetic. Some designers specialize in traditional Indian bridal wear, while others create modern, contemporary dresses. Choose a designer whose style matches your own personal taste.

Price Range

Wedding gowns can be expensive, so it's important to choose a designer whose prices are within your budget.

Experience and Reputation

You want to choose a designer who has plenty of experience creating wedding gowns. Look for designers who have a good reputation and plenty of satisfied customers.

Now that you know what to look for, let's take a look at some of the best wedding gown designers in Kerala:

Label'M

Label'M is a popular designer based in Kochi. They specialize in creating traditional Indian bridal wear with a modern twist. Their designs are known for their intricate embroidery and attention to detail. Their dresses range from elegant and understated to bold and dramatic.

Aswathy Nair

Aswathy Nair is a talented designer based in Thiruvananthapuram. She's known for her unique and innovative designs, which often incorporate traditional Indian textiles and techniques. Her dresses are sophisticated and elegant, with a touch of whimsy.

Beena Kannan

Beena Kannan is a well-known designer based in Thrissur. She's been designing wedding gowns for over 25 years, and her work is highly regarded in the industry. Her designs are classic and timeless, with a focus on traditional Indian bridal wear.

Conclusion

Choosing the right wedding gown designer is an important decision. You want to find someone who can bring your vision to life and create a dress that makes you feel beautiful and confident on your big day. By considering factors like quality of work, style and aesthetics, price range, and experience and reputation, you can find a designer who meets your needs and exceeds your expectations.

Tips for Choosing the Right Wedding Gown Designer

If you're still unsure about how to choose the right wedding gown designer, here are a few tips:

Check out their portfolio and previous work to get a sense of their style and aesthetic.

Read reviews from previous customers to see if they were satisfied with the designer's work.

Ask about their pricing and make sure it fits within your budget.

Meet with the designer in person to discuss your vision and see if you're a good fit.

How to Customize Your Wedding Gown

If you want to customize your wedding gown, there are a few things you can do:

Add or remove sleeves, straps, or other embellishments.

Change the neckline or back of the dress.

Add a train or remove one.

Change the color of the dress.

Question and Answer

Q: How much does a wedding gown designer in Kerala typically cost?

A: The cost of a wedding gown designer in Kerala can vary widely depending on the designer's experience and reputation, as well as the complexity of the design. You can expect to pay anywhere from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 5,00,000 or more.

Q: How far in advance should I book my wedding gown designer?

A: It's a good idea to book your wedding gown designer as soon as possible, especially if you're getting married during peak wedding season. Some designers may require several months to create a custom dress, so make sure you plan ahead.

Q: Can I bring my own fabric to a wedding gown designer?

A: Some wedding gown designers may allow you to bring your own fabric, but it's best to check with them first. Keep in mind that some fabrics may not work well for certain designs, so it's important to discuss your options with the designer.

Q: How many fittings will I need for my wedding gown?

A: The number of fittings you'll need for your wedding gown can vary depending on the complexity of the design and how well it fits initially. Most brides will need at least two or three fittings to ensure the dress fits perfectly.

Conclusion of Best Wedding Gown Designers in Kerala

Choosing the right wedding gown designer is an important decision, but with the right guidance, it can be a stress-free and enjoyable process. By considering factors like quality of work, style and aesthetics, price range, and experience and reputation, you can find a designer who meets your needs and creates a dress you'll cherish for years to come.
