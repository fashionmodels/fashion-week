Mens Indian Wedding Outfits In Melbourne A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Indian weddings are a grand affair, and dressing up for one can be a daunting task. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect outfit. Moreover, living in Melbourne, finding a store that stocks Indian wedding outfits for men can be a challenge. But worry not, we’ve got your back. In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the perfect mens Indian wedding outfit in Melbourne.

When it comes to Indian weddings, the dress code is usually ethnic wear. However, the type of outfit may vary depending on the wedding theme, time of the day, and the role you play in the wedding. It can be confusing to decide what to wear for the various events that take place during an Indian wedding, like the engagement ceremony, sangeet night, wedding ceremony, and reception party.

To make things easier, we have categorized the outfits based on the events:

Engagement Ceremony

The engagement ceremony is usually a formal event, and the dress code is semi-formal to formal. You can opt for a sherwani or a bandhgala suit. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar. It is usually embellished with intricate embroidery and can be paired with a matching dupatta. A bandhgala suit is a type of formal suit that has a Nehru collar and is usually made of silk or velvet. It can be paired with a churidar or formal trousers.

Sangeet Night

The sangeet night is a fun-filled event where the families of the bride and groom come together and celebrate. The dress code is usually semi-formal to formal, and you can opt for a kurta-pyjama or a fusion outfit like a Jodhpuri suit. A Jodhpuri suit is a modern take on the traditional bandhgala suit and has a western-style jacket with a Nehru collar.

Wedding Ceremony

The wedding ceremony is a formal event, and the dress code is usually formal. You can opt for a sherwani or a bandhgala suit, depending on your preference. A sherwani is usually preferred for the groom, while the guests can opt for a bandhgala suit or a three-piece suit. A three-piece suit consists of a suit jacket, waistcoat, and trousers.

Reception Party

The reception party is usually a grand event, and the dress code is formal to black-tie. You can opt for a tuxedo or a three-piece suit. A tuxedo is a type of formal suit that has satin details and is usually worn with a bow tie.

In conclusion, when it comes to selecting the perfect mens Indian wedding outfit in Melbourne, it is important to consider the event, dress code, and your personal style. With the above guide, we hope you find the outfit that makes you look and feel your best on the big day.

The Importance of Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and it’s no different when it comes to Indian wedding outfits. A well-chosen accessory can elevate your outfit and make it stand out. Some popular accessories for mens Indian wedding outfits include:

Turbans

A turban is a traditional headgear worn by men in India. It can be made of various materials like silk, cotton, or velvet and can be plain or embellished with embroidery or jewels. A turban can add a regal touch to your outfit.

Jutti

Jutti is a type of traditional footwear worn by men in India. They are usually made of leather or silk and are embellished with embroidery or embellishments. A well-chosen pair of jutti can complement your outfit and add a touch of elegance.
