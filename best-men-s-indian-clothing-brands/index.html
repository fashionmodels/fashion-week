Discover The Best Men s Indian Clothing Brands For A Modern Look

Written by Bardi Nov 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Indian clothing is known for its vibrant colors, intricate embroidery, and rich cultural heritage. However, finding the right brand that caters to modern sensibilities can be a challenge. Whether you're looking for traditional wear, fusion outfits, or contemporary styles, the options are endless. In this post, we'll explore the best men's Indian clothing brands and help you make an informed choice.

If you're a fashion-conscious man, you know that dressing up is more than just putting on clothes. It's about making a statement, expressing your personality, and feeling confident in your skin. However, finding the right outfit that ticks all the boxes can be a daunting task. From fit to fabric, style to occasion, there are several factors to consider.

The target of this post is to highlight the best men's Indian clothing brands that offer a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. We'll cover the brand's history, design philosophy, signature styles, and product range. By the end of this post, you'll have a better understanding of the Indian fashion landscape and be able to make an informed choice.

Indian fashion has come a long way from the days of traditional dhoti-kurta and sherwani. Today, you can find a range of outfits that cater to different occasions, styles, and budgets. From ethnic wear to fusion outfits, from casual wear to formal wear, the options are endless. Some of the best men's Indian clothing brands that are worth exploring are Fabindia, Manyavar, Biba, Raymond, and Peter England.

Fabindia

Started in 1960, Fabindia is known for its handcrafted textiles, organic fabrics, and sustainable practices. The brand's philosophy is to promote traditional Indian crafts and provide livelihood opportunities to rural artisans. Fabindia's product range includes men's kurtas, jackets, pants, and accessories that are perfect for both casual and formal occasions. Their signature style is a blend of traditional motifs, contemporary cuts, and earthy colors.

Personally, I love Fabindia for its unique designs, comfortable fabrics, and ethical values. Their kurtas are perfect for everyday wear, and their jackets add a touch of elegance to any outfit. I also appreciate the fact that they use natural dyes and organic cotton, which is good for both the environment and the skin.

Manyavar

Founded in 1999, Manyavar is one of the leading men's ethnic wear brands in India. The brand's mission is to make Indian ethnic wear more accessible, affordable, and stylish. Manyavar offers a range of products, including sherwanis, kurtas, jackets, and accessories, that cater to different occasions, styles, and budgets. Their signature style is a fusion of traditional Indian craftsmanship and modern silhouettes.

I have a special place in my heart for Manyavar, as it was the brand that I wore on my wedding day. Their sherwanis are exquisite, with intricate embroidery and rich colors. I also appreciate their attention to detail, from the fit to the finish. Their kurtas are perfect for everyday wear, and their jackets add a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

Biba

Biba is a women's ethnic wear brand that has recently ventured into men's wear. The brand's philosophy is to celebrate the spirit of the modern Indian woman and offer a range of products that cater to different occasions, styles, and budgets. Biba's men's wear collection includes kurtas, jackets, and pants that are perfect for both formal and casual occasions. Their signature style is a fusion of traditional Indian prints, contemporary cuts, and vibrant colors.

Biba is a brand that I've recently discovered, and I'm impressed with their men's wear collection. Their kurtas are comfortable, stylish, and affordable, making them perfect for everyday wear. Their jackets are also worth exploring, as they add a touch of elegance to any outfit.

Raymond

Founded in 1925, Raymond is one of the oldest and most respected brands in India. The brand's mission is to provide high-quality fabrics and garments that cater to the discerning Indian man. Raymond's product range includes suits, jackets, shirts, trousers, and accessories that are perfect for formal occasions. Their signature style is a blend of classic designs, modern cuts, and premium fabrics.

Raymond is a brand that I've grown up with and have a lot of respect for. Their suits are impeccable, with a perfect fit and finish. Their shirts are also worth exploring, as they offer a range of colors and patterns that cater to different styles and occasions.

Peter England

Peter England is a men's casual wear brand that offers a range of products, including shirts, t-shirts, jeans, and accessories. The brand's philosophy is to provide affordable, stylish, and comfortable clothing that caters to the modern Indian man. Peter England's signature style is a blend of classic designs, contemporary cuts, and vibrant colors.

Personally, I appreciate Peter England for its affordability and range of products. Their shirts are comfortable, stylish, and easy to wear, making them perfect for everyday wear. Their t-shirts are also worth exploring, as they offer a range of prints and colors that cater to different styles and occasions.

Question and Answer

Q. What are the best men's Indian clothing brands for weddings?

A. Manyavar, Fabindia, and Biba are some of the best men's Indian clothing brands for weddings. They offer a range of sherwanis, kurtas, and jackets that cater to different styles and budgets.

Q. What are the best men's Indian clothing brands for casual wear?

A. Peter England, Fabindia, and Biba are some of the best men's Indian clothing brands for casual wear. They offer a range of products, including shirts, t-shirts, kurtas, and jackets, that are comfortable, stylish, and affordable.

Q. Where can I buy men's Indian clothing brands?

A. You can buy men's Indian clothing brands online or at their retail stores. Manyavar, Fabindia, Biba, Raymond, and Peter England have their own e-commerce websites and physical stores across India.

Q. What are the latest trends in men's Indian clothing?

A. The latest trends in men's Indian clothing include fusion wear, athleisure wear, and sustainable fashion. Brands like Manyavar, Fabindia, Biba, Raymond, and Peter England are incorporating these trends into their designs and product range.

Conclusion of Best Men's Indian Clothing Brands

Indian fashion is a celebration of culture, tradition, and creativity. With the rise of e-commerce and social media, it's easier than ever to explore different brands and styles. Whether you're looking for traditional wear or contemporary outfits, there's a brand out there that caters to your taste and budget. By exploring the best men's Indian clothing brands, you can add a touch of elegance, sophistication, and style to your wardrobe.
