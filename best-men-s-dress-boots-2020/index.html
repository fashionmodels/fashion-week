Best men's dress boots 2020: Classic Leather Boots

When it comes to dress boots, you can't go wrong with a classic leather pair. Leather is a timeless material that looks great with a variety of outfits and is durable enough to last for years. One of the best options for classic leather dress boots is the Allen Edmonds Fifth Avenue.

Best men's dress boots 2020: Chelsea Boots

Another popular style of dress boots is the Chelsea boot. Chelsea boots are known for their sleek design and elastic side panels, which make them easy to slip on and off. One of the best options for Chelsea boots in 2020 is the Common Projects Chelsea boot.

I recently purchased a pair of Common Projects Chelsea boots and have been extremely happy with them. The leather is high-quality and the boots are incredibly comfortable. The minimalist design looks great with a variety of outfits, from jeans to dress pants.

The Common Projects Chelsea boot is made in Italy and features Blake construction. This means that the sole is directly stitched to the upper, which provides added flexibility and comfort.

Best men's dress boots 2020: How to Care for Your Boots

To ensure that your dress boots last for years to come, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips for caring for your boots:

  • Use a leather conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple
  • Store your boots in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Use a suede brush to remove any dirt or debris from suede boots
  • Apply a suede protector spray to protect against water and stains
