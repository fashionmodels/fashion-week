What Color Shoes To Wear With Black Dress For Wedding A Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Attending a wedding can be a memorable experience, but dressing up for it can be a daunting task. As a woman, choosing the perfect dress is just one part of the equation. You also have to worry about your accessories, including your shoes. What color shoes should you wear with a black dress for a wedding? This guide will help you answer that question.

When it comes to choosing the right shoes to wear with a black dress for a wedding, there are a few pain points to consider. First, you want to ensure that your shoes match the formality of the event. Second, you want to select a shoe color that complements your dress without overpowering it. Lastly, you want to choose a comfortable pair of shoes that you can wear for hours on end.

The answer to what color shoes to wear with a black dress for a wedding is that it depends on the type of wedding you are attending. If you are going to a formal or black-tie wedding, you should opt for black or metallic-colored shoes. For a semi-formal or cocktail wedding, you can choose a colorful shoe that complements the color of your dress. For a casual or beach wedding, you can wear sandals or wedges in neutral colors like beige or nude.

In summary, the key points to remember when choosing shoes to wear with a black dress for a wedding are to match the formality of the event, complement the color of your dress, and prioritize comfort. With those tips in mind, you can confidently select the perfect shoes for any wedding.

Black or Metallic-Colored Shoes for Formal Weddings

When attending a formal or black-tie wedding, your best bet is to stick with black or metallic-colored shoes. Black pumps or strappy sandals are classic choices that will never go out of style. Metallic shoes in gold or silver are also popular options that add a touch of glamour to your outfit.

Personally, I wore a pair of black patent leather pumps with my black dress for a formal wedding, and they looked elegant and timeless. They were also comfortable enough to wear all night long, which was a bonus.

Colorful Shoes for Semi-Formal Weddings

If you are attending a semi-formal or cocktail wedding, you have the freedom to choose a shoe color that complements your dress. You can opt for a shoe in a bold color that stands out, or a shoe in a neutral color that blends in. Some popular shoe colors for semi-formal weddings include red, blue, pink, and green.

For my friend's semi-formal wedding, I wore a pair of nude pumps with my black dress. The nude color matched my skin tone and didn't detract from the dress, but it still added a touch of elegance to my outfit.

Neutral Shoes for Casual or Beach Weddings

If you are attending a casual or beach wedding, you can wear a pair of comfortable sandals or wedges in neutral colors like beige or nude. These colors will match the laid-back vibe of the event and won't stand out too much. Plus, they will keep your feet cool and comfortable in the warm weather.

For a beach wedding I attended, I wore a pair of beige wedge sandals with my black dress. The shoes were comfortable to walk in on the sand and blended in with the beachy surroundings.

How to Choose the Right Shoe Height

When selecting shoes to wear with a black dress for a wedding, you also need to consider the height of the heel or wedge. If you are tall or prefer not to wear heels, you can opt for flats or low wedge sandals. If you want to add some height, you can choose a mid-heel or high-heel shoe. Just make sure you can walk comfortably in them and that they match the formality of the event.

Personally, I prefer to wear mid-heel shoes with my black dress for weddings. They add some height and elegance to my outfit, but they are still comfortable enough to wear for hours on end.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear white shoes with a black dress for a wedding?

A: It's not recommended to wear white shoes with a black dress for a wedding. White shoes can be too stark of a contrast with a black dress and can draw too much attention away from the dress itself.

Q: Can I wear patterned shoes with a black dress for a wedding?

A: It's best to avoid patterned shoes with a black dress for a wedding. The pattern can clash with the simplicity of the black dress and can be too distracting.

Q: Can I wear open-toed shoes with a black dress for a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear open-toed shoes with a black dress for a wedding, as long as they match the formality of the event. For formal weddings, closed-toe shoes are more appropriate. For semi-formal or casual weddings, you can wear open-toed shoes.

Q: Can I wear black shoes with a black dress for a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear black shoes with a black dress for a wedding, especially for formal events. Just make sure the shoes are not too overpowering and match the formality of the event.

Conclusion of What Color Shoes to Wear with Black Dress for Wedding

Choosing the right shoes to wear with a black dress for a wedding can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be stressful. By following these tips, you can select a pair of shoes that match the formality of the event, complement the color of your dress, and prioritize comfort. Whether you choose black, metallic, colorful, or neutral shoes, you can confidently step out onto the dance floor and enjoy the celebration.
