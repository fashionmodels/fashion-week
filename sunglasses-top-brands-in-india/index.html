Sunglasses Top Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Nov 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Summer is here, and it’s time to upgrade your sunglasses collection. But with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we will take a look at the top sunglasses brands in India to help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to sunglasses, there are a few pain points that consumers face. Firstly, the quality of the sunglasses is crucial, as it affects the level of protection they provide for your eyes. Secondly, the style of the sunglasses should be trendy and fashionable. Finally, the price of the sunglasses should be reasonable and within the budget of the consumer.

The target of this guide is to provide a comprehensive list of the top sunglasses brands in India, along with their features, price range, and availability. We will also discuss the latest trends in sunglasses and how to choose the right pair for your face shape and skin tone.

Sunglasses Top Brands in India: Ray-Ban

Ray-Ban is one of the most iconic sunglasses brands in the world. The brand was founded in 1936 and has since become synonymous with quality, durability, and style. Ray-Ban offers a wide range of sunglasses for men and women, including aviators, wayfarers, and round frames. The price range for Ray-Ban sunglasses in India starts from INR 4,000 and goes up to INR 15,000.

Personally, I have been using Ray-Ban sunglasses for the past three years, and they have never failed to impress me. The quality of the lenses is excellent, and the frames are sturdy and long-lasting. I would highly recommend Ray-Ban to anyone looking for a reliable and stylish pair of sunglasses.

Sunglasses Top Brands in India: Fastrack

Fastrack is a popular sunglasses brand in India, known for its trendy designs and affordable prices. The brand offers a wide range of sunglasses for both men and women, including aviators, wayfarers, and round frames. The price range for Fastrack sunglasses in India starts from INR 1,000 and goes up to INR 3,000.

I purchased a pair of Fastrack sunglasses last year and was pleasantly surprised by the quality of the product. The lenses were clear and provided ample protection from the sun, and the frames were lightweight and comfortable to wear. If you’re looking for a budget-friendly option, I would definitely recommend Fastrack sunglasses.

Sunglasses Top Brands in India: Oakley

Oakley is a premium sunglasses brand that offers high-quality products for athletes and outdoor enthusiasts. The brand is known for its innovative designs and advanced technologies, such as Prizm lenses and polarized lenses. The price range for Oakley sunglasses in India starts from INR 8,000 and goes up to INR 25,000.

If you’re someone who enjoys outdoor activities like hiking, cycling, or running, Oakley sunglasses are a great investment. The lenses are designed to enhance contrast and clarity, making it easier to see in bright sunlight. I have been using Oakley sunglasses for the past year, and they have exceeded my expectations in terms of quality and performance.

Sunglasses Top Brands in India: Vogue

Vogue is a popular sunglasses brand for women, known for its chic and stylish designs. The brand offers a wide range of sunglasses in different shapes and sizes, including cat-eye, round, and square frames. The price range for Vogue sunglasses in India starts from INR 2,000 and goes up to INR 6,000.

Last year, I gifted a pair of Vogue sunglasses to my sister, and she absolutely loved them. The frames were lightweight and comfortable, and the lenses provided ample protection from the sun. If you’re looking for a fashionable pair of sunglasses for everyday wear, Vogue is a great option.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying sunglasses?

A: When buying sunglasses, you should look for the quality of the lenses, the durability of the frames, and the level of protection they provide for your eyes. It’s also important to choose a style that suits your face shape and skin tone.

Q: Are expensive sunglasses worth the price?

A: Expensive sunglasses are often worth the price, as they offer better quality lenses, more durable frames, and advanced technologies like polarized lenses. However, it’s important to choose a pair that fits within your budget and provides the features you need.

Q: Which sunglasses brand is best for outdoor activities?

A: Oakley is a great sunglasses brand for outdoor activities, as their lenses are designed to enhance contrast and clarity, making it easier to see in bright sunlight.

Q: Are sunglasses necessary for eye protection?

A: Yes, sunglasses are necessary for eye protection, as they help to prevent damage to the eyes from UV rays and bright sunlight.

Conclusion of Sunglasses Top Brands in India

In conclusion, choosing the right pair of sunglasses can be a daunting task, but with this guide, we hope to make the process a little easier for you. Whether you’re looking for a premium brand like Oakley or a budget-friendly option like Fastrack, there’s a pair of sunglasses out there for everyone. Remember to prioritize quality, durability, and style when making your decision, and you’ll be sure to find a pair of sunglasses that you love.
