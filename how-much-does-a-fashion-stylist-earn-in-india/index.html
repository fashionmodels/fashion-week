How Much Does A Fashion Stylist Earn In India

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're interested in fashion styling and considering it as a career option, one of your primary concerns may be about the earning potential of a fashion stylist in India. It's a valid concern, as everyone wants to make a decent living doing what they love.

Many people believe that fashion styling is a glamorous profession with high earnings, but the reality is quite different. Fashion styling is a competitive field with a limited number of opportunities, and it can be challenging to earn a stable income.

According to industry experts, a fashion stylist's earning potential in India depends on various factors such as experience, skills, location, and clientèle.

In general, a fashion stylist in India can earn anywhere between INR 20,000 to INR 80,000 per month. However, this range can fluctuate depending on the factors mentioned above.

My Personal Experience

As a fashion stylist based in Mumbai, I've been working in the industry for over five years now. Initially, I struggled to find steady work and had to take up various odd jobs to make ends meet. However, as I gained experience and built a network, I started getting more clients and better-paying projects.

Now, I earn an average of INR 50,000 to INR 60,000 per month, which is a decent income considering the cost of living in Mumbai. However, it's not easy to maintain this income level, as the competition is fierce, and clients' demands are ever-changing.

Factors Affecting a Fashion Stylist's Earnings in India

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect a fashion stylist's earning potential in India. Here are some of the primary factors:

Experience

A fashion stylist's experience plays a significant role in determining their earnings. A stylist with several years of experience and a good track record can command higher fees than a novice stylist.

Skills

A fashion stylist's skills are also crucial in determining their earning potential. Stylists who excel in creating unique and trendsetting looks are in high demand and can charge more for their services.

Location

The location of a fashion stylist can also impact their earnings. Stylists based in metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore may earn higher fees than those based in smaller towns or cities.

Clientèle

A fashion stylist's clientèle can also affect their earnings. Stylists who work with high-profile clients and celebrities can earn more than those who work with everyday people.

Question and Answer

Q1. How can I increase my earning potential as a fashion stylist in India?

A. You can increase your earning potential by gaining experience, improving your skills, building a strong network, and working with high-profile clients.

Q2. Is it necessary to have a degree in fashion to become a stylist?

A. No, having a degree in fashion is not a prerequisite to becoming a fashion stylist. However, it can be beneficial in terms of gaining knowledge and credibility in the industry.

Q3. Can I work as a freelance fashion stylist in India?

A. Yes, you can work as a freelance fashion stylist in India. However, it can be challenging to find steady work and build a clientèle initially.

Q4. How much do celebrity fashion stylists earn in India?

A. Celebrity fashion stylists in India can earn anywhere between INR 1 lakh to INR 5 lakhs per project, depending on their experience, skills, and clientèle.

Conclusion

Overall, the earning potential of a fashion stylist in India can vary significantly depending on various factors. It's essential to build a strong network, gain experience, and improve your skills continually to increase your earning potential in this competitive field.
