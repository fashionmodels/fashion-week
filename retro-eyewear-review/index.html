Round Frames: A Classic Look

One of the most classic and timeless retro eyewear styles is the round frame. These frames have been popular for decades and are still a great choice today. I personally love round frames because they're versatile and suit a range of face shapes.

When it comes to choosing round frames, you'll want to consider factors such as size, color, and material. Some popular materials for round frames include acetate and metal, while popular colors include black, tortoise shell, and gold.

Cat-Eye Sunglasses: Fun and Funky

If you're looking for a retro eyewear option that's a little more playful and fun, cat-eye sunglasses are a great choice. These sunglasses have a unique shape that's reminiscent of the 1950s and 60s, and they're sure to make a statement.

When choosing cat-eye sunglasses, you'll want to consider factors such as size, color, and shape. Some popular shapes include exaggerated cat-eyes and more subtle cat-eyes, while popular colors include black, tortoise shell, and bright colors like pink and red.

The Importance of Quality

When it comes to retro eyewear, quality is key. You want to make sure that you're investing in a pair of glasses or sunglasses that are well-made and will last you for years to come. Some factors to consider when assessing the quality of retro eyewear include the materials used, the construction of the frames, and the quality of the lenses.

How to Choose the Right Retro Eyewear for You

Choosing the right retro eyewear for you can be a challenge, but there are a few key factors to consider. Firstly, think about your face shape and what styles will suit you best. Secondly, consider your personal style and what types of frames and colors you're drawn to. Lastly, think about your budget and what you're willing to invest in a quality pair of glasses or sunglasses.
