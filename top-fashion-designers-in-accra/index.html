Pistis Ghana is a luxury fashion brand based in Accra. The brand has dressed some of the most influential people in Africa, including Ghana's first lady, Rebecca Akufo-Addo. Their designs are known for their elegance, sophistication, and attention to detail.

Christie Brown is a luxury fashion brand that specializes in women's clothing. The brand is known for its use of vibrant colors, bold prints, and intricate detailing. Ophelia Crossland

Ophelia Crossland is a luxury fashion brand that creates timeless and elegant designs. The brand is known for its use of luxurious fabrics, intricate detailing, and impeccable tailoring.
