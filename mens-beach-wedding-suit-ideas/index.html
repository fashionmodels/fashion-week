Mens Beach Wedding Suit Ideas Tips For A Stylish And Comfortable Look

Written by Frank Nov 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you getting married on the beach and wondering what to wear? Choosing the right suit can be a challenge, especially when you want to look stylish and feel comfortable in the hot and humid weather. You want to make sure that your outfit is appropriate for the occasion, matches the wedding theme, and reflects your personal style. In this blog post, we will share some mens beach wedding suit ideas that will help you make a statement on your special day.

If you are like most grooms, you may have some concerns about how to dress for a beach wedding. You want to look your best, but you also want to stay cool and avoid sweating through your suit. You may also be worried about the sand, the wind, and the sun, which can all affect your comfort and style. Fortunately, there are many mens beach wedding suit ideas that can address these concerns and help you enjoy your big day without any wardrobe malfunctions or discomfort.

So, what are the best mens beach wedding suit ideas? The answer depends on several factors, such as the time of day, the location, the season, the wedding colors, and your personal taste. However, there are some general guidelines that can help you choose the right suit for your beach wedding. Let's explore them in more detail below.

When it comes to mens beach wedding suit ideas, there are several options you can consider, such as:

1. Linen Suits

Linen suits are a popular choice for beach weddings because they are lightweight, breathable, and comfortable. They also have a relaxed and casual vibe that fits the beach setting. You can opt for a classic beige or white linen suit, or you can experiment with other colors that match the wedding theme. Keep in mind that linen wrinkles easily, so you may want to choose a blend that has some polyester or silk to reduce the wrinkles.

2. Seersucker Suits

Seersucker suits are another great option for beach weddings, especially if you want to add some texture and pattern to your outfit. Seersucker is a lightweight cotton fabric that has a distinctive puckered texture and a striped or checkered pattern. It is ideal for warm and humid weather because it allows air to circulate and wicks away moisture. You can pair a seersucker suit with a cotton shirt, a bow tie, and loafers for a preppy and dapper look.

3. Cotton Suits

Cotton suits are a versatile and comfortable choice for beach weddings. They come in different weights and textures, so you can choose the one that suits your needs and preferences. For example, you can go for a light and breathable cotton poplin suit in a pastel shade, or a heavier and more structured cotton twill suit in a darker color. You can also mix and match different pieces, such as a cotton blazer with linen pants or shorts, to create a custom look.

4. Tropical Suits

If you want to embrace the beach theme and stand out from the crowd, you can opt for a tropical suit that features vibrant prints and colors. Think of floral, palm, or bird motifs in bold hues that match the ocean and the sky. You can pair a tropical suit with a white or light-colored shirt, a tie or a bow tie in a complementary color, and some suede or leather loafers. Just make sure that the print is not too overwhelming or distracting, and that it fits your body type and skin tone.

5. Shorts Suits

If you want to keep it casual and comfortable, you can consider a shorts suit that combines a blazer or a vest with tailored shorts. This is a modern and alternative mens beach wedding suit idea that can work well for a daytime or a less formal wedding. You can choose a shorts suit in a neutral or a bright color, and pair it with a linen or a cotton shirt, a pocket square, and some boat shoes or sneakers. Just make sure that the shorts are not too short or tight, and that they match the formality of the occasion.

How to Choose the Right Mens Beach Wedding Suit Ideas

Now that you know some popular mens beach wedding suit ideas, how do you choose the one that suits you best? Here are some tips:

  • Consider the weather and the season. If it's hot and humid, choose a lightweight and breathable fabric. If it's cooler or windy, choose a heavier fabric or layer your outfit.
  • Consider the wedding theme and colors. Choose a suit that matches the mood and the palette of the wedding, but also reflects your personal style.
  • Consider your body type and skin tone. Choose a suit that fits well, flatters your shape, and complements your complexion.
  • Consider the accessories and shoes. Choose the right tie, bow tie, pocket square, belt, and shoes that complement your suit and add some personality to your outfit.
  • Consider the comfort and practicality. Choose a suit that allows you to move, dance, and sit comfortably, and that can withstand the sand, the sun, and the wind.

How to Accessorize Your Mens Beach Wedding Suit Ideas

Once you have chosen your mens beach wedding suit idea, you can enhance it with some accessories that complete your look and make it more unique. Here are some ideas:

  • Wear a pocket square in a bold or a complementary color that matches your tie or bow tie.
  • Wear a boutonniere in a flower or a leaf that matches the wedding theme and colors.
  • Wear a watch or a bracelet that adds some shine and texture to your wrist.
  • Wear sunglasses or a hat that protects you from the sun and adds some flair to your face.
  • Carry a handkerchief or a fan that helps you stay cool and fresh throughout the day.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I have to wear a full suit for a beach wedding?

A: No, you can wear a suit jacket or a vest with tailored pants or shorts. You can also wear a blazer or a sport coat with chinos or khakis. Just make sure that your outfit matches the formality and the theme of the wedding.

Q: Can I wear sandals or flip flops with my beach wedding suit?

A: It depends on the dress code and the style of your suit. If you are going for a casual and relaxed look, you can wear sandals or flip flops that match your outfit. However, if you are going for a more formal or dressy look, you may want to opt for loafers, oxfords, or dress shoes.

Q: Should I wear a tie or a bow tie with my beach wedding suit?

A: It depends on your personal style and the formality of the wedding. A tie can add some elegance and formality to your outfit, while a bow tie can add some whimsy and personality. You can also skip the tie altogether and wear an open-collared shirt or a linen shirt with a mandarin collar for a more relaxed look.

Q: Can I wear a black suit to a beach wedding?

A: While black suits are a classic and versatile choice for formal occasions, they may not be the best choice for a beach wedding. Black absorbs heat and can make you feel uncomfortable in the sun. It also looks too formal and somber for a beach setting. You may want to opt for lighter colors like beige, white, gray, blue, or pastels that reflect the light and the mood of the beach.

Conclusion

Choosing the right mens beach wedding suit idea can be a fun and rewarding experience that lets you express your style and celebrate your love in a unique way. Whether you opt for a linen suit, a seersucker suit, a cotton suit, a tropical suit, or a shorts suit, make sure that it fits well, feels comfortable, and matches the occasion. Don't forget to accessorize your outfit with some unique details that reflect your personality and add some charm to your look. With the right mens beach wedding suit idea, you can make a statement and create memories that last a lifetime.
