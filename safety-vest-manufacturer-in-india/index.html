Types of Safety Vests

There are different types of safety vests, and they are designed to meet different safety requirements. Some of the most common types of safety vests include:

High-Visibility Vests

High-visibility vests are designed to make workers visible in low-light conditions. They have reflective strips that reflect light, making it easier for others to see them. These vests are commonly used in construction sites, mining, and transportation industries.

Reflective Vests

Reflective vests are similar to high-visibility vests, but they have more reflective material. They are designed to provide maximum visibility in low-light conditions and are commonly used in traffic control and emergency situations.
