What To Wear With A Black Tennis Skirt A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect outfit to pair with your black tennis skirt? Do you find yourself constantly changing your top or shoes, unsure of what looks best? You're not alone! Many women struggle with what to wear with a black tennis skirt. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

The key to styling a black tennis skirt is to keep it simple and versatile. With the right pieces, you can easily dress it up or down for any occasion.

First, let's start with the basics. A classic white or black t-shirt is a great option to pair with your black tennis skirt. It's simple, timeless, and can be dressed up or down depending on your accessories. For a more casual look, opt for a pair of sneakers or sandals. If you're dressing it up, a pair of heels or ankle boots will do the trick.

To add some color to your outfit, try a bold colored top or a patterned blouse. A bright yellow or pink top will make your black tennis skirt pop, while a floral or polka dot blouse adds some fun to your look. Just make sure to keep the rest of your outfit neutral to avoid clashing.

In summary, the key to styling a black tennis skirt is to keep it simple and versatile. Stick to classic pieces like a white or black t-shirt, and add some color or pattern with your top. With the right accessories, you can easily dress it up or down for any occasion.

The Versatile Black Tennis Skirt

As a tennis player, I've gone through my fair share of tennis skirts. But when I discovered the black tennis skirt, I knew it was a game-changer. Not only is it versatile enough to wear both on and off the court, but it's also easy to style with a variety of tops and shoes.

One of my favorite ways to style a black tennis skirt is to pair it with a denim jacket and white sneakers. It's a classic look that's perfect for running errands or grabbing lunch with friends. For a dressier occasion, I'll swap out the sneakers for a pair of heels and add a statement necklace.

What Shoes to Wear with a Black Tennis Skirt

When it comes to shoes, the options are endless. For a sporty look, sneakers are the obvious choice. White or black sneakers are a classic option, but you can also opt for a bold colored sneaker to add some fun to your outfit. If you're looking for something more dressy, heels or ankle boots are a great option. Just make sure to choose a heel height that you're comfortable in!

Accessorizing a Black Tennis Skirt

Accessories are key when it comes to styling a black tennis skirt. A simple gold necklace or hoop earrings can add some elegance to your outfit, while a statement necklace or colorful scarf can add some fun. Don't be afraid to experiment with different accessories to find what works best for you.

How to Layer with a Black Tennis Skirt

Layering is a great way to add some depth to your outfit. A denim jacket or cardigan is a great option for cooler weather. You can also layer a button-down shirt under your t-shirt for a preppy look.

Styling a Black Tennis Skirt for Work

Believe it or not, a black tennis skirt can be a great option for work too! Pair it with a blouse and blazer for a professional look. Make sure to choose a blouse that's not too revealing and a blazer that's tailored to your body. Add a pair of heels or ankle boots to complete the look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a crop top with a black tennis skirt?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a high-waisted skirt to balance out the crop top.

Q: Can I wear a black tennis skirt with knee-high boots?

A: Absolutely! It's a great option for cooler weather.

Q: Can I wear a black tennis skirt to a formal event?

A: It depends on the event, but you can definitely dress up a black tennis skirt with the right accessories and shoes.

Q: Can I wear a black tennis skirt with a graphic t-shirt?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a graphic t-shirt that's not too casual and pair it with dressier shoes.

Conclusion of What to Wear with a Black Tennis Skirt

As you can see, a black tennis skirt is a versatile piece that can be styled in many different ways. Stick to classic pieces like a white or black t-shirt, and add some color or pattern with your top. Choose shoes that fit the occasion, and don't be afraid to experiment with different accessories. With these tips, you'll be a black tennis skirt styling pro in no time!
