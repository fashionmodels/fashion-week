Question and Answer

Q: What is the price range of men's t-shirts in India?

A: The price range of men's t-shirts in India varies depending on the brand, quality, and design. You can find basic plain tees starting from INR 200, and high-end luxury brands can go up to INR 5000.

Q: Which men's t-shirts brand is suitable for athletic men?

A: Adidas and Nike are popular men's t-shirts brands in India that offer a range of athletic wear. Their t-shirts are made of moisture-wicking fabric that is ideal for workouts and sports activities.

Q: Which men's t-shirts brand offers sustainable options?

A: Many men's t-shirts brands in India are now offering sustainable options. Brands like H&M, Zara, and Levi's offer t-shirts made of organic cotton and recycled materials.

Q: Can I buy men's t-shirts online in India?

A: Yes, you can buy men's t-shirts online in India. Many e-commerce websites like Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra offer a range of men's t-shirts brands with doorstep delivery and easy returns.
