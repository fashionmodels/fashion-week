Best Online Calendar For College Students

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a college student struggling to keep track of your busy schedule? Do you find it difficult to remember all your assignments, exams, and social events? Keeping up with the demands of college life can be overwhelming, but there is a solution to your problem - an online calendar.

An online calendar can help alleviate the stress of college life by providing a centralized location for all your important dates and events. With so many options available, it can be difficult to decide which online calendar is the best fit for you.

The target of this article is to provide you with the best online calendar options for college students, and help you choose the one that will work best for your needs.

In summary, this article will cover the following topics: the importance of using an online calendar as a college student, the best online calendars for college students, how to use an online calendar effectively, and answers to frequently asked questions about online calendars.

The Target of Best Online Calendar for College Students

As a college student, I understand the importance of staying organized and keeping track of important deadlines. During my first semester, I struggled to keep up with all my assignments and extracurricular activities. I knew I needed a better way to manage my time, so I started researching online calendars.

After trying out several options, I found that Google Calendar was the best fit for my needs. It allowed me to color code my events and set reminders for important deadlines. Additionally, it synced seamlessly with my email and other Google apps.

The Best Online Calendars for College Students

1. Google Calendar - As mentioned above, Google Calendar is a popular choice among college students. It is free, easy to use, and syncs with other Google apps.

2. Trello - Trello is a project management tool that can also be used as a calendar. It allows you to create boards for different projects and assign deadlines to each task.

3. Apple Calendar - If you are an Apple user, the Apple Calendar is a great option. It syncs with all your Apple devices and allows you to set reminders and alerts.

How to Use an Online Calendar Effectively

Now that you have chosen the best online calendar for your needs, how can you use it effectively? Here are some tips:

- Color code your events - This will make it easier to identify different types of events at a glance.

- Set reminders - Don't rely on your memory to remember important deadlines. Set reminders for assignments, exams, and other events.

- Schedule time for studying - Make sure to schedule time for studying and homework in your calendar. Treat it like any other important event.

Conclusion of Best Online Calendar for College Students

An online calendar can be a game-changer for college students. It can help you stay organized and on top of your busy schedule. By choosing the best online calendar for your needs and using it effectively, you can take control of your college experience.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use an online calendar on my phone?

A: Yes, most online calendars have a mobile app that you can download.

Q: How often should I update my online calendar?

A: It is a good idea to update your calendar on a daily or weekly basis to ensure that it is accurate and up-to-date.

Q: Can I share my online calendar with others?

A: Yes, most online calendars allow you to share your calendar with others, which can be helpful for group projects or social events.

Q: Are there any downsides to using an online calendar?

A: One potential downside is that you may become too reliant on your calendar and forget to check it regularly. It is also important to make sure that your calendar is secure and that you are not sharing personal information with others.
