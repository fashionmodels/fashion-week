What is the Average Graphic Designer Salary in India per Month?

As a graphic designer, it can be challenging to find accurate information on salary expectations in India. You want to know if you're getting paid fairly, but the information can be hard to come by. In this post, we'll explore the average graphic designer salary in India per month and what factors can influence this.

The average graphic designer salary in India per month varies greatly depending on the designer's experience, location, and employer. However, there are some general trends that can be observed. According to Payscale, the average graphic designer salary in India is around INR 299,935 per year, which works out to about INR 24,995 per month.

It's important to note that this is an average and that salaries can range from as low as INR 117,000 per year to as high as INR 738,000 per year. Experience is a significant factor in salary, with entry-level designers earning around INR 188,000 per year, while those with 20+ years of experience can earn up to INR 1,500,000 per year.

Factors that Influence Graphic Designer Salary in India per Month

Several factors can influence the salary of a graphic designer in India. Here are some of the most important:

Experience

Experience is one of the most significant factors affecting a graphic designer's salary. Entry-level designers typically earn less than those with more experience, and designers with 20+ years of experience can earn significantly more.

Location

The city or region where a graphic designer works can also have a significant impact on their salary. Designers in major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to earn more than those in smaller cities or rural areas.

Personal Experience with Graphic Designer Salary in India per Month

As a graphic designer with five years of experience working in Bangalore, I've found that salaries can vary widely. When I started, I was earning around INR 15,000 per month, but as I gained more experience and moved to different companies, my salary has increased to around INR 50,000 per month. I've found that larger companies tend to pay better and offer more opportunities for growth and development.

Tips for Negotiating a Higher Graphic Designer Salary in India per Month

If you're looking to negotiate a higher salary as a graphic designer in India, here are some tips:

Research Salaries

Before entering into negotiations, research the average salary for your level of experience and location. This will give you a better idea of what you can expect and what you should ask for.

Highlight Your Skills and Experience

During negotiations, be sure to highlight your skills and experience and how they add value to the company. This can help justify a higher salary.

While the average graphic designer salary in India per month can vary widely depending on several factors, it's possible to negotiate a higher salary by highlighting your skills and experience and researching industry standards.
