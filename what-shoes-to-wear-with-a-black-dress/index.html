What Shoes To Wear With A Black Dress A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Black dresses are a wardrobe staple for many women. They're versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. However, choosing the right shoes to wear with a black dress can be a bit tricky. You don't want to go too casual or too formal, and you want the shoes to complement the dress rather than clash with it. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of selecting the perfect shoes to wear with a black dress.

When it comes to choosing shoes to wear with a black dress, the main pain point is figuring out which style of shoe will work best. There are so many options available, from flats to heels to boots, that it can be overwhelming. Additionally, you need to consider the formality of the occasion and the style of the dress itself. It's important to strike a balance between comfort, style, and appropriateness.

The answer to what shoes to wear with a black dress depends on the style of the dress and the occasion. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

Classic Pumps

Classic pumps are a great choice for a formal occasion or a dressier outfit. They elongate the legs and add a touch of elegance to any outfit. Opt for a black or nude pair to keep the focus on the dress.

Ankle Boots

Ankle boots are a versatile option that can be worn with both casual and dressy black dresses. They add a bit of edge to an outfit and are perfect for cooler weather. Choose a pair with a chunky heel for added comfort.

Strappy Sandals

Strappy sandals are a great option for a summer wedding or a dressy event. They add a touch of femininity to the outfit and keep the focus on the dress. Opt for a metallic or nude pair for added glam.

Ballet Flats

If you're going for a more casual look, ballet flats are a great option. They're comfortable and practical for running errands or attending a daytime event. Pair them with a black maxi dress for a boho look.

Wedges

Wedges are a great option for a summer wedding or a dressy event. They're comfortable and add height without the discomfort of wearing heels. Opt for a pair in a metallic or neutral tone to keep the focus on the dress.

Overall, when choosing shoes to wear with a black dress, it's important to consider the occasion, the style of the dress, and your own personal style. Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles to find the perfect combination for you.

Personal Experience with Black Dress and Shoes

When I attended a black-tie wedding, I wore a floor-length black dress with strappy silver sandals. The sandals added a touch of glam to the outfit and complemented the dress without overwhelming it. For a more casual event, I paired a black midi dress with ankle boots and a denim jacket. The boots added a bit of edge to the outfit and made it more suitable for a daytime event.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a black dress?

A: While it's not the most conventional choice, you can certainly wear sneakers with a black dress. Opt for a pair of white sneakers for a more casual look or black sneakers for a more edgy look.

Q: What type of heels should I wear with a black dress?

A: Classic pumps or strappy sandals are great options for heels to wear with a black dress. However, you can also opt for a pair of wedges or chunky heels for added comfort.

Q: Can I wear boots with a black dress?

A: Yes, ankle boots are a great option to wear with a black dress. They add a bit of edge to the outfit and are perfect for cooler weather.

Q: What color shoes should I wear with a black dress?

A: Black or nude shoes are the most classic choices to wear with a black dress. However, you can also opt for metallic or colorful shoes to add a pop of color to the outfit.

Conclusion of What Shoes to Wear with a Black Dress

Choosing the right shoes to wear with a black dress can be a bit intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. By considering the occasion, the style of the dress, and your own personal style, you can find the perfect shoes to complement your black dress. Whether you opt for classic pumps or ankle boots, make sure to choose a pair that complements the dress rather than clashing with it.
