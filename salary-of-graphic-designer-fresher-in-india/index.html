Salary Of Graphic Designer Fresher In India What You Need To Know

Written by William Aug 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a graphic designer fresher in India, looking to start your career in the industry? One of the biggest concerns you may have is the salary you can expect to receive. It can be challenging to know what a fair salary is, especially when you're just starting. In this post, we'll explore everything you need to know about the salary of graphic designer fresher in India.

The salary of a graphic designer fresher in India is one of the most critical factors to consider when starting your career. There are many pain points associated with this, including lack of clarity about salary expectations, pay inequality, and the challenge of finding a job that pays well. These are all issues that can make it challenging to know what to expect when it comes to your salary as a graphic designer fresher in India.

The average salary of a graphic designer fresher in India is around INR 2-3 lakhs per annum. However, this can vary depending on several factors, such as your location, experience, and the type of company you work for. In general, salaries are higher in metropolitan areas like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, but this can also depend on the type of industry you're working in.

In summary, the salary of graphic designer fresher in India is a crucial consideration when starting your career in the industry. While the average salary is around INR 2-3 lakhs per annum, several factors can influence the amount you can expect to earn.

Factors that Affect Salary of Graphic Designer Fresher in India

When it comes to the salary of graphic designer fresher in India, there are several factors that can influence how much you can expect to earn. Some of the most significant factors to consider include:

Your Location

As we mentioned earlier, salaries tend to be higher in metropolitan areas like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. If you're looking to earn a higher salary, it may be worth considering moving to one of these areas to start your career.

Your Experience

Your experience level can also play a significant role in the salary you can expect to earn. If you have more experience, you're likely to earn a higher salary than someone who is just starting.

Type of Company

The type of company you work for can also affect your salary. For example, working for a large corporation may pay more than working for a small design agency.

Industry

The industry you work in can also affect your salary. For example, working in the technology industry may pay more than working in the fashion industry.

My Personal Experience

As a graphic designer fresher in India, I found that my salary was around INR 2 lakhs per annum. However, I quickly realized that this was lower than what many of my peers were earning, even though we had similar qualifications and experience levels. After doing some research, I discovered that salaries can vary depending on many factors, including location and industry.

How to Negotiate a Higher Salary

If you're looking to earn a higher salary as a graphic designer fresher in India, there are several strategies you can use. Some of the most effective strategies include:

Do Your Research

Researching the average salary for your position in your industry and location can give you a good idea of what to expect. This can help you negotiate a higher salary with your employer.

Highlight Your Skills and Experience

Highlighting your skills and experience during the interview process can also help you negotiate a higher salary. Make sure to emphasize any relevant experience you have and any unique skills that set you apart from other candidates.

Be Prepared to Negotiate

Finally, be prepared to negotiate your salary. Your employer may be willing to pay you more than what they initially offer, so don't be afraid to ask for a higher salary.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary of a graphic designer fresher in India?

A: The average salary of a graphic designer fresher in India is around INR 2-3 lakhs per annum.

Q: How can I negotiate a higher salary as a graphic designer fresher in India?

A: You can negotiate a higher salary by researching the average salary for your position, highlighting your skills and experience, and being prepared to negotiate with your employer.

Q: Does location affect the salary of a graphic designer fresher in India?

A: Yes, salaries tend to be higher in metropolitan areas like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore.

Q: What other factors can affect the salary of a graphic designer fresher in India?

A: Other factors that can affect your salary include your experience level, the type of company you work for, and the industry you work in.

Conclusion of Salary of Graphic Designer Fresher in India

Starting your career as a graphic designer fresher in India can be challenging, especially when it comes to salary expectations. However, by doing your research and being prepared to negotiate, you can increase your chances of earning a higher salary. Keep in mind that several factors can influence your salary, including your location, experience level, and the type of company you work for. By considering these factors and using the strategies outlined in this post, you can increase your chances of earning a fair and competitive salary as a graphic designer fresher in India.
