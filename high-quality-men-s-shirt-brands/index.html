Upgrade Your Wardrobe with These High Quality Men's Shirt Brands

When it comes to investing in high quality men's shirts, there are a few pain points that can arise. It can be tough to find brands that offer both style and quality, and it can feel overwhelming to sift through the countless options available. However, by doing your research and investing in the right brands, you can ensure that your wardrobe is filled with shirts that not only look great, but are built to last.

So, what should you look for in high quality men's shirt brands? The answer is simple: attention to detail. The best brands use high quality materials and pay close attention to every aspect of the shirt, from the stitching to the buttons. They also offer a variety of styles and fits to ensure that every man can find the perfect shirt for his body type and personal style.

In this article, we'll explore some of the top high quality men's shirt brands on the market, and what makes each of them stand out. Whether you're looking for a classic dress shirt or a more casual option, these brands have something to offer.

1. Brooks Brothers

As one of the oldest men's clothing brands in America, Brooks Brothers has a long-standing reputation for quality and style. Their dress shirts are made with high quality materials like Egyptian cotton and feature details like mother-of-pearl buttons and reinforced seams. Plus, with a variety of fits and styles available, it's easy to find a shirt that fits your body type and personal style.

2. Bonobos

Bonobos is a newer brand on the scene, but they've quickly made a name for themselves thanks to their attention to detail and commitment to quality. Their dress shirts are made with high quality materials like Supima cotton and feature details like reinforced collars and cuffs. Plus, with a range of fits and styles available, there's a shirt for every occasion.

3. Charles Tyrwhitt

Charles Tyrwhitt is a British brand known for their classic dress shirts. Their shirts are made with high quality materials like Egyptian cotton and feature details like split yokes and removable collar stays. They offer a variety of fits and styles to ensure that every man can find the perfect shirt for his body type.

4. J.Crew

J.Crew is a popular American brand known for their preppy style. Their dress shirts are made with high quality materials like Italian cotton and feature details like reinforced collars and cuffs. They offer a variety of fits and styles to ensure that every man can find the perfect shirt for his personal style.

5. Proper Cloth

Proper Cloth is a newer brand on the scene, but they've quickly gained a following thanks to their custom shirts. Each shirt is made to order based on your specific measurements, ensuring a perfect fit every time. Plus, with a wide range of fabrics and customization options available, you can create a truly unique shirt that reflects your personal style.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for in a high quality men's dress shirt?

A: Look for shirts made with high quality materials like Egyptian cotton or Supima cotton. Pay attention to details like reinforced seams and buttons, as well as the fit of the shirt.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a high quality men's dress shirt?

A: Prices can vary depending on the brand, but expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $200 for a high quality shirt.

Q: What are some of the benefits of investing in high quality men's dress shirts?

A: High quality shirts will last longer and retain their shape better than cheaper options. They'll also look better and feel more comfortable to wear.

Q: Can I find high quality men's dress shirts at a discount?

A: Yes, many high quality brands offer sales and discounts throughout the year. You can also look for deals at outlet stores or online retailers.

Conclusion of High Quality Men's Shirt Brands

Investing in high quality men's shirts is a smart decision for any man looking to upgrade his wardrobe. By choosing brands that prioritize quality and attention to detail, you can ensure that your shirts look great and last for years to come. Whether you prefer classic dress shirts or more casual options, there's a brand out there that has the perfect shirt for you.
