Are Men's Plaid Suits in Style 2021?

When it comes to fashion, it can be hard to keep up with what is in style and what is not. This is especially true for men's suits, as trends tend to move slowly in this area. However, if you are wondering if men's plaid suits are in style for 2021, you have come to the right place.

The answer is yes, men's plaid suits are definitely in style for 2021. Plaid has been a popular pattern in men's fashion for a long time, and it is not going anywhere anytime soon. However, the plaid suits of 2021 are a bit different than those of the past.

So, what are the main points to keep in mind when it comes to men's plaid suits in 2021? First, the fit of the suit is key. A well-tailored suit that fits properly is always in style, no matter what the pattern or color. Second, it is important to choose the right type of plaid for your suit. Small, subtle patterns are great for a classic look, while bold, colorful plaid can make a statement.

Why Men's Plaid Suits are in Style for 2021

Personally, I love wearing plaid suits because they are a great way to add some personality to my wardrobe. They are versatile enough to wear to both formal and casual events, and they can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Plus, they are a great way to stand out in a sea of solid-colored suits.

In addition to being a fun and unique fashion choice, plaid suits are also in style because they are a nod to classic menswear. The pattern has been around for centuries, and it has been worn by everyone from farmers to royalty. There is something timeless and sophisticated about a well-made plaid suit, which is why they will never truly go out of style.

How to Style a Men's Plaid Suit in 2021

There are a few different ways to style a men's plaid suit in 2021. If you want to go for a classic look, stick with a subtle plaid pattern in a neutral color like gray or navy. Pair it with a crisp white dress shirt, a solid-colored tie, and some black or brown dress shoes.

If you want to make a bolder statement, go for a suit with a larger plaid pattern in a brighter color. You can pair this with a patterned dress shirt in a complementary color, or keep things simple with a solid-colored shirt and tie. When it comes to shoes, you can either stick with classic black or brown dress shoes, or go for something a bit more daring like a pair of colorful loafers.

The Best Brands for Men's Plaid Suits in 2021

If you are ready to add a plaid suit to your wardrobe, there are a few brands that are worth checking out. Some of my personal favorites include J.Crew, Bonobos, and Suitsupply. These brands offer a range of plaid patterns and styles, so you are sure to find something that suits your taste.

How to Care for Your Men's Plaid Suit

Once you have invested in a men's plaid suit, it is important to take good care of it to ensure it lasts for years to come. Always follow the care instructions on the label, and take your suit to a professional dry cleaner for any major cleaning needs. To keep it looking fresh between cleanings, use a lint roller to remove any dust or debris, and store it in a garment bag to protect it from moths and other pests.

Conclusion of Are Men's Plaid Suits in Style 2021

In conclusion, men's plaid suits are definitely in style for 2021. Whether you prefer a classic look or something more bold, there is a plaid suit out there for you. Just remember to choose the right fit and pattern, and take good care of your suit to ensure it lasts for years to come.

Question and Answer

Q: Are plaid suits only appropriate for casual occasions?

A: No, plaid suits can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Q: Can plaid suits be worn year-round?

A: Yes, plaid suits are versatile enough to be worn in any season.

Q: What types of shoes should be worn with a plaid suit?

A: Classic black or brown dress shoes are always a safe choice, but you can also get more creative with colorful loafers or other statement shoes.

Q: What accessories pair well with a plaid suit?

A: A solid-colored tie and pocket square are always a good choice, but you can also experiment with patterned ties or colorful socks.
