What Is The Long Indian Shirt Called

Written by William Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a fan of Indian fashion? Do you love the way Indian clothes look and feel, but find yourself confused by the terminology? If you're wondering what is the long Indian shirt called, you're not alone. Understanding the different types of Indian clothes can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. In this post, we'll break down what the long Indian shirt is called, and give you a better understanding of this beautiful garment.

Have you ever found yourself staring at Indian fashion pictures, wondering what to call a particular type of clothing? It can be frustrating when you can't articulate what you're looking for or when you're trying to find a specific style. The long Indian shirt is one of those garments that can be difficult to describe, but it's a staple piece in Indian fashion.

The long Indian shirt is called a kurta. A kurta is a long, loose-fitting shirt that falls below the knee. It's typically worn with leggings or loose-fitting pants, and it can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

In summary, the long Indian shirt is called a kurta. It's a beautiful and versatile garment that's a must-have in any Indian fashion lover's wardrobe.

The Versatility of the Kurta

When I first discovered the kurta, I was pleasantly surprised by how versatile it is. I purchased a simple white cotton kurta, and it quickly became one of my go-to pieces in my wardrobe. I could dress it up with jewelry and heels or dress it down with sandals and a scarf. The best part is that it's comfortable and breathable, making it perfect for hot summer days.

The History of the Kurta

The kurta has a rich history that dates back to ancient India. It was originally worn by men, but over time, it became a garment that both men and women wore. The kurta has evolved over the years, and now it comes in a variety of styles, fabrics, and colors.

How to Choose the Right Kurta

Choosing the right kurta can be daunting, especially if you're new to Indian fashion. Here are a few things to consider when choosing a kurta:

Style: Kurta comes in a variety of styles, including Anarkali, A-line, and straight-cut. Choose a style that flatters your body type and suits the occasion.

Fabric: Kurta comes in a variety of fabrics, including cotton, silk, and chiffon. Choose a fabric that's appropriate for the occasion and suits your comfort level.

Color: Kurta comes in a variety of colors, from bright and bold to muted and understated. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and suits your personal style.

How to Style a Kurta

Styling a kurta is easy and fun. Here are a few ideas:

Accessorize: Add jewelry, a scarf, or a clutch to dress up your kurta.

Layer: Pair your kurta with a jacket or cardigan for a layered look.

Shoes: Pair your kurta with sandals or heels, depending on the occasion.

Question and Answer

Q: Can men wear a kurta?

A: Yes, the kurta was originally worn by men, and it's still a popular garment for men in India.

Q: Is the kurta appropriate for formal occasions?

A: Yes, the kurta can be dressed up or down, making it appropriate for a variety of occasions.

Q: Can the kurta be worn with jeans?

A: Yes, the kurta can be paired with jeans for a more casual look.

Q: Is the kurta comfortable?

A: Yes, the kurta is a comfortable and breathable garment that's perfect for hot summer days.

Conclusion of What Is the Long Indian Shirt Called

Now that you know what the long Indian shirt is called, you can confidently shop for this beautiful garment. Remember to choose a style, fabric, and color that suits your personal style and the occasion. The kurta is a versatile and comfortable garment that's perfect for any Indian fashion lover's wardrobe.
