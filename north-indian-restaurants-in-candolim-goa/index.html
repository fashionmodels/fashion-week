Discovering The Best North Indian Restaurants In Candolim Goa

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Goa is known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and delicious food. However, finding good North Indian food in Candolim can be a challenge for many visitors. From overly spicy curries to bland dishes, the experience can be frustrating for those seeking a true taste of North India. Fortunately, there are some great North Indian restaurants in Candolim that offer authentic and flavorful dishes.

If you are looking for the best North Indian restaurants in Candolim, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will give you a rundown of the top restaurants in the area and what makes them stand out.

When it comes to North Indian cuisine, there are several key factors that we need to consider. The quality of the ingredients, the use of spices, and the cooking techniques are all important factors that contribute to the overall taste and flavor of the dish. Additionally, the ambiance and service of the restaurant can greatly impact your dining experience.

So, let's take a closer look at some of the best North Indian restaurants in Candolim.

1. Bhatti Village

Bhatti Village is a popular restaurant in Candolim that is known for its authentic North Indian cuisine. The restaurant has a cozy and welcoming ambiance that makes it perfect for a romantic dinner or a family gathering. The menu at Bhatti Village features a wide range of dishes, from classic curries to tandoori specialties. The restaurant also serves vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

The standout dish at Bhatti Village is the butter chicken. The chicken is marinated in a special blend of spices and cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. The dish is rich, flavorful, and has just the right amount of spice.

2. Spice Goa

Spice Goa is another great option for those looking for authentic North Indian cuisine in Candolim. The restaurant has a modern and elegant ambiance that makes it perfect for a night out with friends. The menu at Spice Goa features a wide range of dishes, from classic curries to biryanis and kebabs.

The standout dish at Spice Goa is the lamb rogan josh. The lamb is cooked in a spicy tomato-based sauce that is infused with aromatic spices. The dish is served with a side of fluffy basmati rice and is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

3. Pousada By The Beach

Pousada By The Beach is a hidden gem in Candolim that is known for its excellent North Indian cuisine. The restaurant has a laid-back and casual ambiance that makes it perfect for a lazy afternoon lunch or a quick dinner. The menu at Pousada By The Beach features a wide range of dishes, from classic curries to biryanis and tandoori specialties.

The standout dish at Pousada By The Beach is the chicken tikka masala. The chicken is marinated in a special blend of spices and cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. The dish is served with a side of fluffy naan bread and is perfect for dipping.

What Makes a Good North Indian Restaurant in Candolim?

When it comes to finding a good North Indian restaurant in Candolim, there are several key factors that we need to consider. Here are some of the things that make a good North Indian restaurant:

1. Authenticity - The restaurant should use authentic recipes and fresh ingredients to create flavorful dishes that are true to North Indian cuisine.

2. Quality of Ingredients - The quality of the ingredients used in the dishes can greatly impact the taste and flavor. A good North Indian restaurant should use fresh and high-quality ingredients.

3. Spice Level - North Indian cuisine is known for its use of spices. A good North Indian restaurant should be able to balance the spice level in their dishes to cater to different tastes.

How to Order at a North Indian Restaurant in Candolim?

Ordering at a North Indian restaurant in Candolim can be overwhelming, especially if you are not familiar with the cuisine. Here are some tips to help you order:

1. Start with the basics - If you are new to North Indian cuisine, start with the basics like butter chicken, chicken tikka masala, or lamb rogan josh.

2. Ask for recommendations - The waitstaff at the restaurant can recommend dishes based on your taste preferences.

3. Be mindful of the spice level - North Indian dishes can be spicy, so be sure to ask the waitstaff about the spice level before ordering.

Conclusion of North Indian Restaurants in Candolim, Goa

Overall, there are some great North Indian restaurants in Candolim that offer authentic and flavorful dishes. From Bhatti Village to Spice Goa and Pousada By The Beach, these restaurants have something for everyone. When choosing a North Indian restaurant, be sure to consider factors like authenticity, quality of ingredients, and spice level to ensure a memorable dining experience.

If you want to experience the best of North Indian cuisine in Candolim, be sure to check out these top restaurants. Happy dining!
