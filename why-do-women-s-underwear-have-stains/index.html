How to Prevent Women's Underwear from Staining?

There are different ways to prevent women's underwear from staining, and it depends on the cause of the stain. Here are some tips to prevent these stains:

1. Use panty liners: Panty liners are an excellent option for preventing stains caused by vaginal discharge or menstrual blood. They can absorb the fluid and prevent it from staining your underwear. Make sure to change them frequently.

2. Change your underwear frequently: Changing your underwear frequently can prevent stains caused by sweat or urine. Sweat can cause bacteria to grow, which can lead to infections and bad odor. Changing your underwear can help prevent these issues.

3. Use the right detergent: Using the right detergent can help remove stains from your underwear. Make sure to read the label and choose a detergent that is suitable for your underwear material.

4. Wash your underwear in cold water: Washing your underwear in cold water can help prevent stains from setting in. Hot water can cause the stains to set in, making them difficult to remove.
