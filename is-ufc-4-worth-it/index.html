Is Ufc 4 Worth It Find Out In This Comprehensive Review

Written by Bardi Oct 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a fan of mixed martial arts and wondering if UFC 4 is worth your money? Do you want to know if it lives up to the hype and if it's an improvement over its predecessor? Look no further as we dive deep into the game and answer all your burning questions.

UFC 4 is the latest installment in the popular series of fighting games developed by EA Sports. It promises to deliver an authentic experience of the UFC, with improved gameplay mechanics, realistic animations, and an impressive roster of fighters. However, with a price tag of $60, you might be hesitant to make the purchase without knowing if it's worth it.

In short, the answer is yes, UFC 4 is worth it. If you're a fan of the sport and the previous games, you'll enjoy the improvements and additions made to this version. However, if you're not a die-hard fan, the price might not be justifiable for you.

So, what makes UFC 4 worth it? Let's explore the game's features and improvements in detail.

Improved Career Mode

One of the most significant improvements in UFC 4 is the career mode. It's more in-depth and engaging than ever before, with more opportunities for customization and progression. You can create your own fighter and take them through the ranks, building their skills and reputation along the way. The mode is more realistic, with actual training camps, rivalries, and sponsorships to manage.

Realistic Gameplay

The gameplay in UFC 4 is more realistic and fluid than in previous versions. The movements and animations of the fighters are more natural, and the controls are more intuitive. The game also introduces new clinch and ground mechanics, making it more challenging and strategic.

Impressive Roster

UFC 4 boasts an impressive roster of over 200 fighters, including legends like Bruce Lee, Mike Tyson, and Bas Rutten. It also features current champions and rising stars, making it a comprehensive representation of the sport.

Multiplayer Modes

If you're a fan of multiplayer, UFC 4 has several modes to choose from. You can play with friends online or locally, or compete in ranked matches to climb the leaderboards. The game also introduces Blitz Battles, a new mode where you can fight against other players' custom fighters.

Is UFC 4 Worth It For You?

If you're a fan of MMA and the previous UFC games, UFC 4 is definitely worth it. It's a significant improvement over its predecessor, with more content, better gameplay, and improved career mode. However, if you're not a hardcore fan, you might want to wait for a sale or rent it first before making a purchase.

FAQs

Q: Can I play UFC 4 on my PC?

A: No, UFC 4 is only available for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.

Q: Are there microtransactions in UFC 4?

A: Yes, there are microtransactions in the game, but they're not necessary to enjoy the game fully.

Q: Can I create my own fighter in UFC 4?

A: Yes, you can create your own fighter in the game and take them through the career mode.

Q: Is the gameplay in UFC 4 different from the previous games?

A: Yes, the gameplay in UFC 4 is more realistic and fluid than in previous versions.

Conclusion

Overall, UFC 4 is a great game for fans of mixed martial arts and the previous UFC games. It's worth the price for the improved career mode, realistic gameplay, impressive roster, and multiplayer modes. However, if you're not a hardcore fan of the sport, you might want to wait for a sale or rent it first before making a purchase.
