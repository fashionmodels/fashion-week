Indian Traditional Clothes In Dubai A Guide To Finding The Perfect Outfit

Written by Petter Jun 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for Indian traditional clothes in Dubai but don't know where to start? Do you find it overwhelming to choose from the wide range of options available? You are not alone. Many people face the same challenge when it comes to finding the perfect Indian outfit in Dubai.

Indian traditional clothes are known for their intricate designs, vibrant colors, and rich fabrics. They are often worn during special occasions such as weddings, festivals, and religious ceremonies. However, finding the right outfit can be a daunting task, especially if you are new to the culture or don't know where to look.

If you are in Dubai, you are in luck as the city offers a wide variety of options when it comes to Indian traditional clothes. From high-end designer stores to local markets, there is something for everyone.

Here are some tips to help you find the perfect Indian outfit in Dubai:

Visit Local Markets

One of the best ways to find authentic Indian traditional clothes is by visiting local markets such as Meena Bazaar, Karama Market, and Naif Souk. These markets offer a wide range of options at affordable prices. You can find anything from sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga cholis, and more. However, be prepared to haggle as the prices are often negotiable.

Check Out High-End Designer Stores

If you are looking for something more upscale, Dubai has several high-end designer stores that offer a range of Indian traditional clothes. From Manish Malhotra to Sabyasachi, you can find designer outfits that are perfect for weddings and other special occasions.

Consider the Occasion

When choosing an Indian outfit, it's important to consider the occasion. If you are attending a wedding, you may want to opt for a more traditional saree or lehenga choli. On the other hand, if you are attending a festival, you may want to choose something more colorful and vibrant.

Accessorize Wisely

Once you have chosen your outfit, it's important to accessorize wisely. Indian traditional clothes are often paired with jewelry such as bangles, earrings, and necklaces. However, be careful not to overdo it. Choose one or two statement pieces that complement your outfit.

Personal Experience

As someone who grew up in India and now lives in Dubai, I have had the opportunity to explore the different options available when it comes to Indian traditional clothes. I have found that local markets offer the best value for money, while high-end designer stores offer more unique and intricate designs.

FAQs

Q: What is the most traditional Indian outfit?

A: The most traditional Indian outfit is the saree, which is a long piece of fabric draped around the body.

Q: What is the difference between a saree and a lehenga choli?

A: A saree is a single piece of fabric that is draped around the body, while a lehenga choli is a skirt and blouse combination.

Q: What should I wear to an Indian wedding?

A: It's recommended to wear a traditional Indian outfit such as a saree or lehenga choli to an Indian wedding.

Q: Where can I find Indian traditional clothes in Dubai?

A: You can find Indian traditional clothes in local markets such as Meena Bazaar and Karama Market, as well as high-end designer stores such as Manish Malhotra and Sabyasachi.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect Indian traditional outfit in Dubai may seem like a daunting task, but with the right tips, it can be an enjoyable experience. Whether you choose to visit local markets or high-end designer stores, it's important to consider the occasion and accessorize wisely. With these tips, you'll be sure to find the perfect outfit for any special occasion.
