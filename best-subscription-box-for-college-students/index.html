Best Subscription Box For College Students A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

College life is exciting but can also be overwhelming. With all the assignments, projects, and exams, it's important to take a break and treat yourself every once in a while. That's where subscription boxes come in handy. They offer a convenient and fun way to discover new products and indulge in self-care. In this guide, we will explore the best subscription box for college students and help you find the one that suits your needs.

Pain Points for College Students

College students are often on a tight budget and have limited time to shop for personal items. They may also feel stressed and overwhelmed with their academic workload, leading to self-neglect. Additionally, they may be living away from home and miss the comfort of receiving care packages from their loved ones. All these factors make subscription boxes an ideal solution for college students.

What is the Best Subscription Box for College Students?

The best subscription box for college students is one that offers a variety of products at an affordable price. It should also cater to their needs and preferences, whether it's beauty, wellness, snacks, or stationery. In our research, we have found that the FabFitFun box is the most suitable for college students.

Why FabFitFun is the Best Subscription Box for College Students

We tested and reviewed several subscription boxes and found that FabFitFun offers the best value for money. It includes full-size products worth over $200 for only $49.99 per season. The box features a mix of beauty, wellness, fashion, and home items, making it perfect for students who want to pamper themselves and try new things. Moreover, FabFitFun allows customization, so you can choose some of the products you receive based on your preferences.

Personal Experience with FabFitFun

I have been a FabFitFun subscriber for two years and have always looked forward to receiving my box each season. It's like getting a surprise gift from yourself! I love that the box includes a mix of practical and indulgent items, such as a cozy scarf, a face serum, and a candle. I have discovered some of my favorite products through FabFitFun, such as the Summer and Rose beach towel and the Tula skincare line. The customization feature is also a game-changer, as I can select the products I know I will use and enjoy.

Other Recommended Subscription Boxes for College Students

While we believe that FabFitFun is the best subscription box for college students, there are other options worth considering, depending on your interests and budget. Here are some of our top picks:

Birchbox

Birchbox is a beauty-focused subscription box that sends you personalized samples of skincare, makeup, and haircare products. It's affordable at only $15 per month, and you can earn points to redeem for full-size products. Birchbox is perfect for college students who want to experiment with different beauty products without breaking the bank.

Care Package Subscription

If you miss the feeling of receiving care packages from home, you can subscribe to a care package service, such as the Pijon Box. It's a monthly box that includes snacks, personal care items, and fun surprises. It's a thoughtful gift for yourself or your college friends who need a pick-me-up.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

A: Yes, most subscription boxes allow you to cancel or pause your subscription anytime without any penalty. Make sure to read the terms and conditions before subscribing.

Q: How do I choose the right subscription box for me?

A: Consider your interests, budget, and needs. Do you want to discover new beauty products, snacks, or stationery? How much are you willing to spend? Do you prefer full-size or sample-size products? Research and read reviews before subscribing.

Q: Can I give a subscription box as a gift?

A: Yes, most subscription boxes offer gift options. You can choose to send a one-time box or a subscription for a certain period. It's a thoughtful and convenient gift for your college friends or family members.

Q: Are subscription boxes eco-friendly?

A: Some subscription boxes have eco-friendly options, such as using recyclable or biodegradable packaging. Look for boxes that prioritize sustainability if it's important to you.

Conclusion of Best Subscription Box for College Students

Subscription boxes are a fun and easy way to treat yourself or your loved ones, especially during the stressful college years. The FabFitFun box stands out as the best subscription box for college students because of its value, variety, and customization. However, there are other options available depending on your interests and preferences. By choosing the right subscription box, you can discover new products and take care of yourself without breaking the bank.
