Best Stores For Men s Shorts Where To Find Your Perfect Pair

Written by Aprilia Sep 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Summer is here, and it's time to ditch those long pants and embrace the warm weather. But finding the perfect pair of shorts can be a challenge. With so many styles, materials, and colors to choose from, it's easy to get overwhelmed. The good news is, there are plenty of stores that specialize in men's shorts, and we've got the inside scoop on the best ones to check out.

If you've ever spent hours searching for the perfect pair of shorts, you know how frustrating it can be. Whether you're looking for something casual or dressy, finding the right fit and style can feel like an impossible task. But fear not, because we've done the research for you. Our guide to the best stores for men's shorts will help you find exactly what you're looking for, no matter your style or budget.

When it comes to finding the best stores for men's shorts, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your personal style and what kind of shorts you're looking for. Do you prefer classic styles like chino shorts, or do you like something more modern like jogger shorts? Next, consider your budget. Some stores specialize in high-end designer shorts, while others offer more affordable options. Finally, think about the material and quality of the shorts. You want to make sure you're investing in a pair that will last you for several summers.

In summary, finding the perfect pair of shorts can be a challenging task, but with the right stores, it doesn't have to be. Consider your personal style, budget, and the quality of the shorts before making your purchase. Now, let's dive into the best stores for men's shorts.

1. J.Crew

J.Crew is a classic American brand that offers a wide selection of men's shorts. They specialize in chino shorts, which are perfect for a dressier look. Their shorts come in a range of colors and patterns, and they offer both slim and regular fits. J.Crew also offers free shipping on orders over $50, making it a great option for those on a budget.

Personally, I love J.Crew's shorts. I have a few pairs that I wear all the time, and they've held up well over the years. They're comfortable, stylish, and versatile, making them a great investment.

2. Bonobos

Bonobos is a men's clothing brand that specializes in pants, but they also offer a great selection of shorts. Their shorts come in a range of styles, from classic chino shorts to more modern jogger shorts. Bonobos is known for their high-quality materials and attention to detail, making their shorts a great investment for those looking for something that will last.

I recently purchased a pair of jogger shorts from Bonobos, and I've been really impressed with the quality. They're comfortable, stylish, and versatile, making them a great addition to my summer wardrobe.

3. Patagonia

Patagonia is a brand known for their outdoor gear, but they also offer a great selection of shorts. Their shorts are made from high-quality, sustainable materials, and they come in a range of styles and colors. Patagonia is a great option for those looking for shorts that are both stylish and environmentally friendly.

I recently purchased a pair of Patagonia shorts for a hiking trip, and I was really impressed with the quality. They held up well on the trail, and they were comfortable and breathable in the hot weather.

4. REI

REI is another outdoor brand that offers a great selection of men's shorts. They specialize in shorts that are designed for outdoor activities, like hiking and camping. Their shorts are made from high-quality materials and are designed to be both comfortable and functional.

I recently purchased a pair of REI shorts for a camping trip, and they were perfect. They held up well in the wilderness, and they were comfortable and breathable in the hot weather.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying men's shorts?

A: When buying men's shorts, consider your personal style, budget, and the quality of the shorts. Look for shorts that are made from high-quality materials and are designed to last.

Q: What are some popular styles of men's shorts?

A: Some popular styles of men's shorts include chino shorts, cargo shorts, jogger shorts, and athletic shorts.

Q: Where can I find affordable men's shorts?

A: There are plenty of stores that offer affordable men's shorts, including J.Crew, Old Navy, and Target.

Q: What materials are best for men's shorts?

A: When it comes to materials for men's shorts, look for options that are lightweight, breathable, and durable. Some popular materials include cotton, linen, and nylon.

Conclusion of Best Stores for Men's Shorts

Whether you're looking for a classic pair of chino shorts or something more modern like jogger shorts, there are plenty of stores that specialize in men's shorts. J.Crew, Bonobos, Patagonia, and REI are just a few of the best options out there. Consider your personal style, budget, and the quality of the shorts before making your purchase, and you're sure to find the perfect pair for your summer wardrobe.
