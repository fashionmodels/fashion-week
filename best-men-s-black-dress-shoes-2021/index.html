Best Men s Black Dress Shoes 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you on the hunt for the perfect pair of black dress shoes? Look no further! In this guide, we'll explore the best men's black dress shoes of 2021 and help you find the perfect pair for any occasion.

When it comes to dress shoes, comfort and style are key. No one wants to spend the day in uncomfortable shoes, but sacrificing style for comfort isn't an option either. Finding a pair of shoes that strike the perfect balance can be a challenge, but we're here to help.

The target of this guide is to provide an overview of the best men's black dress shoes of 2021, including their features, benefits, and drawbacks. We'll also provide some tips on how to choose the perfect pair for your needs.

So, without further ado, let's dive into the world of men's black dress shoes!

The Classic Black Oxford

When it comes to black dress shoes, the classic black oxford is a timeless option that never goes out of style. This type of shoe is typically made from high-quality leather and features a closed lacing system, which provides a sleek and polished look.

Personally, I own a pair of black oxfords and they are my go-to shoes for any formal occasion. They are incredibly versatile and can be paired with a suit, dress pants, or even jeans for a more casual look.

When looking for a pair of black oxfords, make sure to choose a style with a rounded or slightly pointed toe. This will give the shoe a more modern feel while still maintaining its classic appeal.

The Stylish Loafer

If you're looking for a dress shoe that is both comfortable and stylish, the loafer is a great option. Loafers are slip-on shoes that come in a variety of styles, including penny, tassel, and horsebit. They are typically made from leather or suede and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Personally, I love wearing loafers with dress pants or chinos for a more casual look. They are also great for business casual events or weddings where a suit isn't required.

The Sleek Derby

The derby is another classic black dress shoe that features an open lacing system. This type of shoe is typically less formal than the oxford but can still be dressed up for formal occasions.

Derbys are a great option for those with wider feet as the open lacing system allows for more room in the toe box. They also tend to be more comfortable than oxfords as they don't put as much pressure on the top of the foot.

How to Choose the Perfect Pair

When choosing the perfect pair of black dress shoes, there are a few factors to consider:

  1. Comfort: Make sure to choose a pair of shoes that are comfortable to wear for extended periods of time. Look for shoes with cushioned soles and good arch support.
  2. Style: Consider the occasion you'll be wearing the shoes for and choose a style that fits the dress code. If you're looking for a shoe that can be worn in a variety of settings, a classic black oxford or derby is a great option.
  3. Quality: Look for shoes made from high-quality materials, such as leather or suede. These shoes may be more expensive, but they will last longer and look better over time.

Question and Answer

What is the most comfortable black dress shoe?

The most comfortable black dress shoe is subjective and depends on individual preferences. However, loafers and derbys tend to be more comfortable than oxfords as they have a more relaxed fit.

Can black dress shoes be worn with jeans?

Yes, black dress shoes can be worn with jeans for a more casual look. Make sure to choose a style of shoe that complements the jeans, such as a loafer or derby.

What is the difference between an oxford and a derby?

The main difference between an oxford and a derby is the lacing system. Oxfords have a closed lacing system, while derbys have an open lacing system. Oxfords are typically more formal than derbys.

What is the best brand for black dress shoes?

There are many great brands that make high-quality black dress shoes, including Allen Edmonds, Johnston & Murphy, and Florsheim. It's important to choose a brand that fits your personal style and budget.

Conclusion of Best Men's Black Dress Shoes 2021

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of black dress shoes, it's all about balance. Comfort and style are equally important, and choosing a high-quality shoe will ensure that it lasts for years to come. Whether you prefer a classic oxford or a stylish loafer, there's a black dress shoe out there for everyone.
