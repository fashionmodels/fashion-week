This is where the longline t shirt comes in.

The longline t shirt is a style of shirt that's longer than a traditional t-shirt. It typically falls below the waist and can even extend down to the mid-thigh. This style of shirt is popular for its unique and trendy look, as well as its practicality for those who want more coverage.
