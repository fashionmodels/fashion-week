Top Men's Ethnic Wear Brand Names

One of the top men's ethnic wear brand names is Manyavar. This brand is known for its high-quality ethnic wear that caters to different styles and preferences. They offer a wide range of traditional wear, including sherwanis, kurtas, and jackets. Manyavar is a great option for those who prefer a classic, elegant look.

Another great men's ethnic wear brand name is FabIndia. This brand is known for its use of traditional fabrics and prints, making it a great option for those who prefer a more rustic or traditional look. FabIndia offers a wide range of ethnic wear, including kurtas, jackets, and Nehru jackets.
