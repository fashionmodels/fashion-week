Mens Navy Blue Suit Ideas Tips And Inspiration

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you in need of some mens navy blue suit ideas for your upcoming event or job interview? Look no further! A navy blue suit is a classic and versatile choice for any occasion, but it can be challenging to style it in a unique and fashionable way. In this post, we will give you tips and inspiration on how to wear a navy blue suit with confidence and style.

Many men struggle with finding the right outfit for formal events or job interviews. It can be stressful to choose the right suit that fits well, looks fashionable, and matches the occasion's dress code. Moreover, the navy blue suit can be a bit tricky to style, as it can easily look too conservative or dull. However, with the right accessories and styling, a navy blue suit can be a statement piece that showcases your style and personality.

The target of this post is to provide you with mens navy blue suit ideas that will help you stand out and look your best. Whether you're attending a wedding, a business meeting, or a fancy dinner, we've got you covered.

In summary, this post will give you tips and inspiration on how to wear a navy blue suit with confidence and style. We will cover different ways to style a navy blue suit, suitable occasions to wear it, and accessories that complement the outfit.

Wearing a Navy Blue Suit to a Wedding

One of the most common occasions for wearing a navy blue suit is a wedding. Whether you're the groom, the best man, or a guest, a navy blue suit is a timeless and elegant choice that fits most wedding themes and dress codes.

Personally, I attended my friend's wedding last summer, and I opted for a navy blue suit with a white dress shirt, burgundy tie, and brown leather shoes. The combination of navy blue and burgundy created a stylish and sophisticated look that matched the rustic wedding theme. Additionally, I wore a floral lapel pin that added a pop of color to the outfit and made me stand out from the other guests.

If you're attending a wedding, you can style a navy blue suit in various ways, depending on the dress code and theme. For example, you can pair it with a light blue shirt and a patterned tie for a classic and formal look, or you can go for a more relaxed and stylish outfit by wearing a white shirt, a knit tie, and suede loafers.

Wearing a Navy Blue Suit to a Business Meeting

Another occasion where a navy blue suit shines is a business meeting or job interview. A navy blue suit is a professional and appropriate choice that shows you take the meeting seriously and are willing to dress to impress.

Personally, I wore a navy blue suit to a job interview last year, and I paired it with a white dress shirt, a black tie, and black leather shoes. The combination of navy blue and black created a sleek and modern look that made me feel confident and professional. Moreover, I wore a pocket square that matched the tie's pattern, which added a subtle but sophisticated touch to the outfit.

If you're attending a business meeting or job interview, you can style a navy blue suit in different ways, depending on the company's dress code and culture. For example, you can wear a light blue shirt and a striped tie for a classic and formal look, or you can go for a more contemporary and casual outfit by wearing a white shirt, a patterned tie, and brown leather shoes.

Accessories that Complement a Navy Blue Suit

Aside from the clothing pieces, accessories play a significant role in complementing a navy blue suit and making it stand out. There are various accessories that you can add to your outfit, depending on the occasion and personal preference.

Firstly, shoes are an essential accessory that can make or break the outfit. When choosing shoes to wear with a navy blue suit, you can opt for classic black leather shoes for a formal and traditional look or brown leather shoes for a more relaxed and stylish outfit. Moreover, you can choose suede or patent leather shoes, depending on the occasion's dress code and weather conditions.

Secondly, ties are a versatile accessory that can add color, pattern, and texture to a navy blue suit. When choosing a tie, you can opt for a classic silk tie in a solid color that matches the shirt or a patterned tie that complements the suit's color. Moreover, you can choose a knit tie or a bow tie for a more casual and stylish look.

Thirdly, pocket squares are a subtle but sophisticated accessory that can elevate a navy blue suit. When choosing a pocket square, you can opt for a solid color that matches the tie or a patterned one that complements the suit's color. Moreover, you can fold the pocket square in different ways, depending on your preference and the occasion's dress code.

How to Choose the Right Fit for a Navy Blue Suit

Lastly, one of the most crucial aspects of wearing a navy blue suit is choosing the right fit that flatters your body shape and makes you feel comfortable. A well-fitted suit can make you look more confident, stylish, and professional, while an ill-fitted suit can make you look sloppy and unprepared.

When choosing a navy blue suit, you should pay attention to the jacket's size, the pants' length, and the overall silhouette. The jacket should fit snugly around your chest and shoulders, with enough room to move comfortably. Moreover, the sleeves should end at your wrist bone, and the collar should fit snugly around your neck. As for the pants, they should fit comfortably around your waist and hips, with a slight break at the ankle. Additionally, the pants' length should be long enough to cover your socks but not too long to create bunching or dragging.

Conclusion of Mens Navy Blue Suit Ideas

In conclusion, a navy blue suit is a classic and versatile choice that every man should have in his wardrobe. By following the tips and inspiration in this post, you can wear a navy blue suit with confidence and style, whether you're attending a wedding, a business meeting, or a fancy dinner. Remember to choose the right fit, complement the outfit with suitable accessories, and have fun experimenting with different styles and combinations.

Question and Answer

Q. What color shirt should I wear with a navy blue suit?

A. You can wear a light blue shirt, a white shirt, a pink shirt, a lavender shirt, a patterned shirt, or any other color that complements the navy blue suit. Just make sure that the shirt fits well and matches the occasion's dress code.

Q. Can I wear a navy blue suit in the summer?

A. Yes, you can wear a navy blue suit in the summer, but you should choose lightweight and breathable fabrics, such as cotton, linen, or seersucker. Moreover, you can opt for lighter colors, such as light blue or beige, and pair the suit with a colorful tie or pocket square.

Q. What shoes should I wear with a navy blue suit?

A. You can wear classic black leather shoes, brown leather shoes, suede shoes, or patent leather shoes, depending on the occasion's dress code and your personal preference. Just make sure that the shoes match the belt's color and style.

Q. Can I wear a navy blue suit without a tie?

A. Yes, you can wear a navy blue suit without a tie, especially for more casual occasions or during the summer. However, make sure that the shirt's collar is neat and fitted, and the buttons are not too open.
