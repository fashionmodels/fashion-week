How do Old Clothes Selling Shops in Pune Work?

To sell your old clothes at a shop in Pune, simply gather the items you want to sell and bring them to the shop. The shop owner will go through your clothes and offer you a price for each item. If you accept the offer, the shop will buy the clothes from you and resell them at a higher price.

It's important to note that the shop will only buy clothes that are in good condition. Clothes with stains or holes will not be accepted. It's also a good idea to wash and iron your clothes before bringing them to the shop to ensure you get the best possible price.
