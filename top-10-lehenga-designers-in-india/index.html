Q: How do I choose the perfect lehenga designer?

A: When choosing a lehenga designer, consider factors such as their signature style, quality of craftsmanship, and pricing. You can also look at reviews and photos of their previous work to get an idea of their design aesthetic.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a designer lehenga?

A: Designer lehengas can range from INR 50,000 to INR 5,00,000 or more, depending on the designer and the intricacy of the design.

Q: How far in advance should I book a designer for my lehenga?

A: It is recommended to book your lehenga designer at least 6-8 months in advance to ensure timely delivery and avoid any last-minute rush.

Q: Can I customize my lehenga design with a designer?

A: Yes, most designers offer customizations based on your preferences and vision for your lehenga. However, keep in mind that this may come at an additional cost.
