Veshti/Dhoti

Veshti or dhoti is a garment that is worn around the waist and legs. It is a rectangular piece of cloth that is draped around the waist and legs and is held in place by tucking it in. The veshti is worn by men in South India for special occasions or religious ceremonies. It is made of cotton or silk and usually comes in white or cream color.

When I was a child, I used to wear a veshti for all traditional functions. It was uncomfortable at first, but over time, I got used to it. It is an essential part of our culture, and I feel proud to wear it.

Lungi

Lungi is a garment that is similar to the veshti but is shorter in length. It is a comfortable garment that is worn by men in South India for everyday use. It is made of cotton and comes in various colors and designs.

I remember my grandfather wearing a lungi every day. It was his go-to garment for comfort and ease of movement. It is a versatile garment that is worn by men of all ages.

Mundu

Mundu is a garment that is worn by men in Kerala, which is a state in South India. It is a rectangular piece of cloth that is draped around the waist and legs and is held in place by tucking it in. The mundu is made of cotton or silk and comes in various colors and designs.

I have seen my cousins wearing a mundu for traditional functions in Kerala. It is a beautiful garment that is unique to the state.
