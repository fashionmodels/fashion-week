Where to Find Plus Size Prom Dresses: A Guide to Stores That Cater to Every Body Type

Shopping for prom dresses can be a stressful experience, especially when you're not sure where to find options that cater to your body type. For plus size individuals, this process can be particularly daunting, with many stores only offering limited sizes or styles that don't quite fit the bill. But fear not, as there are plenty of stores that sell plus size prom dresses, and we're here to guide you through them.

If you're on the hunt for stores that sell plus size prom dresses, you're in luck! Many retailers have started to recognize the need for inclusive sizing, and as a result, there are now more options than ever before. Whether you're looking for something classic and elegant or bold and trendy, you're sure to find a dress that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

In this guide, we'll explore some of the best stores that sell plus size prom dresses, along with tips on how to choose the perfect dress for your body type, and what to look for in terms of fit and style.

Azazie

As an online retailer, Azazie is a great option for those who want a wide selection of plus size prom dresses without having to leave their house. Their dresses come in a range of sizes (up to 30W), and they offer a variety of styles and colors to choose from. One of the best things about Azazie is that they have a "try at home" program, which allows you to order up to two dresses to try on before committing to a purchase.

Torrid

Torrid is a popular plus size retailer that offers a range of clothing options, including formal dresses. Their prom dress selection is particularly impressive, with options ranging from classic ball gowns to more modern styles. Their dresses come in sizes 10-30, and they have a variety of styles to choose from, including off-the-shoulder, strapless, and long-sleeved options.

Davids Bridal

Davids Bridal is a well-known retailer that offers a wide range of formal wear, including prom dresses. Their plus size selection is particularly impressive, with dresses available in sizes up to 30W. They have a variety of styles to choose from, including classic ball gowns and more modern options. One of the best things about Davids Bridal is that they offer alterations services, so you can ensure that your dress fits perfectly.

ASOS Curve

ASOS is a popular online retailer that offers a range of clothing options, including their plus size line, ASOS Curve. While they don't have a specific prom dress section, they do offer a variety of formal dresses that would be perfect for prom. Their dresses come in sizes 14-28, and they have options ranging from classic to trendy.

JJ's House

JJ's House is another online retailer that offers a wide range of prom dresses in plus sizes. Their dresses come in sizes up to 26W, and they have a variety of styles to choose from, including ball gowns, A-line dresses, and more. One of the best things about JJ's House is that they offer custom sizing, so you can ensure that your dress fits perfectly.

Questions and Answers

Q: What should I look for in a plus size prom dress?

A: When shopping for a plus size prom dress, it's important to look for a style that flatters your body type. Consider your best features and look for a dress that accentuates them. Additionally, choose a dress that fits well and is comfortable to wear for several hours.

Q: What are some common styles of plus size prom dresses?

A: Some common styles of plus size prom dresses include ball gowns, A-line dresses, and mermaid dresses. It's important to choose a style that flatters your body type and makes you feel confident.

Q: How do I ensure that my dress fits properly?

A: When shopping for a plus size prom dress, it's important to take accurate measurements and compare them to the retailer's size chart. Additionally, consider purchasing a dress with adjustable features, such as a corset back or adjustable straps.

Q: What accessories should I pair with my plus size prom dress?

A: When choosing accessories to pair with your plus size prom dress, consider your dress's style and color. Opt for accessories that complement your dress rather than detract from it. Some common accessories include statement earrings, a clutch, and a pair of heels.

Conclusion of Stores That Sell Plus Size Prom Dresses

Shopping for a plus size prom dress doesn't have to be a stressful experience. With more and more retailers offering inclusive sizing, there are now plenty of options to choose from. By following the tips outlined in this guide and exploring stores like Azazie, Torrid, Davids Bridal, ASOS Curve, and JJ's House, you're sure to find a dress that makes you feel confident and beautiful on prom night.
