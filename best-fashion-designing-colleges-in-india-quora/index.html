The college has a diverse student body, experienced faculty, and offers a range of courses in fashion designing, styling, and management. The college also has tie-ups with several international universities, which provides students with the opportunity to gain global exposure and experience.

FAQs About Best Fashion Designing Colleges in India Quora

Q1. What is the eligibility criteria for admission to a fashion designing college in India?

A1. The eligibility criteria for admission to a fashion designing college in India varies from college to college. However, most colleges require candidates to have completed their 10+2 education from a recognized board with a minimum of 50% marks.

Q2. What are the job prospects after completing a fashion designing course?

A2. After completing a fashion designing course, you can pursue a career as a fashion designer, stylist, fashion merchandiser, fashion journalist, fashion photographer, and more.

A3. The cost of studying fashion designing in India varies depending on the college and course. However, on average, it can cost anywhere between INR 2-5 lakhs per year.
