How Much Does An Average Ufc Fighter Get Paid

Written by Eveline Jun 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you aspire to be a UFC fighter? Are you wondering how much money you can make if you become one? If yes, then you're in the right place. In this blog post, we'll answer the question of how much does an average UFC fighter get paid.

UFC fighters are professional athletes who put their lives on the line to entertain viewers worldwide. However, despite the risks and sacrifices they make, not all UFC fighters make the same amount of money. The payment varies depending on several factors, including rank, popularity, and performance.

On average, a UFC fighter makes around $138,000 per year. However, this amount varies, and some fighters make significantly more than others. The top-ranked fighters can make millions of dollars per year, while the lower-ranked ones make a lot less.

In summary, how much a UFC fighter makes depends on their rank, popularity, and performance. The higher a fighter's rank, the more money they'll make. Similarly, the more popular a fighter is, the more their pay rate will increase. Lastly, a fighter's performance in the ring also plays a significant role in their earnings.

How Much Do UFC Fighters Make Per Fight?

As mentioned earlier, UFC fighters' payment varies depending on their rank, popularity, and performance. That being said, UFC fighters get paid per fight. On average, a UFC fighter can make anywhere between $10,000 to $500,000 per fight. However, the top-ranked fighters can make millions of dollars per fight.

For example, Conor McGregor, one of the most popular UFC fighters, made $3 million for his fight against Nate Diaz. Similarly, Khabib Nurmagomedov made $6 million for his fight against Conor McGregor.

How Do UFC Fighters Get Paid?

UFC fighters get paid through a combination of guaranteed money and bonuses. The guaranteed money is the amount that fighters receive regardless of whether they win or lose the fight. Bonuses, on the other hand, are additional payments that fighters receive for their performance in the ring.

The UFC offers several types of bonuses, including Fight of the Night, Performance of the Night, and Knockout of the Night. These bonuses can range from $50,000 to $100,000.

Factors That Affect UFC Fighter's Pay

Several factors can affect how much a UFC fighter gets paid. These include:

  • Rank - Higher-ranked fighters make more money than lower-ranked ones.
  • Popularity - Popular fighters get paid more than those who are not as well-known.
  • Performance - A fighter's performance in the ring can affect their pay rate.
  • Contracts - The terms of a fighter's contract can also impact how much they get paid.

How To Become A Successful UFC Fighter

If you aspire to become a successful UFC fighter, here are some tips that can help:

  • Train regularly - Consistent training is essential to improve your skills and increase your chances of winning fights.
  • Stay focused - You need to stay focused and disciplined to succeed in the UFC.
  • Build a fan base - Being popular can help increase your pay rate, so try to build a fan base by engaging with your audience.
  • Network - Networking can help you meet influential people who can help further your career.
  • Be patient - Success in the UFC takes time, so be patient and don't give up.

Conclusion of How Much Does An Average UFC Fighter Get Paid

In conclusion, the amount of money a UFC fighter makes depends on several factors, including their rank, popularity, and performance. The higher a fighter's rank, the more money they'll make. Similarly, the more popular a fighter is, the more their pay rate will increase. Lastly, a fighter's performance in the ring also plays a significant role in their earnings.
