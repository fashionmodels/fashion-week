Famous Diamond Jewellery Designers In India A Guide To The Best

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

India is known for its rich culture and heritage, and one of the most significant aspects of this culture is jewellery. Diamonds, in particular, have always been an essential part of Indian jewellery. Whether it's weddings or festivals, diamonds hold a special place in every Indian woman's heart. However, finding the perfect diamond jewellery can be overwhelming for many. With so many options available, it's challenging to know where to begin.

If you're looking for the best diamond jewellery designers in India, you're at the right place. In this article, we'll explore the top designers and their unique styles, so you can make an informed decision and find the perfect piece for you.

When it comes to diamond jewellery, India has some of the best designers in the world. These designers are known for their exceptional craftsmanship, unique designs, and attention to detail. From classic designs to contemporary ones, these designers offer a wide range of styles to choose from.

In this article, we'll be discussing the most famous diamond jewellery designers in India and their signature styles. We'll also share tips on how to choose the right designer and what to look for when buying diamond jewellery.

Tanishq:

Tanishq is one of the most popular diamond jewellery brands in India. Known for its exceptional quality and craftsmanship, Tanishq offers a wide range of designs to choose from. Whether you're looking for classic designs or contemporary ones, you'll find it all here. What sets Tanishq apart is its attention to detail and the use of high-quality diamonds.

My personal experience with Tanishq has been nothing short of exceptional. Their staff is knowledgeable and friendly, and they take the time to understand your needs and preferences. I purchased a diamond necklace from Tanishq for my wedding, and I couldn't be happier with my purchase. The necklace was stunning, and the diamonds were of exceptional quality.

Nakshatra:

Nakshatra is another popular diamond jewellery brand in India. Known for its unique designs and craftsmanship, Nakshatra offers a wide range of styles to choose from. Their signature style is the use of multiple diamonds in their designs, which gives their jewellery a unique and stunning look.

I purchased a diamond ring from Nakshatra for my engagement, and it was breathtaking. The ring had a unique design, and the diamonds were of exceptional quality. The staff at Nakshatra was also very helpful and knowledgeable, and they helped me choose the perfect ring for my fiancé.

Conclusion:

Choosing the right diamond jewellery designer is essential to ensure that you get the best quality and craftsmanship. When choosing a designer, consider their signature style, their reputation, and their customer service. By doing so, you'll be able to choose the perfect piece of jewellery that you'll cherish for years to come.

How to Buy Diamond Jewellery:

Buying diamond jewellery can be overwhelming, especially if you're not familiar with the process. Here are some tips to help you make an informed decision:

1. Look for a reputable dealer: When buying diamond jewellery, it's essential to choose a reputable dealer. Look for dealers who have been in the business for a long time and have a good reputation.

2. Understand the four Cs: The four Cs of diamond quality are cut, clarity, carat weight, and color. Understanding these factors will help you choose the best diamond for your needs and budget.

3. Consider the setting: The setting of the diamond can impact its overall appearance. Choose a setting that complements the diamond and enhances its beauty.

4. Get a certificate: Always ask for a certificate of authenticity when buying diamond jewellery. This will ensure that you're getting a genuine diamond.

FAQs:

1. Who are the most famous diamond jewellery designers in India?
Some of the most famous diamond jewellery designers in India are Tanishq, Nakshatra, PC Jewellers, and Kalyan Jewellers.

2. What should I look for when buying diamond jewellery?
When buying diamond jewellery, you should look for the four Cs of diamond quality, understand the setting, choose a reputable dealer, and get a certificate of authenticity.

3. How do I choose the right diamond jewellery designer?
When choosing a diamond jewellery designer, consider their signature style, reputation, and customer service.

4. What is the best way to care for diamond jewellery?
To care for diamond jewellery, clean it regularly with a soft cloth and mild soap, store it in a separate box, and avoid wearing it while doing strenuous activities.

Conclusion of Famous Diamond Jewellery Designers in India:

India has some of the best diamond jewellery designers in the world, and choosing the right one can be overwhelming. When choosing a designer, consider their signature style, reputation, and customer service. By doing so, you'll be able to find the perfect piece of jewellery that you'll cherish for years to come.
