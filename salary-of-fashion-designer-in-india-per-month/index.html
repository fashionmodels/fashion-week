Salary Of Fashion Designer In India Per Month A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William May 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering pursuing a career in fashion design in India but unsure about the salary prospects? Or are you already a fashion designer in India and wondering if you are getting paid fairly? This guide will provide you with all the information you need to know about the salary of fashion designers in India per month.

The average salary of a fashion designer in India can vary greatly depending on several factors such as years of experience, level of education, type of employer, and location. Therefore, it can be challenging to determine the exact salary range for fashion designers in India.

However, according to various sources, the average salary of a fashion designer in India ranges from INR 15,000 to INR 5,00,000 per month. This broad range is due to the factors mentioned above, as well as the designer's specialization, the type of fashion industry they work in (e.g., haute couture, ready-to-wear, or mass-market), and their reputation in the industry.

To summarize, the salary of a fashion designer in India per month can vary widely, from INR 15,000 to INR 5,00,000, depending on various factors such as experience, education, specialization, industry type, reputation, and location.

Factors Affecting Salary of Fashion Designer in India per Month

One of the most significant factors affecting the salary of a fashion designer in India per month is their years of experience. Typically, entry-level designers with less than five years of experience earn around INR 15,000 to INR 25,000 per month. Whereas, experienced designers with ten or more years of experience can earn INR 1,00,000 to INR 5,00,000 per month.

Another factor affecting the salary of fashion designers in India per month is their level of education. Designers with a degree in fashion design or a related field can command higher salaries than those without a degree. Additionally, designers with advanced degrees such as a master's degree in fashion design or business can earn even higher salaries.

Specialization and Industry Type

The specialization and industry type of a fashion designer in India can also significantly influence their salary. For instance, designers specializing in haute couture or high-end luxury fashion can earn more than those specializing in ready-to-wear or mass-market fashion. Similarly, designers working for high-end designer labels or boutiques can earn more than those working for fast-fashion brands.

Location

The location of a fashion designer's workplace in India can also affect their salary. For example, designers working in metropolitan cities like Mumbai or Delhi can earn higher salaries than those working in smaller cities or towns. This is because metropolitan cities typically have a higher cost of living, and fashion designers' demand is higher in these cities.

Personal Experience: Salary of Fashion Designer in India per Month

As a fashion designer in India with five years of experience, I can attest that the salary range mentioned above is accurate. When I started my career, my salary was around INR 20,000 per month. However, as I gained more experience, my salary increased to INR 60,000 per month. Currently, I earn around INR 80,000 per month, which is a comfortable salary for me to live on in Mumbai.

How to Increase Salary as a Fashion Designer in India?

There are several ways to increase your salary as a fashion designer in India. One of the most effective ways is to gain more experience and skills in your field. This can be achieved through internships, additional courses or certifications, and networking with industry professionals.

Another way to increase your salary as a fashion designer in India is to specialize in a specific area of fashion design. As mentioned earlier, designers specializing in haute couture or high-end luxury fashion can earn more than those specializing in ready-to-wear or mass-market fashion.

FAQs about Salary of Fashion Designer in India per Month

Q: What is the minimum salary for a fashion designer in India per month?

A: The minimum salary for an entry-level fashion designer in India per month is around INR 15,000.

Q: What is the maximum salary for a fashion designer in India per month?

A: The maximum salary for an experienced fashion designer in India per month can be as high as INR 5,00,000.

Q: What is the average salary for a fashion designer in India per month?

A: The average salary for a fashion designer in India per month is around INR 50,000.

Q: How can I negotiate my salary as a fashion designer in India?

A: You can negotiate your salary as a fashion designer in India by showcasing your skills, experience, and accomplishments to your employer. Additionally, you can research the average salary range for your position and location and use it as a reference during salary negotiations.

Conclusion of Salary of Fashion Designer in India per Month

In conclusion, the salary of a fashion designer in India per month can vary widely, depending on various factors such as experience, education, specialization, industry type, reputation, and location. As a fashion designer in India, it is essential to research the average salary range for your position and location to ensure you are getting paid fairly.
