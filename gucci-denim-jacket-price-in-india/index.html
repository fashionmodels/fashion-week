How to Care for Your Gucci Denim Jacket?

To ensure your Gucci denim jacket lasts for years, it's essential to take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle, mild detergent when washing
  • Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners
  • Hang the jacket to dry instead of putting it in the dryer
