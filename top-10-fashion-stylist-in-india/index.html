Top 10 Fashion Stylists In India

Written by Eveline Jul 19, 2023 · 5 min read

India is a country with a rich cultural heritage, and fashion is an essential part of it. Fashion stylists are professionals who help individuals or brands improve their style and appearance. In India, the fashion industry is growing rapidly, and there are many top fashion stylists who have made a name for themselves in the industry.

If you are looking for a fashion stylist in India, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. With so many options available, it is challenging to know who to trust with your fashion choices. However, you can make an informed decision by doing some research and knowing what to look for in a fashion stylist.

The target of this blog post is to provide you with a list of the top 10 fashion stylists in India. Our list is based on factors such as experience, reputation, and client feedback.

Our article will provide a comprehensive guide to the top 10 fashion stylists in India and related keywords. We will give you a brief overview of each stylist, their style, their clients, and their specialties. This article will help you choose a fashion stylist who matches your style and preferences.

1. Anaita Shroff Adajania

Anaita Shroff Adajania is one of the most famous fashion stylists in India. She has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood and has styled for films like Dhoom, Cocktail, and Love Aaj Kal. She is known for her contemporary and edgy style and her ability to create a unique look for her clients. Anaita is also the fashion director of Vogue India.

2. Sanjana Batra

Sanjana Batra is a celebrity stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood. She has styled for films like Tamasha, Ae Dil Hai Mushkil, and Kalank. Sanjana's style is a mix of traditional and contemporary, and she is known for her ability to create unique looks that reflect her client's personality.

3. Mohit Rai

Mohit Rai is a celebrity stylist and the fashion director of Grazia India. He has styled for some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Sonam Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor, and Deepika Padukone. Mohit's style is a mix of contemporary and traditional, and he is known for his ability to create a unique look for each of his clients.

4. Tanya Ghavri

Tanya Ghavri is a celebrity stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood. She has styled for films like Dilwale, Baar Baar Dekho, and Veere Di Wedding. Tanya's style is elegant and sophisticated, and she is known for her ability to create a timeless look for her clients.

5. Shaleena Nathani

Shaleena Nathani is a celebrity stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Deepika Padukone and Shilpa Shetty. She is known for her contemporary and edgy style and her ability to create a unique look for her clients. Shaleena is also the fashion director of Vogue India.

6. Allia Al Rufai

Allia Al Rufai is a fashion stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Anushka Sharma and Kangana Ranaut. She is known for her contemporary and chic style and her ability to create a unique look for her clients.

7. Ami Patel

Ami Patel is a celebrity stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Priyanka Chopra and Alia Bhatt. She is known for her edgy and modern style and her ability to create a unique look for her clients.

8. Niharika Bhasin

Niharika Bhasin is a celebrity stylist who has worked on films like The Lunchbox, Band Baaja Baaraat, and Dil Dhadakne Do. She is known for her ability to create a unique look for each of her clients and her attention to detail.

9. Aastha Sharma

Aastha Sharma is a fashion stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Sonakshi Sinha and Shraddha Kapoor. She is known for her ability to create a unique look for each of her clients and her attention to detail.

10. Nitasha Gaurav

Nitasha Gaurav is a celebrity stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood, including Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor. She is known for her edgy and modern style and her ability to create a unique look for her clients.

Conclusion of Top 10 Fashion Stylists in India

Choosing a fashion stylist can be overwhelming, but with our list of the top 10 fashion stylists in India, you can make an informed decision. Each of these stylists has made a name for themselves in the industry and has worked with some of the biggest names in Bollywood. Whether you are looking for a contemporary, edgy, or traditional style, there is a fashion stylist on this list who can help you achieve your fashion goals.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the role of a fashion stylist?
A: A fashion stylist is a professional who helps individuals or brands improve their style and appearance. They can help with anything from choosing outfits for events to creating a signature look.

Q: How do I choose a fashion stylist?
A: When choosing a fashion stylist, it is essential to consider factors such as experience, reputation, and client feedback. You should also look for a stylist who matches your style and preferences.

Q: How much does a fashion stylist cost?
A: The cost of a fashion stylist can vary depending on their experience and the services they offer. Some stylists charge an hourly rate, while others charge a flat fee for their services.

Q: How do I prepare for a styling session?
A: Before a styling session, it is essential to have an idea of the look you want to achieve. You should also bring any items of clothing or accessories that you want to incorporate into your look. Be open to trying new styles and looks, and trust your stylist's expertise.
