Bata Shoes

Bata Shoes is one of the oldest and most famous shoe brands in India. The brand was founded in 1894 in Zlín, Czech Republic. Bata is known for its wide range of products, including formal shoes, casual shoes, sandals, and sports shoes. The brand is popular among consumers for its affordable pricing, quality, and durability.
