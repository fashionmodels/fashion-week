Understanding Different Types Of Men s Shirt Collars

Written by Oliver May 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to dressing up for work, events or even casual outings, men's shirts are a go-to outfit. However, not all shirts are created equal, and one of the most important features to consider when buying a shirt is the collar. The collar is the most visible part of the shirt and can make or break your look. In this article, we will explore the different types of men's shirt collars, their styles, and which occasions they are best suited for.

Choosing the right collar style can be a daunting task, especially if you are not familiar with the different types available. The wrong collar can make you look slovenly, while the right one can make you look sharp and sophisticated. Understanding the different types of collars and their styles is crucial to ensure that you buy the right shirt for the right occasion.

Classic Collar

The classic collar is the most common type of collar and is suitable for almost all occasions. It is the type of collar that you will find on most dress shirts. The collar points are spread apart and are usually long, making it perfect for people with long faces. The classic collar can be worn with a tie or without one and is perfect for formal and semi-formal events.

Button-Down Collar

The button-down collar is a more casual version of the classic collar. It has two small buttons on the points that fasten it to the shirt, keeping it in place. This type of collar is best suited for casual events and looks great with a sport coat or a blazer. It is not recommended for formal events or when wearing a tie.

Spread Collar

The spread collar is a modern version of the classic collar. The collar points are spread apart more than the classic collar, giving it a wider appearance. It is a versatile collar that can be worn with or without a tie and is perfect for both formal and casual events. The spread collar is best suited for people with narrow faces as it helps to create an illusion of a wider face.

Cutaway Collar

The cutaway collar is a more daring and stylish version of the spread collar. It has a wide spread that angles sharply down from the neck, giving it a unique look. This type of collar is best suited for people with a narrow or long face as it helps to balance out their features. The cutaway collar is best suited for formal events and looks great with a tie.

Conclusion

Choosing the right collar for your shirt can be a game-changer. It is important to understand the different types of collars available and their styles to make the right choice. The classic collar is perfect for almost all occasions, while the button-down collar is best suited for casual events. The spread collar is a modern version of the classic collar and is suitable for both formal and casual events. The cutaway collar is a more daring and stylish version of the spread collar and is perfect for formal events. Remember to choose a collar that complements your face shape and the occasion you are attending.

Classic Collar

When going for a formal event, I pair it up with a tie, and when going for a casual event, I leave it open without a tie.

Cutaway Collar

I remember the first time I wore a cutaway collar shirt, and it made me feel like a million bucks. The sharp angle of the collar gave me a unique and stylish look, and I received a lot of compliments. I wore it with a tie to a formal event, and it looked fantastic.

Spread Collar

I have a narrow face, and the spread collar helps to create an illusion of a wider face. It is a versatile collar that I can wear to both formal and casual events. I usually wear it without a tie for a casual look and with a tie for a more formal look.

Button-Down Collar

Whenever I want to go for a casual look, I go for the button-down collar. It is perfect for a day out or a casual event. I pair it up with a sport coat or a blazer to complete the look.

Question and Answer

Q. Which collar is best suited for formal events?

A. The cutaway collar is best suited for formal events.

Q. Which collar is best suited for casual events?

A. The button-down collar is best suited for casual events.

Q. Which collar is best suited for both formal and casual events?

A. The spread collar is best suited for both formal and casual events.

Q. Which collar is best suited for people with narrow faces?

A. The cutaway collar and the spread collar are best suited for people with narrow faces.

Conclusion of Types of Men's Shirt Collars

Choosing the right collar for your shirt is crucial. It can make or break your look and leave a lasting impression. The classic collar is perfect for almost all occasions, while the button-down collar is best suited for casual events. The cutaway collar is a more daring and stylish version of the spread collar and is perfect for formal events.
