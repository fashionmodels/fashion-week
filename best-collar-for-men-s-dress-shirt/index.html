Best Collar For Men s Dress Shirt A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Oct 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to dressing up for a formal event or a job interview, choosing the right dress shirt can be a daunting task. One of the most important aspects of a dress shirt is its collar, which can make or break your entire look. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the best collar for your men's dress shirt.

Have you ever struggled with finding the perfect collar for your dress shirt? Do you feel like your collar doesn't match your face shape or your tie? If so, you're not alone. Many men face this problem, but the good news is that it's easily fixable by choosing the right collar.

The target of this article is to provide you with the knowledge and tools to choose the best collar for your men's dress shirt. We will cover different types of collars, how to choose the right one for your face shape and tie, and how to properly care for your collar.

In summary, we will guide you through the process of selecting the best collar for your men's dress shirt that complements your face shape and tie, and help you maintain it for long-term use.

The Classic Point Collar

One of the most popular and versatile collar styles is the classic point collar. This collar has a straight edge and a small space between the collar points, making it suitable for any face shape and tie style. It's also easy to maintain, making it a great choice for everyday wear.

Personally, I have a round face shape, so I always go for the classic point collar. It complements my face shape and allows me to wear any tie style I want without any restrictions.

The Spread Collar

Another popular collar style is the spread collar, which has wider collar points and a larger space between them. This collar style is best suited for men with narrow or long faces, as it helps to create an illusion of width. The spread collar is also perfect for wider tie knots, such as the Windsor knot.

When I wear a spread collar, I always make sure to pair it with a wider tie knot, as it complements the collar and creates a balanced look. However, I avoid wearing a spread collar with a narrow tie knot, as it can make my face look longer than it actually is.

The Button-Down Collar

The button-down collar is a more casual collar style that has buttons on the collar points to keep them in place. This collar style is perfect for a casual or business casual dress code, and it pairs well with a blazer or a sweater.

Personally, I like to wear a button-down collar with a sweater for a more casual look. It's comfortable, stylish, and perfect for a weekend brunch or a night out with friends.

Caring for Your Collar

Proper care and maintenance of your collar are essential for its long-term use. To maintain the shape and appearance of your collar, always unbutton it before washing and ironing your dress shirt. Use a collar stay to keep the collar points in place and prevent them from curling up. Additionally, avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the collar and cause it to lose its shape.

The Conclusion of Best Collar for Men's Dress Shirt

Choosing the best collar for your men's dress shirt is an important aspect of your overall look. Whether you opt for a classic point collar, a spread collar, or a button-down collar, make sure it complements your face shape and tie style. Proper care and maintenance of your collar are also essential for its long-term use. By following these tips, you'll be able to choose the best collar for your men's dress shirt and look your best at any formal event or job interview.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a spread collar with a narrow tie knot?

A: It's not recommended to wear a spread collar with a narrow tie knot, as it can make your face look longer than it actually is. Instead, pair it with a wider tie knot, such as the Windsor knot.

Q: How do I choose the right collar for my face shape?

A: If you have a round face shape, a classic point collar is a good choice. If you have a narrow or long face, a spread collar can help to create an illusion of width. If you're not sure, try on different collar styles and see which one complements your face shape the most.

Q: Can I wear a button-down collar with a tie?

A: Yes, you can wear a tie with a button-down collar. However, make sure to choose a tie that's not too wide, as it can overpower the collar.

Q: How do I properly care for my collar?

A: To maintain the shape and appearance of your collar, always unbutton it before washing and ironing your dress shirt. Use a collar stay to keep the collar points in place and prevent them from curling up. Additionally, avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the collar and cause it to lose its shape.
