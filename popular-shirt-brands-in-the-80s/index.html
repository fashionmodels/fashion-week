Popular Shirt Brands In The 80S

Written by Daniel May 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The 80s was a decade of bold fashion statements, and one of the most iconic pieces of clothing from that era was the shirt. From bright colors to bold patterns, shirts in the 80s were all about standing out and making a statement. In this post, we'll be exploring some of the most popular shirt brands from that era and what made them so memorable.

If you were into fashion in the 80s, you knew that not all shirts were created equal. From cheap knockoffs to high-end designer brands, the market was flooded with options. And with so many choices, it could be hard to know which brands were worth investing in.

If you were looking for a shirt that was both stylish and affordable, you couldn't go wrong with brands like J.C. Penney, Sears, and Montgomery Ward. These brands offered a wide range of shirts that were perfect for everyday wear. They were made from comfortable materials, came in a variety of colors and patterns, and were priced to fit any budget.

Overall, the 80s was a decade of bold fashion statements, and shirts were no exception. Whether you were into bright colors or bold patterns, there was a shirt out there for everyone. And while some brands were more popular than others, there was no shortage of options to choose from.

IZOD

One of the most iconic shirt brands from the 80s was IZOD. The brand was known for its preppy style and was a must-have for anyone who wanted to look stylish and sophisticated. I remember wearing IZOD shirts as a teenager and feeling like I was part of the in-crowd.

IZOD shirts were made from high-quality materials and came in a range of colors and patterns. They were perfect for both casual and formal occasions and were a staple in any stylish person's wardrobe. What made IZOD shirts so memorable was their alligator logo, which became a symbol of preppy style in the 80s.

Benetton

If you were into bright colors and bold patterns, then Benetton was the brand for you. The Italian clothing company was known for its colorful and eclectic designs and was a favorite among fashion-forward individuals in the 80s.

Benetton shirts were made from high-quality materials and came in a range of colors and patterns. They were perfect for making a statement and standing out from the crowd. What made Benetton shirts so memorable was their unique designs and use of color. They were unlike anything else on the market at the time.

The Rise of Designer Brands

While brands like J.C. Penney and Sears were popular in the 80s, the decade also saw the rise of high-end designer brands. Brands like Ralph Lauren, Calvin Klein, and Tommy Hilfiger were all the rage and were seen as a status symbol among the fashion elite.

Designer brand shirts were made from high-quality materials and were known for their impeccable craftsmanship. They were often adorned with logos and other design elements that made them instantly recognizable. While they were more expensive than other brands, they were seen as an investment in style and quality.

How to Style 80s Shirts Today

While 80s fashion may seem dated to some, there are ways to incorporate it into your wardrobe today. One way to do this is to pair an 80s-style shirt with modern pieces. For example, you could wear an IZOD shirt with a pair of slim-fit jeans and sneakers for a casual yet stylish look.

Another way to incorporate 80s fashion into your wardrobe is to embrace bold colors and patterns. Don't be afraid to mix and match different prints and colors to create a unique and eye-catching look.

Question and Answer

Q: What made IZOD shirts so popular in the 80s?

A: IZOD shirts were popular in the 80s because of their preppy style and high-quality materials. They were seen as a symbol of sophistication and were a must-have for anyone who wanted to look stylish.

Q: What was unique about Benetton shirts?

A: Benetton shirts were unique because of their use of bold colors and patterns. They were unlike anything else on the market at the time and were a favorite among fashion-forward individuals.

Q: Why were designer brand shirts so popular in the 80s?

A: Designer brand shirts were popular in the 80s because they were seen as a status symbol among the fashion elite. They were made from high-quality materials and were known for their impeccable craftsmanship.

Q: How can you incorporate 80s fashion into your wardrobe today?

A: You can incorporate 80s fashion into your wardrobe today by pairing 80s-style shirts with modern pieces or by embracing bold colors and patterns.

Conclusion of Popular Shirt Brands in the 80s

The 80s was a decade of bold fashion statements, and shirts were no exception. Whether you were into preppy IZOD shirts or colorful Benetton designs, there was a shirt out there for everyone. And while some brands were more popular than others, they all played a role in shaping the iconic fashion of the 80s.
