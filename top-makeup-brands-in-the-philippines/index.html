Top Makeup Brands In The Philippines

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for the best makeup brands in the Philippines? Do you want to know which brands offer high-quality products that can help you achieve your desired look? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the top makeup brands in the Philippines that you should check out!

When it comes to makeup, everyone has different preferences and needs. Some people want long-lasting makeup that can withstand the heat and humidity, while others prefer makeup that is lightweight and easy to apply. There are also those who are looking for makeup products that are cruelty-free and vegan. Whatever your needs may be, there is a makeup brand out there that can cater to them.

Here are the top makeup brands in the Philippines:

1. Happy Skin

Happy Skin is a homegrown brand that has gained a lot of popularity in recent years. The brand offers a wide range of makeup products that are not only high-quality but also affordable. Happy Skin's products are made with skin-loving ingredients that can help improve the overall health of your skin. If you're looking for makeup that is gentle on the skin, Happy Skin is definitely worth checking out.

2. Colourette Cosmetics

Colourette Cosmetics is another local brand that has been making waves in the makeup industry. The brand is known for its wide range of lip products that come in different shades and finishes. Colourette's lip products are also long-lasting and moisturizing, making them perfect for everyday wear. Apart from lip products, Colourette also offers face and eye makeup products that are worth trying.

3. BLK Cosmetics

BLK Cosmetics is a brand that was founded by actress Anne Curtis. The brand offers a range of makeup products that are perfect for achieving a natural, no-makeup makeup look. BLK's products are also cruelty-free and vegan, making them a great choice for those who are conscious about the environment and animal welfare.

4. Sunnies Face

Sunnies Face is a brand that was created by the team behind Sunnies Studios, a popular eyewear brand in the Philippines. Sunnies Face offers a range of makeup products that are inspired by the effortless beauty of French girls. The brand's lip products are a cult favorite, with its Fluffmatte lipstick being one of the most popular products. Sunnies Face's products are also cruelty-free and paraben-free, making them a great choice for those with sensitive skin.

Conclusion of Top Makeup Brands in the Philippines

There are many great makeup brands in the Philippines, and the ones listed above are just some of the best. When choosing a makeup brand, it's important to consider your needs and preferences, as well as the ingredients used in the products. By doing so, you can find a makeup brand that can help you achieve your desired look while keeping your skin healthy and happy.

Yes, these makeup brands offer products that are not only high-quality but also affordable. You don't have to break the bank to achieve your desired look.

Yes, some of these makeup brands are cruelty-free and vegan, making them a great choice for those who are conscious about the environment and animal welfare.

These makeup brands are available in various stores and online shops in the Philippines. You can also purchase them directly from their official websites.
