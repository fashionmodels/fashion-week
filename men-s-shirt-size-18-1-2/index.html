How To Find The Perfect Fit For Men s Shirt Size 18 1 2

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you struggle with finding the right size for your dress shirts? Are you tired of constantly adjusting your shirt throughout the day? Men's shirt size 18 1/2 can be a challenge to shop for, but with the right knowledge, you can find the perfect fit for your body type.

Men's shirt size 18 1/2 is a common size for men with larger neck sizes. However, finding the right fit can be a challenge due to variations in body type and fit preferences. It's important to find a shirt that has the right combination of neck size, sleeve length, and body fit to ensure a comfortable and stylish look.

When shopping for men's shirt size 18 1/2, it's important to consider the following factors:

Neck size

The neck size is the most important factor to consider when shopping for a dress shirt. A shirt with a neck size that is too small will be uncomfortable and restrict your movement, while a shirt with a neck size that is too large will look baggy and unprofessional. It's important to measure your neck size accurately to ensure the perfect fit.

When measuring your neck size, place the tape measure around the base of your neck where the collar would sit. Add 1/2 inch to the measurement to ensure a comfortable fit. This will be your neck size for dress shirts.

Sleeve length

The sleeve length is the second most important factor to consider when shopping for a dress shirt. A shirt with sleeves that are too short will look unprofessional, while sleeves that are too long will look sloppy. It's important to find the right sleeve length to ensure a polished look.

To measure your sleeve length, place your arm at a 90-degree angle and measure from the center of your back to your wrist. Add 1/2 inch to the measurement to ensure a comfortable fit. This will be your sleeve length for dress shirts.

Body fit

The body fit of a dress shirt is also important to consider when shopping for men's shirt size 18 1/2. A shirt that is too tight will restrict your movement and look unprofessional, while a shirt that is too loose will look baggy and sloppy. It's important to find the right body fit to ensure a comfortable and stylish look.

There are three main types of body fit for dress shirts:

Regular fit

A regular fit dress shirt is a classic fit that is comfortable and allows for ease of movement. This fit is ideal for men with larger body types or those who prefer a looser fit.

Slim fit

A slim fit dress shirt is a modern fit that is more fitted to the body. This fit is ideal for men with slimmer body types or those who prefer a more tailored look.

Extra slim fit

An extra slim fit dress shirt is the most fitted option and is ideal for men with very slim body types or those who prefer a very tailored look.

Question and Answer for Men's Shirt Size 18 1/2

Q: Can I wear a regular fit dress shirt if I have a slim body type?

A: Yes, you can wear a regular fit dress shirt if you have a slim body type, but it may not provide the tailored look that you prefer. Consider trying a slim fit or extra slim fit dress shirt for a more fitted look.

Q: How do I know if a dress shirt fits me properly?

A: A dress shirt should fit comfortably around the neck, with enough room to insert two fingers between the collar and your neck. The sleeves should extend to your wrists, with enough room to move your arms comfortably. The body of the shirt should fit comfortably around your torso, without excess fabric or tightness.

Q: Can I alter a dress shirt to fit me better?

A: Yes, a dress shirt can be altered by a tailor to provide a better fit. Consider taking your dress shirt to a tailor if you are having trouble finding the perfect fit off the rack.

Q: How should I care for my dress shirts?

A: Dress shirts should be washed in cold water and hung to dry to prevent shrinking and damage. Iron or steam your dress shirts to remove wrinkles and maintain a polished look.

Conclusion of Men's Shirt Size 18 1/2

Finding the perfect fit for men's shirt size 18 1/2 can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge, you can ensure a comfortable and stylish look. Consider measuring your neck size and sleeve length accurately, and choosing a body fit that is right for your body type and preferences. Don't be afraid to try different styles and brands to find the perfect fit for you.
