Who is the most dangerous boxer in the world 2021? The title of the most dangerous boxer in the world is a coveted one and is constantly changing. Canelo Alvarez is known for his exceptional skills and techniques, which have helped him achieve great success in the ring. He has a powerful punch, excellent footwork, and a strong defense. The Training Regime of Canelo Alvarez

One of the reasons for Alvarez's success is his rigorous training regime. He trains for hours every day, focusing on building strength, agility, and endurance. Alvarez also works on his technique, practicing his punches and footwork until they are perfect.
