How to Care for Your Bathing Suit

Once you've found the perfect bathing suit, it's important to take care of it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are a few tips for caring for your bathing suit:

  1. Rinse your bathing suit after each use to remove chlorine and saltwater.
  2. Hand wash your bathing suit with mild soap and cold water.
  3. Avoid using a washing machine or dryer, as this can damage the material.
  4. Avoid wringing out your bathing suit, as this can stretch out the material.
  5. Store your bathing suit flat or hang it to dry.
