New Men s Brands 2021 The Latest And Greatest In Men s Fashion

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to fashion, men often get the short end of the stick. There are fewer options for men's clothing and many brands seem to offer the same tired styles year after year. But fear not, because 2021 has brought a wave of new men's brands that are shaking up the industry and offering fresh, innovative designs for men of all ages and styles.

If you're tired of feeling like you're wearing the same thing as everyone else, or if you just want to try something new, these new men's brands are definitely worth checking out. From sustainable fabrics to bold prints and colors, there's something for everyone in this year's lineup.

So, what is the target of these new men's brands 2021? In short, they aim to offer men more options and variety when it comes to fashion. Whether you're into streetwear, classic styles, or something in between, these brands have something unique to offer.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the standout new men's brands of 2021, what makes them special, and how you can incorporate them into your wardrobe.

1. Noah Clothing

Target: Men who want stylish, sustainable clothing

When it comes to sustainable fashion, Noah Clothing is leading the charge in the men's space. The brand was founded by Brendon Babenzien, a former design director at Supreme, who wanted to create a brand that combined his love of streetwear with his passion for environmentalism.

Noah Clothing offers a range of clothing and accessories, from graphic tees to tailored suits, all made with sustainable materials and ethical production practices. The brand also donates a portion of its profits to environmental causes, so you can feel good about your purchase.

2. Casablanca

Target: Men who want bold, colorful designs

If you're looking to inject some color and fun into your wardrobe, Casablanca is the brand for you. Founded by Charaf Tajer, a former co-founder of Pigalle, Casablanca offers bold prints and vibrant colors inspired by the founder's Moroccan heritage.

The brand's signature silk shirts have already become a celebrity favorite, and are perfect for adding some personality to your wardrobe. But Casablanca also offers a range of other clothing and accessories, from tailored pants to bucket hats, all with the same eye-catching designs.

3. Homme Plissé Issey Miyake

Target: Men who want comfortable, versatile clothing

Homme Plissé Issey Miyake is the men's line from the iconic Japanese fashion house known for its innovative fabrics and designs. The brand's signature pleated clothing is not only stylish, but also incredibly comfortable and versatile.

From pleated pants to t-shirts and jackets, Homme Plissé Issey Miyake offers a range of clothing that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. And because the clothes are pleated, they're also easy to care for and travel with, making them a great addition to any man's wardrobe.

4. Madhappy

Target: Men who want comfortable, casual clothing with a positive message

Madhappy is a new men's brand that aims to spread positivity through its clothing. The brand's founders believe that clothing can have a positive impact on people's mental health, and they've designed their clothing with that in mind.

The brand's signature sweatshirts and t-shirts feature positive messages and graphics, and are made with high-quality, comfortable materials. Madhappy also offers a range of other clothing and accessories, from joggers to hats, all with the same feel-good vibe.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these new men's brands expensive?

A: Prices vary depending on the brand and item, but many of these brands are priced similarly to other high-end men's clothing brands.

Q: Are these brands available in stores or only online?

A: It depends on the brand, but many of these brands are available both online and in select stores.

Q: Are these brands only for young men?

A: No, these brands offer something for men of all ages and styles.

Q: Can these brands be worn for both casual and formal occasions?

A: Yes, many of these brands offer versatile clothing that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Conclusion of New Men's Brands 2021

If you're looking to refresh your wardrobe or try something new, these new men's brands of 2021 offer a range of options that are both stylish and unique. From sustainable fabrics to bold prints and colors, there's something for everyone in this year's lineup. So go ahead and explore these new brands – you might just find your new favorite clothing item.
