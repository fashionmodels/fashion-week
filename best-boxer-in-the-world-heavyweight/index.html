The Best Boxer In The World Heavyweight A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of boxing? Do you want to know who the best boxer in the world heavyweight is? Look no further as we have all the information you need to satisfy your curiosity.

The best boxer in the world heavyweight is the one who has proven to be the most skilled, experienced, and dominant in the division. This boxer has defeated all the top contenders and has held multiple championship titles. Currently, the best boxer in the world heavyweight is Anthony Joshua.

In this article, we will discuss the journey of Anthony Joshua to become the best boxer in the world heavyweight. We will also look at some of the other top contenders in the division and what sets them apart.

Anthony Joshua: The Best Boxer in the World Heavyweight

Anthony Joshua is a British boxer who currently holds the WBA, IBF, WBO, and IBO heavyweight titles. He has an impressive record of 23 wins, 21 of which came by knockout, and 1 loss. Joshua won a gold medal at the 2012 Olympics and turned professional in 2013.

Joshua's rise to the top was not an easy one. He faced tough opponents and had to overcome setbacks like his loss to Andy Ruiz Jr. in 2019. However, he showed resilience and came back stronger, defeating Ruiz in a rematch later that year.

What sets Joshua apart from other heavyweights is his combination of power, speed, and footwork. He also has a strong work ethic and trains rigorously to stay in top shape.

Other Top Contenders in the Division

While Anthony Joshua is currently the best boxer in the world heavyweight, there are other top contenders who could potentially dethrone him. These include:

Tyson Fury

Tyson Fury is a British boxer who currently holds the WBC heavyweight title. He has an impressive record of 30 wins, 21 of which came by knockout, and 1 draw. Fury is known for his unorthodox style and his ability to outbox his opponents.

Deontay Wilder

Deontay Wilder is an American boxer who held the WBC heavyweight title from 2015 to 2020. He has a record of 42 wins, 41 of which came by knockout, and 1 loss. Wilder is known for his devastating punching power and has one of the highest knockout percentages in heavyweight boxing history.

While these boxers are formidable opponents, Anthony Joshua remains the best boxer in the world heavyweight for now.

How to Watch the Best Boxer in the World Heavyweight

If you want to watch Anthony Joshua defend his titles or see other top contenders in action, there are several ways to do so. You can watch live boxing matches on platforms like DAZN, ESPN+, or Showtime. You can also attend live events at venues like Madison Square Garden or Wembley Stadium.

Question and Answer

A: Anthony Joshua is currently the best boxer in the world heavyweight.

Q: What sets Anthony Joshua apart from other heavyweights?

Q: Who are some other top contenders in the heavyweight division?

Conclusion of Best Boxer in the World Heavyweight

Anthony Joshua is currently the best boxer in the world heavyweight, but there are other top contenders who could potentially dethrone him. No matter who your favorite boxer is, the heavyweight division is always exciting to watch.
