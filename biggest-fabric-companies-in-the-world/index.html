The textile industry is a massive industry with numerous players. However, some companies stand out for their quality fabrics, innovative designs, and vast distribution networks. Here are some of the biggest fabric companies in the world that you should know:

China Textile is one of the largest textile companies in the world, with a market capitalization of about $4.4 billion. The company produces a wide range of fabrics, including cotton, wool, and synthetic fabrics. They also provide auxiliary materials such as dyes and chemicals used in textile production.

2. Luthai Textile

Luthai Textile is another leading textile company in the world, with a market capitalization of about $6.9 billion. The company specializes in producing high-quality cotton fabrics that are used in various fashion designs. They also have a vast distribution network that spans across different countries.

3. Vardhman Textiles

Vardhman Textiles is a leading textile company in India, with a market capitalization of about $1.1 billion. The company produces a wide range of fabrics, including cotton, wool, and synthetic fabrics. They also have subsidiaries that produce yarns, fabrics, and sewing threads.

4. Raymond Ltd

Raymond Ltd is a leading textile company in India, with a market capitalization of about $311 million. The company produces high-quality fabrics, including wool, silk, and cotton fabrics.
