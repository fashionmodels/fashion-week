Where To Shop For Vintage Clothes Exploring Brands That Sell Vintage Clothes

Written by Daniel Nov 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the monotonous fashion trends that come and go each season? Do you crave a unique and timeless style that sets you apart from the crowd? If so, vintage clothing may be the perfect solution for you. However, finding high-quality vintage pieces can be a daunting task. With so many brands that sell vintage clothes out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best brands that sell vintage clothes and share tips on how to find the perfect vintage pieces for your style.

Shopping for vintage clothes can be frustrating, especially if you don’t know what to look for. Vintage clothes tend to be one-of-a-kind, which means you won’t find a wide selection of sizes or colors. Additionally, vintage pieces may require alterations or repairs, which can add to the overall cost. However, the benefits of vintage clothing outweigh these challenges. Not only are vintage pieces unique and stylish, but they also have a lower impact on the environment than fast fashion.

If you’re new to vintage shopping, it’s important to do your research. Start by identifying your personal style and the era of clothing you’re interested in. Then, research brands that sell vintage clothes that specialize in that era or style. It’s also a good idea to familiarize yourself with common vintage terms, such as “deadstock” (unused vintage clothing) and “vintage-inspired” (new clothing designed to look vintage).

Overall, shopping for vintage clothes requires patience, persistence, and a willingness to dig through racks of clothing. However, the rewards are worth it. Vintage clothing adds character and charm to any wardrobe, and it’s a sustainable way to stay stylish.

1. Beyond Retro

One of the most popular brands that sell vintage clothes is Beyond Retro. Founded in 2002, Beyond Retro has stores in the UK, Sweden, and the USA. Their stock ranges from 1920s flapper dresses to 1990s grunge, with everything in between. Beyond Retro is known for its high-quality vintage pieces, and they often collaborate with designers to create unique collections.

Personally, I’ve had great luck finding unique vintage pieces at Beyond Retro. Their stores are well-organized and easy to navigate, which makes the shopping experience enjoyable. Additionally, their prices are reasonable, and they often have sales or promotions.

2. The RealReal

If you’re looking for luxury vintage pieces, The RealReal is the place to go. Founded in 2011, The RealReal is an online consignment store that specializes in high-end fashion. They have an extensive collection of vintage designer clothing, shoes, and accessories, including brands like Chanel, Hermes, and Gucci.

I’ve personally never shopped at The RealReal, but I’ve heard great things about their selection and customer service. However, it’s important to note that their prices reflect the quality of their pieces, so they may not be the best option for those on a tight budget.

3. Etsy

Etsy is a treasure trove of vintage clothing, accessories, and home goods. With over 2 million sellers, it can be overwhelming to navigate, but the search filters make it easy to narrow down your options. Many Etsy sellers specialize in specific eras, styles, or sizes, so you can find exactly what you’re looking for.

Personally, I love shopping for vintage jewelry on Etsy. It’s a great way to find unique pieces that add a pop of color to any outfit. However, it’s important to read seller reviews and check their return policy before making a purchase.

4. ASOS Marketplace

ASOS Marketplace is a platform for independent vintage sellers to showcase their collections. With over 800 boutiques, ASOS Marketplace has a wide range of vintage pieces, from 70s bohemian dresses to 90s grunge. The platform also has a section for vintage-inspired clothing, so you can get the vintage look without the hassle of hunting for authentic pieces.

I’ve never personally shopped on ASOS Marketplace, but I’ve heard positive reviews from friends who have found unique vintage pieces at affordable prices.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a vintage piece is authentic?

A: Look for tags or labels that indicate the era or designer of the piece. Additionally, examine the stitching, fabric, and hardware for signs of wear or aging. If you’re unsure, it’s best to consult with a vintage expert or do some research on the specific piece.

Q: How do I care for vintage clothing?

A: Vintage clothing requires gentle care to prevent damage or further aging. Always check the care label before washing or dry cleaning, and avoid harsh detergents or bleach. Hang or fold vintage pieces carefully to prevent stretching or creasing.

Q: How can I incorporate vintage pieces into a modern wardrobe?

A: Vintage pieces add character and charm to any outfit. Try pairing a vintage blouse with modern jeans, or a vintage skirt with a contemporary sweater. Accessories like scarves, jewelry, and bags are also a great way to incorporate vintage pieces into your everyday look.

Q: What are some other tips for shopping for vintage clothes?

A: Always try on vintage pieces before purchasing, as sizes can vary widely. Additionally, don’t be afraid to haggle on price, especially if there are flaws or damage to the piece. Finally, have fun and experiment with different styles and eras!

Conclusion of Brands that Sell Vintage Clothes

Shopping for vintage clothes can be a fun and rewarding experience. By exploring brands that sell vintage clothes and doing your research, you can find unique pieces that reflect your personal style. Whether you’re shopping at a brick-and-mortar store or online, vintage clothing is a sustainable and stylish way to express yourself.
