Navy Blue Dress Shoes Mens A Guide To Finding The Perfect Pair

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of dress shoes that will complement your navy blue suit? Look no further, as we have the ultimate guide to finding the right navy blue dress shoes for men.

It can be frustrating to spend hours searching for the right pair of shoes, only to find that they don't match your suit or they're uncomfortable to wear. That's where we come in. We understand the pain points of finding the right navy blue dress shoes for men, and we're here to help.

The target of this article is to provide you with all the information you need to know about navy blue dress shoes for men, including why they're a great choice, what to look for when purchasing a pair, and how to style them with your navy blue suit.

In summary, this article will cover the following points:

  • The benefits of navy blue dress shoes for men
  • What to look for when purchasing a pair
  • How to style navy blue dress shoes with your suit
  • Tips for maintaining and caring for your shoes

The Benefits of Navy Blue Dress Shoes for Men

As a color, navy blue is versatile and can be worn in a variety of settings, from formal to casual. Navy blue dress shoes are a great choice for men because they offer a unique alternative to traditional black dress shoes.

Not only do navy blue dress shoes add a pop of color to your wardrobe, but they also pair well with a wide range of colors, including grey, white, and beige. They're perfect for both daytime and evening events, and they're suitable for both indoor and outdoor occasions.

Personally, I've had success wearing navy blue dress shoes to weddings, job interviews, and formal events. They're a great way to stand out from the crowd while still maintaining a professional and sophisticated look.

What to Look for When Purchasing a Pair

When shopping for navy blue dress shoes for men, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Material: Look for shoes made from high-quality leather or suede. These materials will not only look better but will also last longer.
  • Comfort: Make sure the shoes fit well and are comfortable to wear. You'll be wearing them for extended periods, so it's important to choose a pair that won't cause discomfort.
  • Style: Consider the occasion you'll be wearing the shoes for and choose a style that complements your outfit. Wingtip and oxford styles are great choices for formal events, while loafers are better suited for casual occasions.
  • Price: Set a budget and stick to it. While it's tempting to splurge on a pair of expensive shoes, it's important to find a pair that fits your budget and meets your needs.

Personally, I prefer to invest in a high-quality pair of navy blue dress shoes that I can wear for years to come. It's worth spending a little extra money to ensure that the shoes are comfortable, stylish, and durable.

How to Style Navy Blue Dress Shoes with Your Suit

When it comes to styling navy blue dress shoes with your suit, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right shade: Make sure the shade of navy blue on your shoes complements the shade of your suit. If your suit is a dark navy, opt for shoes that are a shade or two lighter.
  • Pair with complementary colors: Navy blue dress shoes pair well with a range of colors, including grey, white, and beige. Avoid pairing them with other shades of blue, as this can look too matchy-matchy.
  • Accessorize: Add a pocket square or tie in a complementary color to complete the look. Just make sure not to overdo it with too many accessories.

Personally, I like to pair my navy blue dress shoes with a light grey suit and a white shirt. I add a navy blue tie and a white pocket square to complete the look. It's a classic and timeless outfit that's perfect for any formal occasion.

Tips for Maintaining and Caring for Your Shoes

To ensure that your navy blue dress shoes last as long as possible, it's important to take good care of them. Here are a few tips:

  • Polish your shoes regularly to keep them looking shiny and new.
  • Use shoe trees to help maintain their shape and prevent creasing.
  • Avoid wearing them in wet or muddy conditions, as this can damage the leather or suede.
  • Store them in a cool, dry place to prevent damage from moisture or sunlight.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your navy blue dress shoes look great for years to come.

How to Choose the Right Pair of Navy Blue Dress Shoes for Men

When it comes to choosing the right pair of navy blue dress shoes for men, it's important to consider your individual style and needs. Think about the occasions you'll be wearing them for, as well as your budget and comfort level.

Personally, I like to invest in a high-quality pair of navy blue dress shoes that I can wear for years to come. I look for shoes made from high-quality leather or suede, with a comfortable fit and a style that complements my wardrobe.

Conclusion of Navy Blue Dress Shoes Mens

Navy blue dress shoes for men are a great way to add a pop of color to your wardrobe while still maintaining a professional and sophisticated look. When shopping for a pair, consider factors like material, comfort, style, and price, and make sure to choose a pair that complements your individual style and needs. With the right care and maintenance, your navy blue dress shoes will last for years to come.

Question and Answer

Q: Are navy blue dress shoes appropriate for job interviews?

A: Yes, navy blue dress shoes are a great choice for job interviews. They're professional and sophisticated while still adding a pop of color to your outfit.

Q: Can I wear navy blue dress shoes with a black suit?

A: While it's possible to wear navy blue dress shoes with a black suit, it's not the best choice. Black shoes are a better option for black suits, as they create a more cohesive and polished look.

Q: Are navy blue dress shoes suitable for casual occasions?

A: Yes, navy blue dress shoes can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. Loafers and other casual styles are perfect for more relaxed events, while wingtip and oxford styles are better suited for formal occasions.

Q: How do I know if my navy blue dress shoes fit properly?

A: Your navy blue dress shoes should fit snugly but not be too tight. Make sure there's enough room for your toes to move around, and that your heel doesn't slip out of the shoe when you walk. If you're unsure about the fit, it's a good idea to try on different sizes or consult a shoe specialist.
