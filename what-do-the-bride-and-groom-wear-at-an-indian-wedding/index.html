What Do The Bride And Groom Wear At An Indian Wedding

Written by Oliver Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur, vibrant colors, and elaborate rituals. One of the most important aspects of an Indian wedding is the traditional attire worn by the bride and groom. In this blog post, we will discuss what the bride and groom wear at an Indian wedding.

Choosing the right attire for an Indian wedding can be overwhelming, especially for those who are not familiar with Indian culture. The bride and groom want to look their best on their special day, and the attire they choose plays a crucial role in making them feel confident and comfortable.

What Do The Bride and Groom Wear at an Indian Wedding?

The bride usually wears a traditional Indian outfit called a saree or lehenga. A saree is a long piece of fabric draped around the body, while a lehenga is a skirt paired with a blouse and a dupatta (stole). The outfit is usually heavily embellished with embroidery, sequins, and beads.

The groom, on the other hand, wears a traditional Indian outfit called a sherwani or a bandhgala. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment worn with a pair of trousers, while a bandhgala is a shorter version of the sherwani. The outfit is usually made of silk or other luxurious fabrics and is adorned with intricate embroidery.

My Personal Experience

As an Indian, I have attended many weddings, and I have always been in awe of the bride and groom's attire. I remember my sister's wedding, where she wore a beautiful red lehenga with gold embroidery, and my brother-in-law wore a cream-colored sherwani with intricate work. They looked absolutely stunning together!

Importance of Traditional Attire

Traditional attire plays a significant role in Indian weddings as it reflects the cultural heritage and values of the couple. It also adds an element of grandeur and elegance to the occasion. The bride and groom's attire is carefully chosen to complement each other and create a cohesive look.

Choosing the Right Attire

Choosing the right attire for an Indian wedding can be a daunting task. It is crucial to consider factors such as the wedding theme, venue, and personal preferences. It is also essential to choose a reputable designer who can create a custom outfit that fits perfectly and is tailored to the individual's needs.

Tips for Choosing the Right Attire

1. Research different styles and designs online or in magazines to get an idea of what you like.

2. Consider the wedding theme and venue when choosing the color and style of the outfit.

3. Choose a reputable designer who can create a custom outfit that fits perfectly and is tailored to your needs.

4. Don't forget to accessorize! Traditional Indian jewelry such as bangles, earrings, and necklaces can add an extra element of elegance to the outfit.

My Personal Experience

When I got married, I chose a traditional red lehenga with gold embroidery, and my husband wore a cream-colored sherwani with intricate work. We both felt comfortable and confident in our outfits, and we received many compliments from our guests.

Question and Answer

Q: Can the bride wear a different color besides red?

A: Yes, the bride can wear any color she chooses, but red is the traditional color for Indian weddings as it symbolizes love, prosperity, and fertility.

Q: Can the groom wear a suit instead of a sherwani?

A: While it is possible to wear a suit, it is not traditional for Indian weddings. A sherwani or a bandhgala is the most appropriate attire for the groom.

Q: Can the bride and groom wear matching outfits?

A: Yes, it is possible to wear matching outfits, but it is not common in Indian weddings.

Q: Can the bride and groom wear Western attire instead of traditional Indian attire?

A: While it is possible to wear Western attire, it is not traditional for Indian weddings. Traditional Indian attire adds an element of grandeur and elegance to the occasion.

Conclusion of What Do The Bride and Groom Wear at an Indian Wedding

The bride and groom's attire is a crucial aspect of an Indian wedding. Choosing the right attire can be overwhelming, but it is essential to consider factors such as the wedding theme, venue, and personal preferences. Traditional Indian attire reflects the cultural heritage and values of the couple and adds an element of grandeur and elegance to the occasion.
