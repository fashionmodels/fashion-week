Where To Buy Cargo Pants Womens A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter May 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for comfortable and stylish cargo pants for women but don't know where to buy them? Look no further! In this article, we'll guide you through the best places to buy cargo pants for women that are not only fashionable but also affordable.

It can be challenging to find cargo pants for women that fit well and look great. Plus, with so many stores and online shops to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to start your search. You may also struggle with finding the right size, color, or style that suits your taste and body type.

The good news is that there are plenty of options for you to choose from when it comes to buying cargo pants for women. You can find them at department stores, outdoor retailers, online shops, and even thrift stores. Here are some of the best places to buy cargo pants for women:

1. Outdoor Retailers

If you're looking for durable and functional cargo pants for women, outdoor retailers like REI, Patagonia, and Columbia are excellent options. These stores carry a wide range of cargo pants that are designed for outdoor activities like hiking, camping, and climbing. They are made with high-quality materials that can withstand harsh weather conditions and rugged terrain. Plus, they come in various sizes, colors, and styles that can fit any body type and personal preference.

2. Department Stores

Another great option for buying cargo pants for women is department stores like Macy's, Nordstrom, and Target. These stores carry a variety of cargo pants that are suitable for both casual and formal occasions. They come in different fabrics such as cotton, denim, and twill, and can be paired with different tops and shoes to create different looks. You can also find cargo pants that have unique features like pockets, zippers, and drawstrings that add a touch of style and functionality.

3. Online Shops

Online shopping has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for a good reason. It allows you to browse through a vast selection of products from the comfort of your home and compare prices and reviews easily. When it comes to buying cargo pants for women, online shops like Amazon, Zappos, and ASOS are excellent choices. They carry a vast selection of cargo pants that come in different colors, sizes, and styles from various brands. You can also filter your search based on your preferences and budget, making it easier to find the perfect pair of cargo pants for you.

4. Thrift Stores

If you're on a tight budget or looking for unique and vintage cargo pants for women, thrift stores are worth checking out. You can find a variety of pre-loved cargo pants that are still in good condition and at a fraction of the cost of new ones. Plus, shopping at thrift stores is environmentally friendly as it reduces waste and supports local communities. You never know what treasures you might find!

My Personal Experience with Buying Cargo Pants for Women

I have always been a fan of cargo pants for women as they are comfortable, practical, and stylish. However, finding the right pair of cargo pants can be challenging, especially when it comes to sizing. I have a petite frame, so most cargo pants I tried on were either too long or too loose. I also wanted cargo pants that could transition from casual to formal occasions, which made my search even more challenging.

After browsing through several stores and online shops, I finally found the perfect pair of cargo pants for women at Nordstrom. They were made with stretchy cotton fabric, had a slim fit, and came in a beautiful olive green color. They also had several pockets and zippers that added a touch of style and functionality. I paired them with a white blouse and black ankle boots for a chic and effortless look.

Tips for Buying Cargo Pants for Women

If you're still unsure about where to buy cargo pants for women, here are some tips to consider:

1. Know Your Size

Before you start shopping, make sure you know your size. Cargo pants for women come in various sizes, so it's essential to measure your waist, hips, and inseam to ensure a proper fit. You can also consult the store's size chart or customer reviews to get an idea of how the pants fit.

2. Consider Your Body Type

Cargo pants for women come in different styles that can flatter different body types. If you have a curvy figure, look for cargo pants with a high waist and a wide leg that can balance your curves. If you have a petite frame, opt for cargo pants with a slim fit and a cropped length that can elongate your legs.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About Where to Buy Cargo Pants for Women

Q: How much do cargo pants for women cost?

A: Cargo pants for women can range from $20 to $200, depending on the brand, material, and features. You can find affordable options at department stores and online shops, while high-end options are available at outdoor retailers and designer stores.

Q: Are cargo pants for women suitable for formal occasions?

A: Cargo pants for women can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion and styling. You can pair them with a blouse, blazer, and heels for a more formal look or with a t-shirt, sneakers, and a denim jacket for a more casual look.

Q: Can cargo pants for women be worn year-round?

A: Yes, cargo pants for women can be worn year-round as they come in different fabrics and styles that can adapt to different seasons. You can wear them with sandals and a tank top in the summer or with boots and a sweater in the winter.

Q: Are cargo pants for women comfortable?

A: Yes, cargo pants for women are comfortable as they are made with soft and stretchy fabrics that allow for ease of movement. They also have several pockets that can hold your essentials without weighing you down.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Cargo Pants for Women

Buying cargo pants for women doesn't have to be a daunting task. With the right knowledge and resources, you can find the perfect pair of cargo pants that fit well, look great, and suit your lifestyle. Whether you prefer outdoor retailers, department stores, online shops, or thrift stores, there's a pair of cargo pants waiting for you. Happy shopping!
