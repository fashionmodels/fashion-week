Features to Look for When Choosing a Raincoat

When choosing a raincoat, there are several features that you should look for. These include:

1. Waterproof Material - The raincoat should be made of waterproof material to keep you dry.

2. Breathable Fabric - The raincoat should be made of breathable fabric to prevent sweating.

3. Hood - The raincoat should have a hood to protect your head from rain.

4. Pockets - The raincoat should have pockets to carry essentials like a phone, wallet, or keys.

5. Adjustable Cuffs and Waistline - The raincoat should have adjustable cuffs and waistline to ensure a perfect fit.

How to Take Care of Your Raincoat?

Taking care of your raincoat is essential to ensure its longevity. Here are some tips to take care of your raincoat:

1. Follow the washing instructions mentioned on the label.

2. Do not use fabric softeners or bleach.

3. Hang the raincoat to dry instead of using a dryer.

4. Store the raincoat in a dry and cool place.
