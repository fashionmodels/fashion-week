Where To Get Vintage Clothes Online A Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of scouring thrift stores, flea markets, and vintage shops in your area for that perfect vintage piece? Do you live in an area where vintage shops are scarce? Are you looking for a more convenient way to shop for vintage clothes? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then this guide is for you.

The internet has made shopping for vintage clothes more accessible than ever before. With just a few clicks, you can browse through thousands of vintage pieces from all over the world. In this guide, we will explore the best places to buy vintage clothes online.

There are many online shops that specialize in vintage clothing. Some of the most popular include Etsy, ASOS Marketplace, Beyond Retro, and The RealReal. Etsy is a great place to find unique vintage pieces from independent sellers all over the world. ASOS Marketplace features vintage and retro clothing from independent boutiques. Beyond Retro is a UK-based vintage store that offers a wide range of vintage clothing for men and women. The RealReal is a luxury consignment store that offers high-end vintage clothing and accessories.

In summary, there are many options for buying vintage clothes online. Whether you're looking for unique pieces on Etsy, retro finds on ASOS Marketplace, or high-end luxury items on The RealReal, there's something for everyone. Shopping for vintage clothes online is a convenient way to find that perfect vintage piece without leaving your home.

Etsy: A One-Stop Shop for Vintage Clothes

As mentioned earlier, Etsy is a great place to find unique vintage pieces from independent sellers all over the world. I personally love shopping on Etsy because I can find one-of-a-kind items that I wouldn't be able to find anywhere else. Plus, the prices are often very reasonable.

One of my favorite vintage shops on Etsy is Atomic Bombshell. They specialize in vintage clothing from the 1940s to the 1980s. I recently purchased a beautiful 1960s dress from them and it arrived in perfect condition. The seller was also very responsive to my questions and provided excellent customer service.

ASOS Marketplace: Retro Finds at Your Fingertips

If you're looking for retro clothing, then ASOS Marketplace is the place to go. They have a wide selection of vintage and retro clothing from independent boutiques all over the world. I love that they have a section specifically for 90s vintage, which is my favorite era for fashion.

One of my go-to shops on ASOS Marketplace is Rokit Vintage. They have a great selection of vintage denim and band tees. I recently purchased a pair of vintage Levi's shorts from them and they fit perfectly. The shipping was also very fast, which is always a plus.

Beyond Retro: A UK-Based Vintage Store

If you're based in the UK, then Beyond Retro is a great option for buying vintage clothes online. They have a wide selection of vintage clothing for men and women, as well as a great selection of vintage accessories.

One of my favorite finds on Beyond Retro was a 1970s suede fringe jacket. It's one of my favorite pieces in my closet and I always receive compliments when I wear it. The quality of the jacket is amazing and it's held up well over the years.

The RealReal: High-End Vintage Finds

If you're looking for luxury vintage items, then The RealReal is the place to go. They specialize in high-end consignment, so you can find designer vintage clothing and accessories at a fraction of the retail price.

One of my favorite finds on The RealReal was a vintage Chanel jacket. It was in excellent condition and I got it for a fraction of the retail price. The RealReal also offers free shipping and returns, which is great if you're not sure about a purchase.

Question and Answer

1. Is it safe to buy vintage clothes online?

Yes, it is safe to buy vintage clothes online. Just make sure to read the seller's reviews and check their return policy before making a purchase.

2. How do I know if a vintage item will fit me?

Most sellers will provide measurements for their vintage items. Make sure to compare the measurements to your own to ensure a proper fit.

3. Are there any online vintage shops that specialize in plus size clothing?

Yes, there are many online vintage shops that specialize in plus size clothing. Some of the most popular include Hey Viv, Curvy Girl Vintage, and A Curated Closet.

4. Can I sell my own vintage clothes online?

Yes, you can sell your own vintage clothes online. Etsy, ASOS Marketplace, and eBay are all great options for selling vintage clothing.

Conclusion of Where to Get Vintage Clothes Online

Shopping for vintage clothes online is a great way to find unique pieces that you wouldn't be able to find anywhere else. With so many online shops to choose from, there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking for unique pieces on Etsy, retro finds on ASOS Marketplace, or high-end luxury items on The RealReal, you're sure to find that perfect vintage piece.
