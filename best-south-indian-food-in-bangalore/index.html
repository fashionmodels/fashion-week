The Ultimate Guide To The Best South Indian Food In Bangalore

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a foodie looking for the best South Indian food in Bangalore? Do you want to explore the rich flavors of this region and indulge in some mouthwatering delicacies? If yes, then you have come to the right place. Bangalore is known for its vibrant food culture, and South Indian cuisine is one of the most loved and relished cuisines here. In this blog post, we will guide you through the best South Indian food in Bangalore and where to find them.

South Indian food is a popular food choice for many people, but finding the best places to eat can be a daunting task. From breakfast to dinner, South Indian cuisine has a lot to offer, and we understand that it can be overwhelming to decide where to start. That's why we have curated a list of the best South Indian food in Bangalore that you must try.

If you're looking for the best South Indian food in Bangalore, then you must try dosa, idli, vada, sambar, and chutney. These dishes are a staple in South Indian cuisine and are loved by everyone. You can find these dishes in almost every South Indian restaurant, but we recommend trying them at famous restaurants in Bangalore to get the authentic taste.

After extensive research and personal experience, we have narrowed down the best South Indian food in Bangalore to the following list:

1. MTR

When it comes to South Indian food in Bangalore, MTR is a household name. This iconic restaurant has been serving delicious South Indian food since 1924 and is a must-visit for anyone who wants to experience the authentic flavors of South Indian cuisine. Their Masala Dosa, Rava Idli, and Filter Coffee are some of the most popular dishes that you must try.

2. Vidyarthi Bhavan

Vidyarthi Bhavan is another famous restaurant in Bangalore that is known for its crispy and delicious Masala Dosa. This iconic restaurant has been serving South Indian food since 1943 and is a must-visit for anyone who loves dosa. Their dosas are so popular that people often have to wait for hours to get a table.

3. Brahmin's Coffee Bar

If you're looking for a budget-friendly option for South Indian food in Bangalore, then Brahmin's Coffee Bar is the place for you. This small eatery is known for its crispy and tasty idlis and vadas. Their Filter Coffee is also a must-try for anyone who loves South Indian coffee.

4. CTR

CTR, also known as Shri Sagar, is a popular South Indian restaurant in Bangalore that is known for its delicious Benne Masala Dosa. This iconic restaurant has been serving South Indian food since 1920 and is a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

5. Halli Mane

If you're looking for an authentic South Indian dining experience in Bangalore, then Halli Mane is the place for you. This traditional restaurant serves delicious South Indian food on banana leaves, giving you a taste of the authentic flavors of the region. Their Bisi Bele Bath, Akki Roti, and Ragi Mudde are some of the must-try dishes.

Question and Answer Section:

Q1. What is the most famous South Indian food in Bangalore?

A1. The most famous South Indian food in Bangalore is Masala Dosa, which is a crispy and savory pancake made from rice and lentil batter and filled with a spicy potato filling.

Q2. What is the best time to eat South Indian food in Bangalore?

A2. South Indian food is available throughout the day in Bangalore, but breakfast is the most popular time to eat South Indian food. Many restaurants specialize in serving breakfast dishes like dosa, idli, and vada.

Q3. Is South Indian food in Bangalore vegetarian?

A3. Yes, South Indian food in Bangalore is mostly vegetarian. Many restaurants serve only vegetarian food, and even the non-vegetarian dishes are limited in number.

Q4. What is the price range for South Indian food in Bangalore?

A4. The price range for South Indian food in Bangalore varies depending on the restaurant and the dishes you order. Budget-friendly restaurants like Brahmin's Coffee Bar serve dishes for as low as INR 50, while high-end restaurants like MTR can cost INR 500 per person.

Conclusion of Best South Indian Food in Bangalore:

In conclusion, Bangalore is a food lover's paradise, and South Indian cuisine is a must-try for anyone who visits this city. We hope this guide helps you in finding the best South Indian food in Bangalore and satisfies your taste buds. From MTR to Vidyarthi Bhavan, these restaurants serve some of the most delicious South Indian food in Bangalore that you must try. So, what are you waiting for? Go ahead and indulge in some mouthwatering South Indian delicacies.
