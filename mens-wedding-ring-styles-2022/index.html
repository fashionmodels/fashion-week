Classic wedding rings are timeless and elegant, and will never go out of style. These rings are usually made of gold or platinum, and feature a simple design with a smooth, polished surface. Classic wedding rings are perfect for grooms who prefer a traditional and understated look.

Contemporary Wedding Rings

Contemporary wedding rings are modern and edgy, and feature unique designs and finishes. These rings are perfect for grooms who want a ring that stands out and makes a statement. Contemporary wedding rings are usually made of alternative metals such as tungsten or titanium, and feature a brushed or matte finish.

Vintage wedding rings are inspired by designs from the past, and feature intricate details and unique finishes. These rings are perfect for grooms who appreciate history and want a ring with a story.
