How To Choose The Best Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese

Written by Bardi Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a pizza lover who craves the authentic Italian flavor and texture? Do you want to know how to choose the best cheese for your homemade pizza? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of selecting the perfect authentic Italian pizza cheese that will take your pizza game to the next level.

What are the Pain Points Related to Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese?

If you are a pizza enthusiast, you know that cheese is the most important ingredient of a pizza. However, choosing the right cheese can be a daunting task. There are so many varieties and brands available in the market, each claiming to be the best. Moreover, not all cheeses are created equal when it comes to making an authentic Italian pizza. Some cheeses may not melt properly, while others may overpower the flavor of the other ingredients.

What is Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese?

Authentic Italian pizza cheese is a type of cheese that is traditionally used to make pizzas in Italy. The most popular types of cheese used in Italian pizzas are mozzarella, Parmigiano-Reggiano, Pecorino Romano, and Gorgonzola. These cheeses are made using traditional methods, and they have a distinct flavor and texture that cannot be replicated by other types of cheese.

How to Choose the Best Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese?

To choose the best authentic Italian pizza cheese, you need to consider the following factors:
  • The type of cheese: As mentioned earlier, mozzarella, Parmigiano-Reggiano, Pecorino Romano, and Gorgonzola are the most popular types of cheese used in Italian pizzas. You can choose one or a combination of these cheeses based on your preference.
  • The quality of cheese: Always choose high-quality cheese that is made using traditional methods. Look for cheese that has a DOP (Denominazione di Origine Protetta) or DOC (Denominazione di Origine Controllata) certification, which ensures that the cheese is made using specific methods and ingredients.
  • The texture of cheese: The cheese should have a soft and elastic texture that melts easily. It should not be too dry or too oily.
  • The flavor of cheese: The cheese should have a mild and creamy flavor that complements the other ingredients of the pizza. It should not overpower the flavor of the sauce or toppings.

Why is Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese Important?

Authentic Italian pizza cheese is important because it adds the unique flavor and texture that makes Italian pizza so popular. The cheese is an integral part of the pizza, and it can make or break the taste of the pizza. Using authentic Italian pizza cheese ensures that your pizza has an authentic Italian taste that cannot be replicated by other types of cheese.

How to Store Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese?

To store authentic Italian pizza cheese, wrap it in parchment paper or wax paper and then place it in a plastic bag or airtight container. Store the cheese in the refrigerator at a temperature between 34°F and 40°F. Do not freeze the cheese as it can affect the texture and flavor of the cheese.

How to Use Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese?

To use authentic Italian pizza cheese, shred or slice the cheese and then sprinkle it over the pizza dough. Make sure to distribute the cheese evenly, leaving some space around the edges. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at 450°F for 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

FAQs About Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese

Q1. Can I use other types of cheese instead of authentic Italian pizza cheese?
A1. While you can use other types of cheese, they may not give you the same flavor and texture as authentic Italian pizza cheese. Q2. Can I use pre-shredded cheese?
A2. While pre-shredded cheese is convenient, it may contain additives that affect the flavor and texture of the cheese. It is best to shred the cheese yourself. Q3. Can I use low-fat cheese?
A3. While you can use low-fat cheese, it may not melt properly and may not give you the same flavor and texture as authentic Italian pizza cheese. Q4. Can I use vegan cheese?
A4. While you can use vegan cheese, it may not give you the same flavor and texture as authentic Italian pizza cheese. You can try using vegan mozzarella or vegan Parmesan cheese.

Conclusion of Authentic Italian Pizza Cheese

Choosing the right cheese is crucial for making an authentic Italian pizza. By following the tips mentioned above, you can choose the best authentic Italian pizza cheese that will take your pizza game to the next level. Remember to always choose high-quality cheese that is made using traditional methods and has a DOP or DOC certification. Happy pizza-making!
