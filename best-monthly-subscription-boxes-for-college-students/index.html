Best Monthly Subscription Boxes For College Students

Written by William Jul 17, 2023 · 3 min read

As a college student, it can be challenging to balance classes, homework, and social life. One thing that can add to the stress is finding time to shop for necessities or things that make life more enjoyable, like snacks or self-care items. That’s where monthly subscription boxes come in. They can make your life easier by delivering items directly to your door, and they can also be a fun surprise to look forward to each month.

If you’re a college student, you may be interested in finding the best monthly subscription boxes for your needs. You may be looking for something that provides snacks to fuel you through long study sessions or self-care items to help you relax and destress.

So, what are the best monthly subscription boxes for college students? In this article, we’ll go over some options that cater to different needs and interests.

Snack Subscription Boxes

If you’re someone who needs a quick bite to eat between classes or while studying, a snack subscription box might be perfect for you. One popular option is the Graze box, which delivers a variety of healthy snacks each month. Another great option is Sips by, which delivers a selection of teas tailored to your preferences.

Self-Care Subscription Boxes

College can be stressful, so taking care of your mental health is important. A self-care subscription box can help you do just that. Bombay & Cedar is a box that delivers aromatherapy, skincare, and wellness products each month. Another option is TheraBox, which delivers items like journals, aromatherapy, and skincare products.

Beauty Subscription Boxes

If you’re someone who loves trying new beauty products, a beauty subscription box might be for you. One popular option is Ipsy, which delivers a bag of makeup and skincare products each month. Another option is Birchbox, which delivers a mix of beauty and lifestyle products.

How to Choose the Best Subscription Box for You

When choosing a subscription box, it’s important to consider your needs and interests. Ask yourself what you want to get out of the box. Are you looking for snacks to fuel your studies or self-care items to help you relax? Do you want to try new beauty products or receive a mix of items each month? Consider your budget as well, as some boxes can be pricey.

Conclusion of Best Monthly Subscription Boxes for College Students

Overall, subscription boxes can be a great way to make college life a little easier and more enjoyable. Whether you’re in need of snacks, self-care items, or beauty products, there’s a subscription box out there for you. Consider your needs and interests, and do some research to find the best one for you.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do subscription boxes typically cost?

A: The cost can vary depending on the box, but most range from $10-$50 per month.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

A: Yes, most subscription boxes allow you to cancel or pause your subscription at any time.

Q: Can I customize my subscription box?

A: Some boxes allow you to customize your items based on your preferences, while others deliver a set selection of items each month.

Q: Are subscription boxes eco-friendly?

A: Some subscription boxes prioritize eco-friendly and sustainable products, while others may not. It’s important to do your research and choose a box that aligns with your values.
