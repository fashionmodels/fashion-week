How to Use the Ralph Lauren Men's Dress Shirt Size Chart

To use the Ralph Lauren men's dress shirt size chart, you'll need to take accurate measurements of your neck, sleeve length, and chest size. Once you have these measurements, you can use the chart to find the perfect fit for your body type.

Neck Size

The first measurement you'll need to take is your neck size. To do this, wrap a tape measure around the base of your neck where the collar of a shirt would sit. Make sure the tape measure is snug but not too tight. Once you have your neck size, you can use the chart to find the corresponding shirt size.

Sleeve Length

The second measurement you'll need to take is your sleeve length. To do this, extend your arm out to the side and bend your elbow at a 90-degree angle. Measure from the center of the back of your neck to your wrist. This measurement will give you your sleeve length, which you can use to find the corresponding shirt size on the chart.

Choosing the Right Fit

The Ralph Lauren men's dress shirt size chart offers three different fits: Classic Fit, Custom Fit, and Slim Fit. Classic Fit is the roomiest fit, Custom Fit is a slightly slimmer fit, and Slim Fit is the most tailored fit. It's important to choose the right fit for your body type to ensure that the shirt looks and feels great.
