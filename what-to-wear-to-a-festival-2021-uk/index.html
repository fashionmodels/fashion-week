What To Wear To A Festival 2021 Uk A Guide To Festival Fashion

Written by Oliver Aug 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you excited for the upcoming festival season in the UK but stressing out about what to wear? You're not alone. Festival fashion can be tricky to navigate, especially when you want to look stylish and comfortable while also being practical. With so many options out there, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

When it comes to festival fashion, the key is to strike a balance between style and practicality. You want to wear something that looks good but is also comfortable enough to wear all day and night. You also need to consider the weather, as festivals in the UK can be unpredictable. Rain, mud, and chilly nights are all possibilities, so you need to be prepared for anything.

So, what should you wear to a festival in 2021? Here are some tips to help you put together the perfect festival outfit:

1. Comfortable Shoes

One of the most important things to consider when choosing your festival outfit is footwear. You'll be on your feet all day, so you need to wear something comfortable and supportive. Avoid heels or anything too flimsy, as you'll be walking on uneven ground and may even have to navigate through mud. Opt for boots, trainers, or sandals with a sturdy sole.

2. Layers

As mentioned before, UK festivals can be unpredictable when it comes to weather. It's essential to layer your outfit to ensure you're prepared for any temperature changes. Start with a t-shirt or vest top and add a hoodie, denim jacket, or bomber jacket on top. You can always tie the jacket around your waist if you get too warm.

3. Practical Bags

You'll need to bring some essentials with you to the festival, such as your phone, money, and perhaps a water bottle. However, you don't want to carry a heavy bag around with you all day. Opt for a small backpack, crossbody bag, or bumbag to keep your belongings safe and your hands free.

4. Statement Accessories

A festival is the perfect opportunity to have fun with your accessories. Think statement sunglasses, hats, and jewellery. However, don't go overboard – you don't want to look like you're trying too hard. Pick one or two statement pieces and keep the rest of your outfit simple.

5. Comfortable Clothes

Last but not least, it's essential to wear comfortable clothes to a festival. You'll be dancing, walking, and sitting on the ground, so you need to wear something that allows you to move freely. Opt for loose-fitting trousers, shorts, or a floaty dress. Just make sure you're comfortable and confident in what you're wearing.

Personal Experience

When I went to a festival for the first time, I made the mistake of wearing sandals with no support. My feet were aching after just a couple of hours, and I had blisters by the end of the day. I learned my lesson and now always wear comfortable shoes to festivals. I also make sure to layer my outfit so I can adjust to any temperature changes.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a dress to a festival?

A: Yes, you can wear a dress to a festival. Just make sure it's comfortable and allows you to move freely. If it's a hot day, a floaty maxi dress can be a great option.

Q: What should I do if it rains at the festival?

A: If it rains, you'll be glad you packed a waterproof jacket and wellies. You can still have fun in the rain, but it's essential to stay dry and warm.

Q: Can I wear heels to a festival?

A: It's not recommended to wear heels to a festival. You'll be walking on uneven ground, and it's easy to trip and fall. Opt for comfortable shoes with a sturdy sole instead.

Q: Should I bring a change of clothes to the festival?

A: It's a good idea to bring a spare outfit, just in case. You never know what might happen at a festival, so it's always better to be prepared.

Conclusion

When it comes to festival fashion, the key is to strike a balance between style and practicality. You want to look good, but you also need to be comfortable and prepared for any weather conditions. By following these tips, you'll be able to put together the perfect festival outfit that allows you to dance, sing, and have fun all day and night.
