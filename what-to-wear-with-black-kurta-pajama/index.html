What To Wear With Black Kurta Pajama

Written by Daniel Oct 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Black kurta pajama is a classic Indian attire that is perfect for any occasion. Whether it is a wedding, a festival or a formal event, a black kurta pajama is always a safe bet. However, when it comes to pairing it with the right accessories, many people are often confused. This blog post will guide you on what to wear with black kurta pajama to create a perfect look.

When it comes to Indian traditional attire, accessories play a vital role in completing the look. The right accessories can enhance the overall appearance of the outfit, while the wrong ones can ruin it. Choosing the right accessories to go with a black kurta pajama can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be effortless.

The first thing to consider when choosing accessories for a black kurta pajama is the occasion. For a formal event, it is best to keep it simple and elegant. A classic watch, a pair of leather shoes, and a simple pocket square can do the trick. For a wedding or a festival, you can experiment with bolder accessories such as a statement necklace or a pair of jhumkas.

In summary, when it comes to accessorizing a black kurta pajama, it is important to consider the occasion and keep it simple for a formal event and experiment with bolder accessories for a wedding or a festival.

Accessories for a Formal Event

For a formal event, it is best to keep it simple and elegant. A classic watch, a pair of leather shoes, and a simple pocket square can do the trick. The classic watch adds a touch of sophistication to the outfit, while the leather shoes complete the look. The pocket square is a subtle accessory that can elevate the outfit to the next level. A black kurta pajama with a white pocket square is a classic combination that never goes out of style.

Accessories for a Wedding or a Festival

For a wedding or a festival, you can experiment with bolder accessories such as a statement necklace or a pair of jhumkas. A statement necklace can add a pop of color to the outfit and make it stand out. Jhumkas are a classic Indian earring that can add a touch of elegance to the outfit. You can also experiment with a pair of mojris or kolhapuris to complete the look.

Choosing the Right Footwear

Choosing the right footwear to go with a black kurta pajama is just as important as choosing the right accessories. For a formal event, a pair of leather shoes is the best option. It adds a touch of class and sophistication to the outfit. For a wedding or a festival, you can experiment with mojris or kolhapuris. Both are traditional Indian footwear that can add a touch of elegance to the outfit.

Experimenting with Colors

Black is a versatile color that can be paired with a variety of colors. For a formal event, it is best to stick to classic colors such as white, beige, or gray. For a wedding or a festival, you can experiment with brighter colors such as red, green, or gold. However, it is important to remember not to go overboard with the colors. The accessories should complement the outfit and not overpower it.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a blazer with a black kurta pajama?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a blazer with a black kurta pajama. It is a great way to add a touch of sophistication to the outfit.

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a black kurta pajama?

A: It is not recommended to wear sneakers with a black kurta pajama. It is best to stick to traditional footwear such as leather shoes, mojris or kolhapuris.

Q: Can I wear a tie with a black kurta pajama?

A: No, it is not recommended to wear a tie with a black kurta pajama. It is a traditional Indian outfit and does not require a tie.

Q: Can I wear a wristwatch with a black kurta pajama?

A: Yes, a wristwatch is a great accessory to pair with a black kurta pajama. It adds a touch of sophistication to the outfit.

Conclusion of what to wear with black kurta pajama

Accessorizing a black kurta pajama can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be effortless. When choosing accessories for a black kurta pajama, it is important to consider the occasion and keep it simple for a formal event and experiment with bolder accessories for a wedding or a festival. The right accessories can enhance the overall appearance of the outfit and create a perfect look.
