What To Wear To A Fall Outdoor Wedding 2021 A Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you've got an upcoming fall outdoor wedding to attend, you might be wondering what to wear. With the changing weather and unpredictable temperature, dressing up for a fall wedding can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered with this guide on what to wear to a fall outdoor wedding in 2021.

Attending a wedding can be stressful enough, but figuring out what to wear to a fall outdoor wedding can add an extra layer of stress. The weather can be unpredictable, and you don't want to be too hot or too cold. Plus, you want to make sure you're dressed appropriately for the occasion. But with a few tips and tricks, you can put together the perfect outfit for a fall outdoor wedding.

What to Wear to a Fall Outdoor Wedding 2021: The Answer

When it comes to what to wear to a fall outdoor wedding, the key is to dress in layers. You never know what the temperature might be like, so it's always a good idea to have layers that you can add or remove as needed. Start with a dress or a skirt and top combo, and add a light jacket or sweater. You can also accessorize with a scarf or shawl.

For footwear, opt for comfortable shoes that are appropriate for the outdoors. Flats or low heels are a safe bet, and make sure to avoid stilettos or anything that might sink into the grass. If the wedding is in the evening, you can also opt for closed-toe shoes or boots.

When it comes to colors and patterns, fall is the perfect time to embrace warm and rich hues. Jewel tones such as emerald green, burgundy, and navy blue are great choices. You can also opt for floral patterns with a darker background or plaid prints. Just remember to avoid anything too casual or too flashy.

Personal Experience: What to Wear to a Fall Outdoor Wedding 2021

As someone who has attended several fall outdoor weddings, I've found that the key is to be comfortable and stylish. One of my go-to outfits is a midi dress with a light jacket or cardigan. I also like to accessorize with a scarf or statement earrings. For footwear, I usually go for ankle boots or ballet flats.

Another important tip is to check the dress code. While fall outdoor weddings are usually more casual than indoor weddings, you still want to make sure you're dressed appropriately. If the dress code is formal, opt for a floor-length dress or a suit. If it's semi-formal, a cocktail dress or dressy separates are appropriate.

Accessories for a Fall Outdoor Wedding

When it comes to accessories for a fall outdoor wedding, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure to bring a small purse or clutch to carry your essentials. You don't want to be lugging around a big bag all night. Second, consider wearing a hat or fascinator for a touch of glamour. Third, don't forget about your jewelry. A statement necklace or earrings can add some sparkle to your outfit.

Makeup and Hair for a Fall Outdoor Wedding

When it comes to makeup and hair for a fall outdoor wedding, it's best to keep it simple and natural. Opt for a natural-looking foundation, a pop of color on the lips, and a subtle smokey eye. For hair, a low bun or loose waves are perfect for an outdoor wedding.

What Not to Wear to a Fall Outdoor Wedding

While fall outdoor weddings are more casual than indoor weddings, there are still a few things to avoid. Avoid anything too skimpy or revealing, as well as anything too casual such as denim or sneakers. Also, avoid anything too flashy or attention-grabbing, as you don't want to steal the spotlight from the bride and groom.

Personal Experience: What to Wear to a Fall Outdoor Wedding 2021

As someone who has attended several fall outdoor weddings, I've found that the key is to be comfortable and stylish. One of my go-to outfits is a midi dress with a light jacket or cardigan. I also like to accessorize with a scarf or statement earrings. For footwear, I usually go for ankle boots or ballet flats.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear white to a fall outdoor wedding?

A: While it's okay to wear white to a wedding, it's best to avoid it unless the bride has specifically requested it. Opt for a different color such as jewel tones or rich hues.

Q: Can I wear black to a fall outdoor wedding?

A: Yes, black is acceptable for a fall outdoor wedding. Just make sure to dress it up with some accessories and avoid anything too somber.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a fall outdoor wedding?

A: No, jeans are not appropriate for a fall outdoor wedding. Opt for dressy separates or a cocktail dress instead.

Q: Can I wear sandals to a fall outdoor wedding?

A: Sandals are generally not appropriate for a fall outdoor wedding. Opt for closed-toe shoes or flats instead.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Fall Outdoor Wedding 2021

Attending a fall outdoor wedding can be a fun and exciting experience, but figuring out what to wear can be a challenge. With these tips and tricks, you can put together the perfect outfit that is both stylish and appropriate for the occasion. Remember to dress in layers, opt for comfortable shoes, and accessorize with warm and rich colors. And most importantly, have fun and enjoy the celebration!
