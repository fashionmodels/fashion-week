Types of Mens Watches

There are several types of mens watches available, each with their own unique style and design. Some of the most popular types include dress watches, sports watches, and dive watches.

Dress watches are typically more formal and feature a simple, elegant design. They're perfect for special occasions and can be worn with a suit or formal attire.

Sports watches are designed with durability in mind and are perfect for outdoor activities and sports. They often feature a stopwatch and other useful functions.

Dive watches are designed for underwater use and are typically water-resistant up to a certain depth. They often feature a rotating bezel and a durable strap or bracelet.
