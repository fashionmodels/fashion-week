Where To Buy Vintage Halloween Costumes A Guide

Written by Petter Oct 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As Halloween approaches, many people start to think about what they will wear as a costume. Some people like to buy brand new costumes every year, but others prefer to wear something unique and vintage. However, finding vintage Halloween costumes can be a challenge, and it's not always easy to know where to start looking. In this guide, we will explore some of the best places to buy vintage Halloween costumes, so you can stand out from the crowd and have a costume that nobody else has.

If you're looking for a vintage Halloween costume, you might be feeling overwhelmed and not sure where to start. Perhaps you've tried searching online, but you're not finding what you're looking for. Or maybe you've looked in local thrift stores, but you're not having any luck. This can be frustrating, especially if you have a specific costume in mind that you really want to wear. However, don't worry - there are plenty of places where you can find vintage Halloween costumes.

The first place to start looking for vintage Halloween costumes is online. There are a number of websites that specialize in selling vintage clothing and costumes, such as Etsy, eBay, and Ruby Lane. These websites often have a wide variety of costumes to choose from, and you can search for specific styles or time periods. However, it's important to be cautious when buying online, as you won't be able to try the costume on before you buy it. Make sure you read the seller's reviews and return policy before making a purchase.

Another option is to look for vintage Halloween costumes in local thrift stores or vintage shops. These stores often have a dedicated Halloween section during the month of October, and you might be able to find some unique and interesting costumes. You can also try searching for vintage clothing stores in your area, as they may have a selection of costumes available as well.

In summary, there are many different places where you can buy vintage Halloween costumes, both online and in-person. Some of the best places to look include online marketplaces like Etsy and eBay, as well as local thrift stores and vintage shops. By taking the time to search for a vintage Halloween costume, you can have a one-of-a-kind costume that will make you stand out at any Halloween party.

Buying Vintage Halloween Costumes Online

When it comes to buying vintage Halloween costumes online, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure you know your measurements so you can be sure the costume will fit correctly. Vintage clothing often runs smaller than modern clothing, so don't be surprised if you need to order a larger size than you would normally wear. Second, read the seller's reviews and check their return policy before making a purchase. You want to make sure you're buying from a reputable seller who will provide accurate descriptions and photos of the costume.

One of the best places to buy vintage Halloween costumes online is Etsy. This website is home to thousands of vintage sellers, many of whom specialize in vintage clothing and costumes. You can search for specific styles or time periods, and you can also filter your search by size and price. Many Etsy sellers also offer custom-made costumes, so you can get a costume that is tailored to your specific measurements and style preferences.

Buying Vintage Halloween Costumes In-Person

If you prefer to see and touch vintage Halloween costumes before you buy them, you might want to try looking for them in local thrift stores or vintage shops. These stores often have a Halloween section during the month of October, and you might be able to find some unique and interesting costumes. You can also try searching for vintage clothing stores in your area, as they may have a selection of costumes available as well.

When shopping for vintage Halloween costumes in-person, it's important to take your time and try on different costumes to find the perfect one. Vintage clothing often fits differently than modern clothing, so don't be discouraged if the first costume you try on doesn't fit quite right. You might also want to bring a friend along to help you with your search and offer a second opinion.

Choosing the Right Vintage Halloween Costume

When choosing a vintage Halloween costume, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the time period you want to dress up as. Do you want to go for a 1920s flapper look, a 1950s housewife, or a 1970s disco queen? Once you've decided on a time period, you can start looking for costumes that fit that style.

You'll also want to consider the fit of the costume. Vintage clothing often runs smaller than modern clothing, so make sure you know your measurements and order a size that will fit you comfortably. If you're buying a costume in-person, make sure you try it on before you buy it to ensure a good fit.

Taking Care of Your Vintage Halloween Costume

Once you've found the perfect vintage Halloween costume, it's important to take care of it so it will last for years to come. Vintage clothing should be hand-washed or dry cleaned to ensure it stays in good condition. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, as this can damage the fabric. Store your costume in a cool, dry place to prevent any damage from moisture or insects.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Vintage Halloween Costumes

When it comes to finding a vintage Halloween costume, there are plenty of options available both online and in-person. By taking the time to search for a unique and interesting costume, you can stand out from the crowd and have a costume that nobody else has. Whether you choose to buy online or in-person, make sure you take your time and choose a costume that fits your style and personality. Happy Halloween!
