Factors That Affect Turnaround Time

The time it takes to get your suit altered at Men's Wearhouse can vary depending on several factors. Below are some of the factors that can affect the turnaround time:

Type of Alteration

The type of alteration needed is one of the most significant factors that can affect the time it takes to get your suit back. Minor alterations such as hemming pants or sleeves may take less time compared to more complex alterations such as adjusting the shoulders or waist.

Number of Alterations Required

The number of alterations required can also affect the turnaround time. If you need several alterations done, it may take longer to complete them all compared to just one alteration.

Tips for a Faster Turnaround Time

If you're in a hurry and need your suit altered as soon as possible, there are a few things you can do to help speed up the process:

Schedule an Appointment

Scheduling an appointment can help ensure that a tailor is available to work on your suit right away. It also helps to avoid long wait times.

Be Clear About Your Needs

When you speak to the tailor, be clear about the type of alteration you need and when you need your suit back. This will help the tailor prioritize your alteration and work on it quickly.
