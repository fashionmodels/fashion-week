How to Shop at Vintage Clothing Stores like Urban Outfitters

If you're new to vintage shopping, it can be overwhelming to navigate the racks of a vintage clothing store like Urban Outfitters. Here are a few tips to help you get started:

  1. Have an open mind: Vintage clothing stores have a wide variety of styles and eras to choose from, so be open to trying on pieces that may not be your usual style.
  2. Check for damage: Vintage clothing is often well-loved, so be sure to check for any holes, stains, or other damage before purchasing.
  3. Try things on: Vintage sizing can be very different from modern sizing, so be sure to try things on before purchasing to ensure a good fit.
  4. Ask for help: If you're not sure where to start, don't be afraid to ask for help from the store staff. They can often recommend pieces that will fit your style and body type.

How to Care for Vintage Clothing from Stores like Urban Outfitters

Once you've purchased your vintage clothing from stores like Urban Outfitters, it's important to care for it properly to ensure that it lasts for years to come. Here are a few tips:

  1. Hand wash or machine wash on gentle: Vintage clothing is often more delicate than modern clothing, so be sure to wash it on a gentle cycle or by hand.
  2. Avoid the dryer: Vintage clothing is often made of natural fibers, which can shrink in the dryer. Hang dry your vintage clothing to ensure that it keeps its shape.
  3. Store properly: Store your vintage clothing in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid storing it in plastic, which can trap moisture and cause mildew.
